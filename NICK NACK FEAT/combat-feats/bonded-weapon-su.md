# Bonded Weapon (Su)

<mark style="color:green;">Requirements</mark> -&#x20;

Gain the ability to perform a 10 minute ritual that allows you to bond with 2 specific weapons.

You gain DR 10 against disarm combat maneuvers made against these weapons.

When you throw these weapons, they automatically return to your hand after the attack is resolved \[teleportation] if desired.

If these weapons somehow manage to leave your possession otherwise, you can spend a swift action to have them return to your hand \[teleportation].
