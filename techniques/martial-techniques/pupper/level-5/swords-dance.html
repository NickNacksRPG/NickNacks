Swords Dance

Level 5 Pupper
Tags: Su, Buff
Action: Swift

Range -
Target/AoE Self
Duration 3 rounds
Save -

Create 3 copies of a held melee weapon, natural attack or Unarmed Attack that float around your head. While these persist, gain +2 Attack. As part of casting using this technique, and as a swift action each subsequent turn, you can make a simple melee attack with that weapon and consume a copy. This attack functions exactly like making a simple attack with the weapon normally, but deals an additional d8 (4) dmg.
