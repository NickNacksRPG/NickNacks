Bear Arms

Level 1 Beastmode
Tags: Prepared
Action: -

Range -
Target/AoE -
Duration -
Save -

While this technique is prepared, your arms get coated in a thick fur and your fingers get claws at their ends, granting you a Natural Attack. The claw attacks deal d8 slash damage, or d10 if your hands are empty. They have the CM (disarm) and CM (grapple) weapon properties. If you already had claws, these qualities are only in addition to what you already had. AKA, cannot decrease your claw dmg.

While wielding a gun, you get DR 20 vs disarm attacks.

You can make grapple and disarm attacks as AoOs with your bear arms, as though you you possessed the +Maneuver Initiate feat.

You gain a multi-attack option:
Bear Rights - Make 2 attacks with your bear claws.
