# Dragon Weapon Special Attacks

<mark style="color:red;">**Sever**</mark> - This attack gains +2 attack. On-hit sever the connection a creature has to their deity. Any abilities related to prayer they cannot use until they rest. Similar abilities that rely on divine power like [Smite](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/classes/classes-1/zealot) are unaffected.
