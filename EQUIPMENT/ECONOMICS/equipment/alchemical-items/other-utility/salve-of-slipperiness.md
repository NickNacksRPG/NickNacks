# Salve of Slipperiness

## <mark style="color:green;">Common, DC 15 (500g)</mark>

Oils and secretions of all kinds can be used to make these salves. They are the most effective lubricant on the market, preventing friction in all kinds of scenarios.&#x20;

Applying it to a creature takes 3 rounds and a _thorough_ rub down. It temporarily gives them DR 10 vs grapples and the compression quality for 10 minutes.
