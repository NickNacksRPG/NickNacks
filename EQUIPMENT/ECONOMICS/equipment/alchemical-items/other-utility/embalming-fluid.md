# Embalming Fluid

## <mark style="color:green;">Cheap, DC 15 (150g)</mark>

A slightly less extreme version of flash freeze, adapted for a specific purpose. Often used by morticians to make funeral rites more pleasant.

Application of this ointment on a corpse prevents its decomposition and negates its stench for 1 week.&#x20;

With 2 doses the embalming lasts a month.\
With 3 doses, and a creature trained in the medicine skill, it can last many years.
