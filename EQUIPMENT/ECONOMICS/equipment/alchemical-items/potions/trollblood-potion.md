# Trollblood Potion

## <mark style="color:green;">Common, DC 20 (800g)</mark>

By scraping off the natural excretions of a troll's skin, trollblood potions can be made.

When consumed, the creature gains regeneration 1 for 1 minute. This effect is suppressed for 1 turn if the creature takes fire/poison damage.

Trollblood gives you potion sickness.
