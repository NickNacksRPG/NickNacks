# Hunter's Rifle

## <mark style="color:green;">6,000g (DC 14)</mark>

This gun has a barrel that grows to comically large size at its tip. You get a +4 bonus on survival checks to track any creature you have hit or grazed with this weapon in the last week.

<mark style="color:green;">Special Attack: My Brand</mark> - On hit apply a brand to a creature, physically marking them. You and your companion creatures attacks do an additional d6 damage until the end of your next turn.

The gun functions 2h rifle (see exotic weapons).
