# Orcish Hammer

## <mark style="color:green;">5,000g (DC 14)</mark>

This hammer is infused with the power of the wind itself by orcish shamans. It feels practically weightless while you swing it through air, despite its otherwise unwieldy size. Its wielder can innately cast [storm stomp](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/clairvoyance/level-1/storm-stomp) 1/day. The wielder also gains this special attack...

<mark style="color:green;">Windfury</mark> -\
Attack twice in rapid succession, applying DR only once.

The hammer is a 1h crush weapon with the strings attached and one-and done quality.
