# Soul Eater

## <mark style="color:green;">22,000g (DC 20)</mark>

This black great sword has a red mana crystal in the shape of an eye affixed to its hilt. Any who hold the blade in their hands can feel its blood lust. While wielding this blade, the wielder has [weakness 3](https://app.gitbook.com/s/LcHjOjPnBbxeu9BQoJ8P/monster-qualities/weaknesses#weakness) to all damage, but reduces the mana cost of all techniques by 1. This effect cannot prevent mana burn. The wielder gets a +2 bonus to attack with this weapon.

The Soul Eater can be enchanted 3 times.

It is a 2h slash weapon with the bleed and easy repair properties.
