# Wand, Enhanced

## <mark style="color:green;">5,000g (DC 15)</mark>

This piece of enchanted wood is infused with mana from those adept at magic casting, allowing you to channel their experience through it.

While wielding a wand, you can increase the CL of a spell you cast by 1 as a swift action.

An enhanced wand, in additional to casting a cantrip, allows you to innately cast a tier 1 technique 1/day.
