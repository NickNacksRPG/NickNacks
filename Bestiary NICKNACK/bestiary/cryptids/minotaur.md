## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**A-MAZE-ing**</mark> - Minotaurs are exceptionally good at not getting lost. Once upon a time their people built elaborate mazes between their settlements and the outside world. Most lie in ruin. A minotaur always knows how to get to any point in their maze quickly, and can even physically change the maze with earth bending to create shortcuts. Their tremorsense increases to include any area that can be considered a "maze."

<mark style="color:blue;">**Can't Look Away**</mark> - Minotaurs have disadvantage on saves against effects that taunt originating from creatures who are significantly red.
