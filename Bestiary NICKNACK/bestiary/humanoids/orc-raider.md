# Orc Raider

**Level 1 Medium Orc**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 14\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 32\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 27  (DR 5 vs poise)\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +6, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +4, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +2 (+4 fear)

<mark style="color:green;">**Ferocity**</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**Naked Courage**</mark> - Gain a +4 bonus to saves against \[fear] effects while not wearing armor.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 13)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**2h Axe (S)**</mark>  +4 (+2 ALL combat maneuvers, +2 topple, +4 MI)\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Crossbow (P)**</mark>  +4 (60/120)\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Misc Heavy Objects (C)**</mark> +4 (20/40)\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Make an axe attack and a combat maneuver

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - 1/day - [Boasting Taunt](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/warfare/level-1/boasting-taunt), [Sonic Boom](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/sonic-boom), [Thunder Stomp](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/thunder-stomp)

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: Guillotine**</mark> - Leap into the air with an overhead swipe, dealing an additional d6 damage. If this attack defeats a foe, you can use this special attack again.

<mark style="color:red;">**Toss Up**</mark> - Orc raiders are blessed by [The Cataclysm](https://app.gitbook.com/s/qFzTamwcoNEgFoEbLz7c/gods/demon-lords/caldera-the-cataclysm) with ludicrous strength. They can work together to lift objects that are 10s of thousands of pounds. If they throw generally heavy objects like boulders, and such objects are in supply, they can toss them as an attack.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Greener Pastures**</mark> - Orc raiders are often interested in wealth, supplies and glory in battle. By rolling a DC 14 persuasion or deception check in a neutral situation, you can convince them that an even bigger haul is on the way.
