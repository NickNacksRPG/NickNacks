# Zombie

**Level 0 Medium Undead**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 12\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 25\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 20\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +5, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> -1, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +0

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Necrotic

<mark style="color:green;">**Undead Fortitude**</mark> - The first time this creature is defeated, it is instead reduced to 1 hp unless it is defeated by holy damage or a critical hit.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 12)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Unarmed (C)**</mark>  +3 (+infection)\
d8+3+d8 necrotic (12)

<mark style="color:red;">**Infection**</mark> - Creatures that take damage from the zombie are inflicted with a T1 affliction (fort negates).

<mark style="color:orange;">Boils</mark> - A bunch of puss filled boils slowly form on your skin. At the end of each day, you become drained 1. If this kills the creature by reducing its max hp to 0 or the creature otherwise dies while this infection affects them, they rise as a [zombie](zombie.md) a few minutes later.

<mark style="color:orange;">Cure</mark> - Application of holy water to the affected area immediately dissolves the boils, removing the affliction, but not any drain it inflicted.
