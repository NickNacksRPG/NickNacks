## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Fear the Styx**</mark> - Procrastinating spirits fear the finality of their deaths, and seek to delay their journey into the Underworld whenever possible. Exposure to water from the river Styx immediately destroys a procrastinating spirit and sends it to the Underworld.
