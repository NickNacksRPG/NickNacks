# El Stitchisimo

**Level 4 Large Undead \[unique]**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 17\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 61\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 26\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +9, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +4, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +6

<mark style="color:green;">**Immune**</mark> - Poison

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Necrotic

<mark style="color:green;">**Order Up \[healing] \[recharge]**</mark> - Stitches can launch food at his allies to heal them. He comes equipped with 1 meal for the living, and one snack for the dead. He can launch this food up to 30 feet as a swift action, and it heals 10 hp.

<mark style="color:green;">**Munchies \[healing]**</mark> - Any time Stitches lands a bite attack or devours an enemy, he heals 5 hp.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 16)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Ham Bat (C+P)**</mark>  +10\
d10+3 (8)

<mark style="color:red;">**Bite (P)**</mark>  +8\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - 1 ham bat attack and 1 bite attack

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Blood for Blood](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/barbarism/level-1/blood-for-blood)\
1/day - [Ups and Downs](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/barbarism/level-2/ups-and-downs), [Jaws of the Beast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-2/jaws-of-the-beast)

<mark style="color:red;">**Hook, Line and Sinker \[recharge]**</mark> - Stitches can throw his hook at a creature within 30 ft. If he also spends his swift action to reach, its range increases to 60 ft. The creature is pulled adjacent to him and takes 4d6 (14) slashing damage (reflex halves dmg and negates pull).

<mark style="color:red;">**Devour \[recharge]**</mark> - Stitches can consume an ally within reach to put them in stasis for 1 round, using the ability like this does not have the recharge tag. Stitches can also consume an enemy automatically for 1 round.&#x20;

During this round, stitches can move around freely. At the end of Stitches' next turn, he can choose to deposit them in any adjacent space.

<mark style="color:red;">**Putrid Bile \[recharge] \[poison] \[persistent]**</mark> - When Stitches dashes, he can choose to unleash a toxic concoction. For the remainder of his turn, any space he moves into and all adjacent spaces are coated in a toxic and corrosive goo. This area is treated as difficult terrain. Creatures within this area take 4d8 (18) poison damage (fort halves).

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Hands Full**</mark> - Stitches has multiple abilities that are tied to objects he wields. He cannot use his hook or his pan if he is disarmed, disabling _hook, line and sinker_ and _order up_ respectively.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Hungry Hungry Stitches**</mark> - By breaking Stitches' poise, a creature can shove an object into his mouth-like abdomen in addition to any other effects.
