# Copy of Cherub

**Level 0 Small Celestial**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 14\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 20\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 20\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +0, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +3, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +3

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Holy

<mark style="color:green;">**Wanderer (Su)**</mark> - The cherub has a magical aura that prevents it from being seen by lesser creatures. You must have a perception bonus of at least +4 to see a cherub. This ability deactivates for 1 minute when a cherub deals damage.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 12)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Shortbow (P)**</mark>  +3 (40/80) +charming shot\
d6+3 (6)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Splatter](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/goomshroom/level-1/splatter)

<mark style="color:red;">**Charming Arrow \[charm]**</mark> - On hit inflict charmed for 1 minute. The cherub can choose to not deal damage with the arrow to gain +4 attack.

If the creature is DD (including if the cherub isn't visible thanks to wanderer) the cherub can choose another creature within 30 ft of its target that it isn't currently in combat with to be the subject of the charm. When the cherub charms in this way, it lasts 24 hours.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Sucker for Love**</mark> - Cherubs can easily be persuaded to assist creatures in facilitating romantic relationships, usually requiring no check.
