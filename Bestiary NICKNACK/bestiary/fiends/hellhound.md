## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Give a Dog a Bone**</mark> - Hellhounds always prefer attacking any kind of boney creatures, such as skeletons. When not already supplied with such, a creature can toss a sizable bone within 30 feet of one, functioning like the [fetch](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/mother-earth/level-2/fetch) technique.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Burning Heart**</mark> - A hellhound has a red-hot stone at its core. If carefully handled and placed inside of a vessel, such as a piece of jewelry, it enhances fire magic. Such an item increases the CL of fire spells by 1.

It must be removed completely from the body and submerged in water for the stone to cool. Extracting the stone requires a DC 17 survival check, and you have disadvantage on the check without specialized tools, such as those a blacksmith uses, or fire resistance. Failing shatters the stone, rendering it worthless.
