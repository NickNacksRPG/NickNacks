# Meathook

**Level 3 Medium Fiend \[minion]**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 17\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 50\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 25\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +4, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +8, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +3

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Fire

<mark style="color:green;">**Fired Up**</mark> - A meathook takes no penalty to AC when ablaze.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 15)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Chains (C+Fire)**</mark>  +6 (10 ft reach, +4 drag)\
d8+3+d8 fire (12)

<mark style="color:red;">**Gore (P)**</mark>  +6 (+d6 bleed and embed)\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Make a drag attack and another natural attack.

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Headbutt](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/warfare/level-2/headbutt), [Boiling Blood](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/pyromancy/level-2/boiling-blood)

<mark style="color:red;">**Constrict**</mark> - d8 (4) fire

<mark style="color:red;">**Embed**</mark> - Immediately after dragging a creature adjacent, if the meathook uses a gore attack, the creature and the meathook are entangled on hit. While entangled in this way, both the meathook and the attached creature are blinded to everybody but each other as they are stuck face to face.

If the creature separates themselves, they immediately take bleed damage again. The bleed cannot be healed while the creature remains embedded. Embedded creatures are automatically crit by concealed carry.

<mark style="color:red;">**Infernal Chains**</mark> - A meathook can increase the range of her chains to 30 ft during her turn. When she does so, they become ranged attacks. On hit she can drag herself to a creature \[elusive] or make a drag attack against the creature to pull them adjacent. She can also attach to terrain.

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: Concealed Carry**</mark> - The meathook has the ability to fire grapeshot projectiles from its mouth. Functions as a blunderbuss attack (2d6+3, 15 ft cone). Because of the subtle nature of the attack, creatures are DD unless they succeed on a DC 16 perception check.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Washed Up**</mark> - When wet or frosty, a meathooks chain attacks deal no fire damage. At the end of a meathooks turn, they lose the wet or frosty condition.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str +1, Dex +3, Lucc +2, Int +0, Wis +0, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Speed**</mark> - 40

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Feats**</mark> - [Blind Fight](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/blind-fight), [Point Blank Master](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/point-blank-master)

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Skills**</mark> - +5 perception, +10 acrobatics, +5 planes, +8 Sleight of Hand
