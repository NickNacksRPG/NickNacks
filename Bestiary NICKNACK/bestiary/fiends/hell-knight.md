# Hell Knight

**Level 5 Large Fiend \[monster]**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 17\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 80\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 35 (DR 3)\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +10, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +6, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +5

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Poison, Sonic

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 17)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Claw (S or C)**</mark>  +8 (+8 shove, +4 MI)\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - 2 claws, attacking the same creature (no MA penalty)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Sonic Boom](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/sonic-boom), [Thunder Stomp](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/thunder-stomp), [Training Arc](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/training-arc-su)\
1/day - [Fracture](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-3/fracture)\
Constant - [Shockwave](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-3/shockwave)

<mark style="color:red;">**Rend**</mark> - 2 claws, 2d6 (7) sonic

<mark style="color:red;">**Plow Through**</mark> - Creatures shoved by a Hell Knight are pushed 20 feet instead of 10. If they impact a solid surface, they take 2d6 (7) crush damage.

<mark style="color:red;">**Explosive Entry \[sonic]**</mark> - In place of making attacks at the end of a charge, when it spends its action to break a barrier or when it willingly takes fall damage (still a standard action), the hell knights slam erupts in concussive force. Creatures within reach take 6d6 (21) sonic damage and are staggered for 1 round (fort halves damage and negates stagger).

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Bloodlust**</mark> - Once fighting begins, a hell knight must use its standard action to attack or otherwise attempt to inflict damage/CC. It takes 3 psychic damage every turn it does not.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Weakness**</mark> - 3 holy

## <mark style="color:yellow;background-color:yellow;">Other Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str +3, Dex +1, Lucc +0, Int -3, Wis +0, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Improved Leap**</mark> - Can high jump as high as its long jump (20 ft)

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Smashing**</mark> - 1/turn as a free action, roll an athletics check to break free, break an object or move things

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Speed**</mark> - 40

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Feats**</mark> - [Unstoppable](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/unstoppable), [Diehard](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/diehard), [Maneuver Initiate](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/maneuver-initiate)

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Skills**</mark> - +7 perception, +18 athletics, +10 intimidation
