<mark style="color:red;">**Censor \[sonic]**</mark> - Whenever a creature says a curse word, or something particularly vulgar, a censorship fairy can cause a black box to appear over their mouth, and substitute the word with a loud beeping sound as an immediate action. The target creature's word cannot be heard, and they take d10 (5) sonic damage.

This breaks invisibility.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str -2, Dex +1, Lucc +3, Int +0, Wis +3, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Censorship (Su) \[figment] \[concentration]**</mark> - Censorship fairies specialize in a very particular type of censorship: That of protecting the nudey bits of naked creatures. They can create incredibly detailed and fine-tuned illusions, but only to cover up breasts and other erogenous zones in incredibly tasteful ways, such as a bunch of leaves in the wind, a ray of light or something more elaborate and unusual.

They can censor such things by creating the figment as a standard action, and maintaining it as a free action. They need to stay within 30 feet of the creature they censor to maintain the illusion.
