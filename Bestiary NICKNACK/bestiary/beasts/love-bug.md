<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +2, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +2, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +0

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 12)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Grapple**</mark> +6 (+4 MI)

<mark style="color:red;">**Pheromones \[charm]**</mark> - Whenever a love bug grapples a creature, it releases powerful pheromones, charming the creature for 1 minute (fort negates). This charm does not stop the love bug from making grapple attacks.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Juicy**</mark> - You can extract the juices from a love bug with a DC 12 alchemy or survival check. Used on its own it can inflict the charmed condition for 1 minute on the first creature it sees after ingesting. With a DC 20 alchemy check, it can be turned into a love potion.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Charmed I'm Sure**</mark> - Love bugs have disadvantage on \[charm] effects.
