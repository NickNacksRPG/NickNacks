# Big Cat

**Level 4 Large Beast**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 18\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 60\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 32\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +7, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +9, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +5

<mark style="color:green;">**Graceful**</mark> - Big cats take 1 damage per 10 feet they fall and take no poise damage from falling.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 16)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Bite (P)**</mark>  (can only be used against a grappled creature)\
4d6+3 (17)

<mark style="color:red;">**Claw (S)**</mark>  +7\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Make 2 claw attacks, one of which must be a grapple

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Technique**</mark> - [Roar](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/cataclysm/level-1/roar), [Pack Attack](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-1/pack-attack)\
1/day - [Jaws of the Beast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-2/jaws-of-the-beast)

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: Bushwack**</mark> - While hiding, a big cat can take the jump action and attack a creature, dealing an additional 2d8 (9) dmg. If big cat successfully hides, it can also grapple the creature as an instant action.

<mark style="color:red;">**Rend**</mark> - 2 claws, or a grapple and a claw (2d6)

<mark style="color:red;">**Species**</mark> - Big cats come in a variety of shapes and sizes, each with their own unique boons.

<mark style="color:orange;">Cheetah</mark> - Gain powerful charge. When it charges or dashes in a straight line, it can move up to 160 feet.\
<mark style="color:orange;">Lion</mark> - Can innately use the [Warcry](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/warfare/level-3/warcry) technique 1/day.\
<mark style="color:orange;">Jaguar/Panther</mark> - While grappled by a jaguar, the target is also staggered.\
<mark style="color:orange;">Tiger</mark> - While grappled by a tiger, the creature is also knocked down.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Watery Grave**</mark> - Big cats will avoid water if they can get away with it and prefer attacking targets not in water. If made wet, a big cat will always spend their swift action to remove the wet condition with a violent shake.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str +3, Dex +3, Lucc +0, Int -3, Wis +1, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Speed**</mark> - 40

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Blindsense**</mark> - Scent (10 ft)

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Enhanced Leap**</mark> - Big cats can leap up to 30 ft

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Favored Terrain**</mark> - In its natural habitat, a big cat gets a +4 bonus on stealth checks

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Feats**</mark> - [SF Stealth](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/general-feats/skill-focus), [Maneuver Initiate](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/maneuver-initiate), [Swift Grappler](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/maneuver-initiate/swift-grappler)
