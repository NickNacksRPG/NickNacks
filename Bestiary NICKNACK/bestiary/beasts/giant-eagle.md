# Giant Eagle

**Level 2 Large Beast**

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 15\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 40\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 19\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +3, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +7, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +3

<mark style="color:green;">**Let Freedom Ring (Su) \[recharge]**</mark>  - As a standard action, an eagle can cry out to free all creatures within 100 ft. Any creature that is grappled, entangled or entombed automatically loses the condition and becomes immune for 1 round.

<mark style="color:green;">**Freedom**</mark> - At the start of the eagle's turn, it loses the entangled and entombed conditions. It is freed from a grapple.

<mark style="color:green;">**Flyby Attack**</mark> - An eagle's movement is \[elusive] against targets it has attacked this round.
