# Bone Beetle

**Level 2 Small Undead/Beast**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 23\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 50\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 24\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +3, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +9, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +3

<mark style="color:green;">**Immune**</mark> - Poison, Disease

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Necrotic

<mark style="color:green;">**Reformation**</mark> - Instead of being defeated, a bog creeper immediately dissipates into several bone beetles. These beetles act on the bog creepers turn. If any remain at the end of the bog creepers next turn (including any it summoned previously), the bog creeper reforms at the point closest to all that remain with (beetles\*10) hp.

<mark style="color:green;">**Carrion Consumption \[healing] \[recharge]**</mark> - Bog creepers often keep decaying and diseased creatures inside of its lair for later consumption. As a swift action, a bog creeper can consume such a creature to gain 20 temp hp and recharge its breath weapon.
