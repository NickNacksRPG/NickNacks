# The Wall

**Level 3 Medium Dwarf**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 21 (+4 vs creatures attacked)\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 60\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 49  (DRA 1, DR 1, DR 10 vs knockdown)\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +8, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +6, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +4

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Poison, +2 vs charm

<mark style="color:green;">**Can't Go Through It**</mark> - The wall gets advantage on reflex saves against acrobatics to avoid AoOs. Creatures cannot move through the wall's space through any means.

<mark style="color:green;">**Can't Knock it Down**</mark> - The wall has DR 10 vs knockdown. Any other effect that would otherwise inflict knockdown, the wall has advantage against.

<mark style="color:green;">**Chip Damage**</mark> - The wall is immune to being grazed.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 16)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Dual Wield Tower Shield (C)**</mark>  +6 (+2 topple, +2 shove)\
d8+3 (7)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Boasting Taunt](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/warfare/level-1/boasting-taunt), [Shield Bash](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/protector/level-2/shield-bash), [Elemental Barrier](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/protector/level-2/elemental-barrier), [Colossal Smash](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/warfare/level-2/colossal-smash), \
1/day [Bouncing Bulwark](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/techniques/martial-techniques/protector/level-2/bouncing-bulwark)

<mark style="color:red;">**Freight Train**</mark> - Whenever the wall makes a shove attack while charging, he can carry the creature along with him for the full distance of the charge, depositing them adjacent at the end of his charge.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Jam**</mark> - The wall has his own theme song that he cant help but dance to when he hears it. By rolling a DC 20 perform check as a standard action, a creature can daze the wall on his next turn.
