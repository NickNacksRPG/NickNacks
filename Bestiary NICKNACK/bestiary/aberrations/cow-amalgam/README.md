# Cow Amalgam

**Level 4 Large Aberration/Beast**

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 18\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 69\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 27\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +6, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +11, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +4

<mark style="color:green;">**Amorphous**</mark> - AC +15

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 16)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Stomp (C)**</mark>  +9\
3d6+3 (13)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Power Overwhelming](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/frogg-saran/level-1/power-overwhelming)

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: 8-Sided Stomp -**</mark> Make a stomp attack against all creatures within reach. Can be used an unlimited number of times a combat.

<mark style="color:red;">**Breath Weapon \[poison] \[recharge]**</mark> - A bovider can vomit a torrent of rotten milk from its many mouths. Targets a 30 ft cone, dealing 6d6 (21) poison damage, making creatures wet and sickened 2 (reflex halves damage and negates additional status). Must use its calciumancer ability to recharge.

<mark style="color:red;">**Calciumancer (Su)**</mark> - A bovider can target any female mammalian creature within 30 ft and drain them of their milk (even if they aren't currently producing). The creature is drained 1 and made incredibly uncomfortable (fort reduces drain to stagger for 1 round).

<mark style="color:red;">**Spawn**</mark> - A bovider under duress will spit out a single spawn at the start of its turn unless it already has 2 fighting by its side.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Jolt**</mark> - Whenever a bovider takes zap damage, it immediately recoils away from the source. It and its spawn are unable to attack creatures that has dealt zap damage to it in the last 1 round.
