Hellion

The horny race.

Language Infernal
Size Medium or small
Type Humanoid, Fiend
Major Default
Darkvision You can see in the dark as well as you could in light, albeit in grayscale.
Innate Techniques You can cast a series of spells, each 1/day
LV 1 - wave of darkness
LV 3 - Cloud of Darkness
LV 5 - encroaching darkness
Minor Default
Tail You have an additional prehensile limb that can manipulate objects and hold them just as your hands would. You cannot wield weapons with your tail.
Delicious and Nutritious You can consume dirt, sand, ash, dust etc. for nourishment.

Replacement Features

Major Replace
Infernal Engine You gain resistance to fire damage. Anything you touch takes 1 fire damage per turn. This does not apply to attacks, but it does apply when you grapple or are grappled.
Sacrificial Pact (Su) (Healing) By cutting yourself and bleeding on another creature as a standard action, you take (3+LV) True Damage, and heal the creature for double that amount. You can only heal a maximum of your current hp.
Horny You have pointed or ram-like horns and a skull that's harder than most. You gain a natural attack with d10 (crush or pierce) base damage.
You can make a simple attack with these horns as a swift action, but its damage decreases to d4 and you become clumsy 1 on hit from how disorienting it is to swing your head into creatures.
Natural Armor Your body is covered in scales, obsidian, or metal plates that protect you, but limit your movement and chafes against traditional armor. Your AC is 14+lvl. You cannot add DEX to AC and cannot wear armor. Natural armor also does not give poise.
Minor Replace
Maw and Claw Even without a weapon, you are always armed. You have a set of claws and a bite that deal d10 base damage.
Glide You have tiny vestigial wings. While not movement impaired, take no fall damage. When falling, move horizontally 10 feet per 5 foot fallen at a speed of 60.
Alter Self poly, true Choose one specific human shape that cannot be altered. Usable 1/day for 10 minutes. Minor fiendish features remain.

Lore

Numbers Stand around 5'6 and weigh around 150 lbs
Small variants are about half as tall and 1/5 the weight

Hellion are adults by 15 and live to be around 70 years old.
When created they usually form biologically in early adulthood.
Appearance and Biology Hellion are roughly human-shaped, but can come in many different forms. Many have spaded or forked tails they can use to grab objects. Some have vestigial wings. The most common feature of a Hellion is the horns on their head. They come in all different shapes and sizes: Long curved horns like a ram, short pointed horns, multiple pairs of horns, only a single horn. They can superficially resemble the race they were before their fiendish transformation with pointed elf ears or orcish tusks, or they can more closely resemble the fiend they transformed int. Hellion can have wildly different skin colors from crimson red to a grayish blue. Some of them even have furry digitigrade lower halves with cloven hooves.

Hellion that try to mesh well into society will dress in whatever the locals wear. As a result of how they are created, many take the opportunity to devote themselves to 🌖Brazzeria and wear her ceremonial garb. Though the stereotypical freshly created hellion will likely get piercings, decorate their horns in jewelry and wear indecent clothing just to be contrarian.
Open Your Heart Hellion are created by a Brazzerian purification ritual used on demons that were once mortal. Many fiends are beyond redemption, but those that aren't can be exposed to kindness, care and respect to slowly restore their humanity. Brazerrian priests and priestesses are able to read the hearts to fiends to tell whether they are closed or open, representing their receptiveness to healing the fiendish corruption. Over time they can (usually) be exposed to enough kindness to eventually open their heart and begin the transformation.

Freshly transformed hellions have no memories of their previous life before being a fiend, but they do vividly remember what happened during their time as a fiend: All of the suffering they caused, all the lives they’ve taken and all of the kindness they were treated with in order to regain their humanity. After some rehabilitation, they are given a choice to join the cause or return back to society.
Racism Unfortunately for hellion, there are scars caused by fiends all over the world. Many people do not understand the intricacies of fiendish creation, or the humanity restoration the hellion undergo. As such, newly created hellion that do not stay under the protective wings of Brazzeria can often be in for a rough time. Small communities of hellion can often be found on the fringes of places where there is a great deal of Brazzerian influence.

The racism can go both ways; providing hardship as well as opportunity. Hellion will often find themselves getting approached by unsavory individuals looking for a new hire in their organized crime ring. They will also find it difficult to shake off the "sex demon" stereotype perpetuated by the prolific and ever-present succubi/twinkubi. It does serve them well when finding dates or getting jobs at brothels at least.
