Cheezette

Level 6 Medium Elemental

Defense Traits

AC 19
HP 79
Poise 26
Fort +8, Refl +8, Will +8

Resistance - Fire

Amorphous - +13

Never Alone - Cannot be flanked

Soggy [recharge] - As an instant action, a cheezette can absorb a [water] effect, automatically negating it. It can do this once an hour.

Offense Traits (DC 18)

Molten Cheese Touch (fire) +8 +Sizzle
4d8+3 (21)

Innate Techniques - Blazing Palm, Flame Cleanse Me, Gooball, Splatter
1/day - Mirror Images, To the Rescue!, Blazing Beam

Sizzle - Some of the hot cheese is left behind by a cheezettes touch. It is applied on hit, and functions exactly as ablaze.

Sisters in Arms - Cheezette functions more like a two-headed creature, so it still only gets 1 turn, but the two assist each other in combat. At start of turn, gain 1 benefit for 1 round.

  • Gain advantage on attacks
  • Gain advantage on CL checks
  • Gain advantage on skill checks
  • Gain advantage on saving throws

Dangerously Cheesy [fire] - When creature melee attacks a cheezete, the weapon they use to attack gets stuck unless they succeed on athletics to pull it out. If they fail and they don’t let go of the weapon, it is heated up and they take 3d6 (10) fire damage. It can wield any weapons that are left behind in it.

If the attack is a projectile weapon, they can spit it back out at their enemy, making an attack with the same range as an immediate action.

Summon Ally - 1 Cheetah (big cat variant)

Weaknesses/Deep Lore

Topping Overload - For every weapon stuck inside of it past the first, a cheezette has its speed lowered by 5. It can remove a stuck weapon from itself as a swift action.

Weakness - Poison 3

Other Traits

Ability Scores - Str +2, Dex +2, Lucc +3, Int +0, Wis +1, Cha +1

Speed - 30 Hover

Feats - Divine Interference, Point Blank Master, Toughness

Skills - +9 perc, +11 medicine, +9 persuasion
