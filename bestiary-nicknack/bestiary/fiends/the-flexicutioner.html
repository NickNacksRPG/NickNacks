Multiattack - Make a combat maneuver attack and an axe attack.

Innate Techniques - Foe Toss, Colossal Smash, Charming Gaze

Flexicution - Fang forces a creature within reach to roll an opposed athletics check. If they win, she provokes an AoO. If she wins, the creature is crushed, dealing 4d6 (14) crush damage and inflicting drained 1. If this defeats the creature, she can immediately foe toss them.

Breath Weapon (Su) [fire] [recharge] - Fang unlocks her jaw and unleashes a torrent of concentrated flames in a 60 ft line. Affected creatures take 6d6 (21) fire damage and are set ablaze (reflex halves dmg and negates ablaze).

Abyssal Rift (Su) [concentration] [persistent] [recharge] [necrotic] - Fang slices the ground with her axe and temporarily opens up a rift to the underworld. A 15 ft line in front of her cracks open and becomes inaccessible, shunting all creatures in the AoE to the side. Damned souls reach for any creatures within 15 feet to pull them into the rift. They roll a +13 drag attempt immediately against all creatures within 15 feet, and passively when a creature walks into range. All poise broken creatures are partially dragged into the rift before getting ejected, taking 6d6 (21) necrotic damage.

The rift persists for 1 minute and has a 1 minute cooldown.

Summon Ally [companion] - 2 pampers

Boss Actions - A boss action is usable 1/turn as an instant action with specific triggers. Can only be used at the end of an opposing creature's turn.

Mwah - When a creature ends their turn within reach, use charming gaze targeting it.
Out of my Way - Roll athletics instead of acrobatics as though trying to evade AoOs. If Fang succeeds, she tosses the creature aside 10 feet and they are knocked down for 1 round.
Mwah - W

Weaknesses/Deep Lore

Choice Attire - Whenever Fang is subject to an effect with the [air] tag, she always spends her immediate action to keep herself covered up. If she is unable to, she is shaken 1.

Flattery - By rolling a DC 20 persuasion or deception check as a standard action, a creature can fluster fang, causing her to be charmed by them. Succeeding on a second check gives her the time out condition, provided there are no obvious signs of continued aggression or betrayal. Any fiends of her level or lower automatically stop fighting at her request. Higher level fiends require persuasion from her.

Hey Mom - By expending prayer, Fang can communicate directly with her mother, The Cataclysm, as though upcasting sending.

Ability Scores - Str +4, Dex +0, Lucc +0, Int -1, Wis +3, Cha +1
