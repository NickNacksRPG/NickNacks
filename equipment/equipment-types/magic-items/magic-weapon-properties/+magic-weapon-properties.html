+Magic Weapon Properties

These properties can be attached to most weapons. Classes like ⚔Fighter and Magus can add these properties to their weapon temporarily.

A weapon can typically only be enchanted with one of these properties. Weapons with additional enchantment slots can get more than 1.
Such enchantments cost 5,000 gold.

Bane+2 att/dmg vs a specific creature type (plant, giant, etc).
Feels strange to hold a weapon baned against your creature type
BenevolentIncrease the bonus of the aid action by +2 while in combat while wielding this weapon (doesn't stack with itself).
DivingThis weapon doesn't have reduced effectiveness when used underwater.
ElementalWeapon can swap between its usual damage and a specific type of elemental damage (determined on creation) as a free action 1/turn.
FortuitousOnce per round, when the wielder of a fortuitous weapon hits with an attack of opportunity, they can make a second attack of opportunity with this weapon against that foe at a –5 penalty.
Ghost TouchWeapon treats incorporeal creatures as corporeal
GroundingWeapon attacks against flying creatures inflict grounded for 1 round (fort negates)
HemorrhagingWeapon gains the bleed property.
ImperviousDR 10 vs ALL sunder, not just against the enchanted item
InvigoratingWhen you defeat a creature with this weapon, you lose the fatigued condition. If you are not fatigued, get +10 speed this turn.
LockingOn hit you can lock the weapon in place, entangling the creature struck (reflex negates). While entangling a creature, you cannot use the weapon.
MimeticWeapon attacks against a creature allows the wielder to copy one of the creatures elemental resistances for one round. You can only have one copied resistance at a time. On hit the wielder has an intuitive sense the creature is resistant to, allowing them to choose.
Phase LockingOn hit apply anchored for 1 round. Anchored creatures cannot use teleportation effects.
RadiantThe weapon is constantly emitting light as per the Radiance spell.
ReturningAs a free action, you can call your weapon back to your hand. If it is within 60 feet of you it returns instantly. Anything beyond requires 3 rounds. If there are any physical barriers preventing its return, this does not work.
If a creature possesses the weapon (reflex negates).
SeekingWeapon attacks ignore partial concealment, total concealment and other forms of miss chance created by the environment or magic.
Summon BaneFunctions as bane, but against summoned creatures.
StickyDR 10 vs ALL disarm, not just against the enchanted item
TransformativeAs a swift action you can change your weapon into another type of weapon of any type (sword>gun>dagger etc).
You can use this to give new special attacks based on damage type, but not special attacks that normally require weapon points like determination.
Vicious+2 dmg. On hit the wielder takes 2 true damage.
