Seal, Standard

500g

A seal is a vague title given to an item of significance to channel magic power through. For more information see Staff Component

Seals are typically attuned to a specific type of magic, such as Brazzeria (T) or 🔥Pyromancy, allowing you to cast spells of that tree with +1 CL.
