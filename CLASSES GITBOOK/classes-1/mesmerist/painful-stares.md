# Painful Stares

Effects with a number require a specific level.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Staring Daggers</mark>

Your stare is painful in the most literal sense. When triggered, the creature takes an additional d6+CL (3+CL) pierce damage.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Stink Eye</mark>

Your stare fills the creatures nostrils with a powerful stench. When triggered, the creature becomes sickened 1.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Bloodthirst</mark>

Your stare causes the creature to believe they are hemophiliac. The immediately begin bleeding for d6 damage.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Ice Cold Stare \[frost]</mark>

The creature believes ice creeps across its body. When triggered, the creature becomes frosty.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Dirty Look</mark>

Your gaze holds a deep unsatisfied hunger for the creature so powerful it forces them to avert their gaze from you. When triggered, they are DD against you specifically until the end of your next turn.
