# Cheerleader

Cheerleader bards excel at bringing their allies up when times are the toughest with encouraging words and healing effects.&#x20;

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Malefic Performance</mark>

Blues bards have the bardic performance ability, but with a few key differences.&#x20;

* Their performances vary in action type, with some of the more powerful performances requiring a standard action to begin.&#x20;
* They can only ever effect enemies with their performances.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Performance: Raise Morale</mark>

The cheerleader only ever gets a singular performance, and allies can choose to gain any one benefit from the performance, deciding at the start of their turn. Below are level requirements for providing a particular benefit.

1 - <mark style="color:green;">Raise Morale</mark> - Increase allied speed by 10 and give them advantage on saves against fear

1 - <mark style="color:green;">Inspire Courage</mark> - +2 attack

4 - <mark style="color:green;">Song of \[Healing]</mark> - Restore 3 hp.

1 - <mark style="color:green;">Raise Dongers</mark> - ???

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Inspire Competence</mark>

A cheerleader can use the aid action as a swift action. They are always grant the improved +2 bonus when aiding in a skill check through their tunes.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Finale: Purging Finale \[healing]</mark>

You automatically remove all non-permanent negative status effects from an ally within 30 ft of you. They can take a swift or immediate action as an instant action.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Inspiration \[mana burn]</mark>

By burning 1 mana as an immediate action, you can allow a creature within 30 ft to add d6 to a d20 roll (minus luck checks).\
@6 increase to d8\
@11 increase to d10

## <mark style="color:green;">4 - Finale: Reviving Finale \[healing]</mark>

Restore (3\*lvl) hp to all allies within 30 ft. Defeated creatures healed are not dazed on their next turn.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Finale: Bass Drop</mark>

The music sticks with your allies even after the performance ends. They gain (5\*lvl) temp hp. This temp hp disappears at the end of your next turn.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Finale: Stoccato of Surrender</mark>

Enemies within 60 ft are pacified for 1 round (will negates). When they are attacked by you or your allies, they get a new save against the effect.
