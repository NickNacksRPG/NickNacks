## <mark style="color:green;">1 - One With Nature (Su) \[poly, true]</mark>

As a swift action, you can copy any inanimate plant you can see within 30 ft. This transformation changes your size/appearance. You can speak normally while in plant form. You can also have the face of someone you know appear along the surface of the plant in leaves or bark. This transformation lasts indefinitely, but can be ended as a swift action.

While transformed you have advantage on stealth, perception, disguise and deception. If a creature saw you transform or is already very familiar with your voice, you lose the advantage on deception, disguise and stealth.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Talent</mark>

You have advantage on wild empathy checks against plant creatures.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Bramble Back</mark>

You can cause the vegetation that grows on you to become dangerously pointy as a free action. Creatures you are currently in contact with take 2d6 (7) pierce damage. Creatures you initiate contact against, or that initiate contact against you also take this damage.

## <mark style="color:green;">4 - New Recruits (Sp)</mark>

You are constantly under the effects of arboreal army (mother earth 2). Their attack bonus increases by 2.

You can use them to perform any combat maneuvers instead of only bullying.
