# Martial Artist

Many warriors fight with weapons or spells, but some choose to make their own body the ultimate weapon. Through grit, asceticism or flashy techniques, a martial artist's combat prowess shines without the aid of arms or armor.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Technique Specialization (Martial)</mark>

You gain 1 technique points at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.\
These technique points can be used on the unarmed or barbarism trees.

Characters with martial specialization gain points in warfare equal to their highest tier of martial technique.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Feat</mark>

Gain the [unarmed combatant](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/unarmed-combatant) feat. Your unarmed damage dice increases to d10.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Unarmored Defense</mark>

You are considered to be wearing medium armor for the purposes of determining your AC. Gain 6 poise.\
Functions only while not wearing armor and not wielding a shield.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Flurry</mark>

Make an unarmed attack as a swift action. Some additional bonuses/restrictions exist depending on your archetype.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Fitness</mark>

+5 speed per level. The jump action can be used with 1/turn as a free action. Still only usable 1/turn. You can jump an unlimited number of times when you dash. You can dash as as swift action.\
Functions only while not wearing armor and not wielding a shield.
