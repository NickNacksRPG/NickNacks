# Beast Totem

Embrace your wild side. Beast totem barbarians tend to be furrier and feathery-er than most.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Totem Feature - Beast Buddy \[companion]</mark>

You gain the beast trainer's [beast companion](../../beast-trainer/#id-1-beast-companion-companion) class feature. You are limited to choosing the <mark style="color:red;">stronk</mark> type companion. Your companion starts off with 2 adaptation points, and gains no more as it levels. Its level is your level-1. (yes you can be level 0)

## <mark style="color:red;">Rage Powers</mark>

Numbers are level requirements

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Bestial Movement (Su)</mark>

You and your beast companion gain an additional 40 speed each round that can only be used to climb or swim. As a swift action, you can sprout wings to gain the glide feature for 1 round.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Druidic Training</mark>

You get knowledge nature proficiency with a +4 bonus. You can use it while raging. You also know [druidic](https://app.gitbook.com/s/ffUc9JSjKiYL5XuDfVdc/skills/linguistics/languages).

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Blood Scent (Su)</mark>

Gain blindsense (scent, 10 ft). You and your beast companion gain seeking against any creature that does not have full hp.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Spirit Animal (Su) \[poly, true]</mark>

Choose a specific animal you can transform into and back from as a swift action. Gain a single natural attack that deals 2d6 damage and 3 points worth of abilities from the [shapeshifter's shapeshift](../../shapeshifter/polymorph.md) class feature that only apply when in spirit animal form. Once chosen, these features cannot be altered.

Your appearance is slightly unnatural for the type of creature you transform into, typically being larger and more dangerous looking with glowing eyes or strange fur patterns.

Your companion can also transform into this form as an instant action when you do.

## <mark style="color:green;">2 - Beast Speech (Su)</mark>

You can speak with beasts as though you shared a common language. They are able to much better articulate their feelings as though they possessed the intellect of a simple humanoid, but their instincts, desires and attitude toward you remain largely the same.

## <mark style="color:green;">4 - Fetch</mark>

When your beast companion successfully performs a drag maneuver, it gains an additional 30 speed. It must be able to end this movement with the drag target in a space you threaten if it uses this bonus speed.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Twice as Angry</mark>

Whenever you rage, your beast companion can rage as an instant action, gaining the benefits of your rage.
