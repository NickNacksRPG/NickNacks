## <mark style="color:green;">4 - Shape Change (Su) \[poly, true]</mark>

You can change your appearance to that of any small or medium playable race. Can change back as a standard action, or as a swift action when you break the seal.

## <mark style="color:green;">6 - Hellfire (Su) \[persistent] \[fire]</mark>

Gain a breath weapon.\
Unleash a 30 ft cone or 60 ft line of flames that persists until the start of your next turn. Creatures within the area, or that later move into it take (2d6+d6/odd lvl) fire damage (reflex halves). This ability ignores fire resistance.

Recharged by consuming an organ of a freshly killed playable race, or by resting.

## <mark style="color:green;">10 - Death From Above</mark>

Grow a pair of wings that grant you a fly speed equal to your land speed.
