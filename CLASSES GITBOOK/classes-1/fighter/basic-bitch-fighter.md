# Basic Bitch Fighter

For when you really don't want to think about complicated class features.

## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Superior Stats</mark>

You gain a bonus feat every level. Every time you gain a technique point, you can choose to instead turn it into a feat.

You get access to the following feats, each of which can be taken any number of times.

<mark style="color:green;">Ability Increase</mark> - Increase an ability score by +1 (up to +3)

<mark style="color:green;">Super Strength</mark> - Increase strength by +1 (up to +5).

<mark style="color:green;">Super Armor</mark> - Gain +1 AC and poise.

<mark style="color:green;">Paladin Cosplay</mark> - Get a +1 bonus to saving throws.

<mark style="color:green;">Superior Genetics</mark> - Get an additional major feature from your race
