## <mark style="color:green;">1 - Elemental Intuition: Firebending \[concentration]</mark>

You can physically manipulate fire within 30 ft. Unlike water and earth, fire cannot create structures, nor can it lift enormous quantities of matter. You can ignite objects as well as move or snuff out flames of (5 ft cube per lvl). By touching objects, you can drastically increase their temperature or absorb extreme heat.

Initiating and maintaining concentration on fire bending requires a standard action each turn.

## <mark style="color:green;">3 - Elemental Intuition: Charge</mark>

You can decrease your speed by 30 to add your level to damage on the next (Su) ability from the bender class that deals fire or zap damage this turn. This has a distinct audio and visual effect that imposes -10 penalty to stealth.

## <mark style="color:green;">5 - Elemental Intuition: Firebending Adept</mark>

Your blast or elemental wave damage can deal fire or zap damage interchangeably. You gain resistance to zap damage. You can manipulate the flow of electricity similar to how you can manipulate fire.

## <mark style="color:green;">9 - Elemental Intuition: Jet Propulsion \[recharge]</mark>
