# Legendary Weapon

You have a special weapon that you use to vanquish your enemies. It's name, legendary. It's purpose, divine. This indestructible weapon will be passed down from champion to champion for all of eternity.

(if dual wielding is your thing, no issue in making it twinned weapons)

<mark style="color:green;">Bonus Feat</mark> - Gain the bonded weapon feat, applying only to your legendary weapon(s).

<mark style="color:green;">Indestructible</mark> -  This weapon cannot be sundered. It's destruction can only be brought about through a series of complex rituals, requiring the intervention of deities or some other incredibly powerful force of destruction, determined upon ascension.

<mark style="color:green;">Intelligent</mark> - It may or may not be able to communicate, but it can at least understand language and perceive the world around it. Its bonded wielder can receive knowledge from it through language or psychic impressions conveying the information. It has a +20 knowledge bonus concerning monsters of a specific type (plants, fiends etc.). Gain bane against this creature type.

<mark style="color:green;">Unique Property</mark> - Each legendary weapon is unique and has something that aids and defeating its favored foe. Some examples below.
