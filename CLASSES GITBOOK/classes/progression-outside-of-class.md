# Progression Outside of Class

Just so its all in one place.

<mark style="color:green;">Boons/Banes</mark> - Gained at level one as a result of having positive or negative luck.

<mark style="color:green;">Feats</mark> - Gained at level 1, and every odd level after.

<mark style="color:green;">Technique Points</mark> - Gain one that can be used on any school of techniques at level 2, and every even level after.

<mark style="color:green;">Ascendance</mark> - Unique template applied at level 12.

<mark style="color:green;">Achievements</mark> - Can be gained at any time, even retroactively, when certain conditions are met, varying based on the achievement
