# Profession

The skill for the working class fellas. Defaults to wisdom, but see the experienced feature.

<details>

<summary>Recall</summary>

If you are ever quizzed on particular aspects of a profession and you are not proficient, you must roll an intelligence check to answer correctly (DC 10 for most professions). Proficient creatures automatically know most aspects of their profession.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Hard Work</summary>

Every day you work, you earn money. See economics for more details.\
Your talent could be anything from music, to craftsmanship to working as an innkeeper.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Experienced (P)</summary>

You can select what ability score to use to determine your profession bonus for all your professions. Your given ability score colors the approach you have to your professions.

</details>
