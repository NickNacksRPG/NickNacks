# Alchemy

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Potion/Poison (creation DC)</mark></summary>

With a quick wiff or taste, you can determine the effects of an alchemical substance.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Reverse Engineer (creation DC+5)</mark></summary>

You can take an unfamiliar substance and learn what it's made out of with the intention of creating it again in the future. A success indicates that you managed to write down the necessary ingredients and processes to create the item in the future.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Create Alchemical Item (DC varies)</mark> </summary>

Alchemy can make all kinds of different things: common things include healing wounds, inflicting wounds, preventing sickness, adhesives, solvents, starting fires, temporary adaptations. Uncommon things include cloning and permanent transformations.

All found under the equipment tab.

</details>
