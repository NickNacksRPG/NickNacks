# Planes

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Portal (DC 10)</mark></summary>

In the presence of a portal, you can tell where its destination plane is. Rolling higher can give more specific locations, more details about the portals creation and how often it gets used.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Find Portal Location (DC 30)</mark></summary>

Portals are strange phenomenon that tend to come and go, but there are signs that they exist miles away from where the portal is actually located.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Disable/Destroy Portal (DC 30)</mark></summary>

A task that is very difficult by one's self. Failure could mean getting stuck on the wrong side of the portal or hyper charging the portal to attract extraplanar creatures.

</details>
