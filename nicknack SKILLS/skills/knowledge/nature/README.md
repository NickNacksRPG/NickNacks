# Nature

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Natural Phenomenon</mark></summary>

You know about natural processes like photosynthesis, the reproduction of creatures, and tectonic activity. You can do things like figuring out if a volcano is about to erupt, what could be causing plants to die or how long until an egg hatches.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Identify Elements of Nature</mark></summary>

If it doesn't have a stat block and it isn't man-made or magic, then it probably falls under natural element. Nature allows you to identify animal behavior. It also lets you identify types of plants and minerals and their uses/dangers. It also allows you to tell if something is unnatural.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Weather</mark></summary>

Predict the weather, usually only for the day, but with higher checks, even farther into the future. You can also tell if these effects are naturally occurring or created by something unusual like magic.

</details>
