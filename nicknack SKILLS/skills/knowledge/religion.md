# Religion

It is assumed that if you are of a specific religion, you can do many of these things without rolling for your own faith.

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Religious Symbol (DC 10)</mark></summary>

Followers of gods often wear regalia, use specific types of seals and put their gods symbol on their stuff. Sometimes just by looking at a building or a person you can tell where their religious affiliations lie.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Recall Tenets (DC 15)</mark></summary>

When prompted by specific actions, you can tell whether or not these actions align with the deity in question. Good for finding fakers or guiding the faithful with poor memory.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Accrue GBP (DC 15)</mark></summary>

You can figure out what kinds of things would appease a specific deity and earn you GBP.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Identify Chosen (DC 20)</mark></summary>

Gods will often play favorites when it comes to specific people, peoples or items/places when deciding who will receive their blessing.

</details>
