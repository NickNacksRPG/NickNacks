# Arcana

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Spell Being Cast (DC 15+spell lvl, no action)</mark> </summary>

You must be able to clearly see or hear the creature in order to identify a spell that they are casting. Useful for determining whether or not you want to dispel something, or figuring out what spell a creature cast that has no immediate visible effect.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;">Identify Active Spell (DC 10+spell lvl, free action 1/turn)</mark></summary>

You examine the magical effect to determine what spell it is, and what the spell does.

</details>

<details>

<summary><mark style="color:green;">Identify Magic Properties of Item (DC=Craft DC, +10 if cursed, standard action)</mark> </summary>

You learn all of the qualities of a magic item that you are adjacent to.

</details>
