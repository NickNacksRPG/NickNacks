# Example DCs

<table data-header-hidden><thead><tr><th width="131"></th><th width="63"></th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Difficulty</td><td>DC</td><td>Examples</td></tr><tr><td>Mundane</td><td>0</td><td><p>Assistance with a simple task, like cutting veggies</p><p>Gathering information on a topic people are concerned with</p></td></tr><tr><td>Requires Focus</td><td>5</td><td>Assistance with a time consuming task,</td></tr><tr><td>Trained</td><td>10</td><td>Gathering less relevant information: did x person wander into town lately?</td></tr><tr><td>Difficult</td><td>15</td><td>Gathering dangerous information, the kind of thing that makes people say "you didn't hear it from me, but…"</td></tr><tr><td>Very Difficult</td><td>20</td><td>Convince people to do something dangerous and potentially life threatening, like escorting you through wilderness, standing up against a bully or breaking the law</td></tr><tr><td>Masterful</td><td>25</td><td>Gathering secretive information, such as hideouts, passwords or scandals</td></tr><tr><td>Nearly Impossible</td><td>30</td><td>Convince people to do something against their ethos, but could be conceivable, such as killing baby monsters, putting someone out of their misery or ditching the religion of a corrupt priest</td></tr><tr><td>Ascended</td><td>40</td><td>Convincing people inconceivable things, like killing family members, nobles donating their wealth to charity or rallying commoners against an ancient dragon (given adequate circumstances)</td></tr></tbody></table>

&#x20;Common modifiers to make the task more difficult can include…\
\>Racism\
\>Your questions/requests are offensive or closely guarded secrets\
\>You are not well liked by the person/people you are persuading

&#x20;Common modifiers to make the task less difficult can include\
\>You are well liked by the person/people you are persuading\
\>You hold a position of authority over the person/people you are persuading\
\>The information you are looking for is well known or noteworthy
