# Peace

## <mark style="color:green;">Expensive (5,000g)</mark>

This badge is a symbol of peace. If you perform no action on your turn in combat, you are affected as per [sanctuary](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/protector/level-1/sanctuary-su) until the start of your next turn.

This has a 1 minute cooldown.
