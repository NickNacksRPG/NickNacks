# Elemental Staff

## <mark style="color:green;">2000g (DC 12)</mark>

This staff has a mana crystal affixed to its end, shimmering with a color that indicates the type of element it favors. All spells with the elemental tag shared by the staff get +1 CL. 1/day it can reduce the mana cost of such a spell by 1.

<mark style="color:red;">Manathirst 12</mark>\
1/day you can spend 1 mana to change the elemental damage type of the spell to the stave's elemental affinity.



The standard staff is a 1h crush weapon.\
Also comes in a battle staff and light variant, like most staves.
