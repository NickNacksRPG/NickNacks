# Benevolent Blade

## <mark style="color:green;">1,200g (DC 11)</mark>

This silver sword has a hilt that shines a radiant gold. This weapon is used to non-lethally dispatch creatures when defeating them since it has no chance of potentially killing them.

<mark style="color:green;">Special Attack: Lifesteal \[healing]</mark>\
On hit heal (2+lvl).

<mark style="color:red;">Manathirst 7</mark>\
<mark style="color:green;">Special Attack: Benevolence \[healing]</mark>\
Strike at an ally as the blade transforms into a warming radiance. It grants the attacks damage as temp hp and has no harmful on hit effects.



The sword is a 1h slash or pierce weapon with the one and done property.
