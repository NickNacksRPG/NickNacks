# Fiery War Axe

## <mark style="color:green;">5000g (DC 14)</mark>

Favored by veteran fighters, this axe is the color of obsidian, with a blade that looks white hot. When pointing the weapon at any creature that is considered a minion to you, it ignites in flames. Against any such foe, the axe deals an additional d10 fire damage.

The axe is a 1h slash weapon with the one and done property.
