# Loot Goblin

**Level 0 Small Goblin**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 13\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 20\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 18\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> -1, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +5, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +0

<mark style="color:green;">**Flee!**</mark> - A loot goblin's movement is \[elusive] while its hp is below 10.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 12)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Unarmed (C)**</mark>  +4 (+4 disarm)\
d8+3 (7)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - 1/day - [Break Free](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/warfare/level-1/break-free)

<mark style="color:red;">**Claimed**</mark> - A loot goblin can use disarm to take anything on a creatures person that is readily accessible, such as potions, weapons, magic items etc. When a loot goblin disarms a creature, it can grab hold of the item and immediately stow it away in its loot pouch.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Shinies**</mark> - A creature can wave around a valuable object to tantalize a loot goblin. A valuable enough object taunts the goblin, forcing it to steal the item (will negates). The object must have at least a 1k gold value. If it is worth at least 10k, the goblin gets no save.

A pouch of gold is satisfactory as well. Once presented, the item can be the target of the loot goblin's "claimed" ability.

## <mark style="color:yellow;background-color:yellow;">Other Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str -1, Dex +3, Lucc +3, Int +0, Wis +0, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Night Vision**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Speed**</mark> - 40

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Loot Scent**</mark> - A loot goblin has the ability to smell the value of anything in its possession or adjacent to it. Objects with more than 1k gold of value can be smelled from 30 ft, and more than 10k can be sniffed out from hundreds of feet away.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Loot Enthusiast**</mark> - When fleeing after scoring some loot, a loot goblin can carry up to 100lbs of loot, despite its small size. Once the adrenaline wears off after the coast is clear, they must drag anything heavier than 50 lbs.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Gear**</mark> - 1 durable and elastic sack

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Feats**</mark> - [Fleet Footwork](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/fleet-footwork)

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Skills**</mark> -  +3 perception, +6 acrobatics, +6 stealth, +6 sleight of hand
