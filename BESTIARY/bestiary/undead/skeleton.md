## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Disarmed**</mark> - When a skeleton is disarmed, one of its arms falls off instead of the usual effect. Its claws damage is reduced by two with only one arm. It cannot use claw attacks with no arms. A skeleton can pick up and reattach one of its arms as a standard action.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Glass Bones**</mark> - A skeleton can be sundered repeatedly to reduce its AC, even if it does not wear armor.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Weakness**</mark> - Crush 2
