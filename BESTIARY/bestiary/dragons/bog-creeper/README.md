# Bog Creeper

**Level 7 Large Dragon \[boss]**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 23\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 130\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 24\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +12 (stalwart), <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +8, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +7

<mark style="color:green;">**Immune**</mark> - Poison, Disease

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Necrotic

<mark style="color:green;">**Reformation**</mark> - Instead of being defeated, a bog creeper immediately dissipates into several bone beetles that all appear 30 ft away from where it's defeated. These beetles act on the bog creepers turn. If any remain at the end of the bog creepers next turn (including any it summoned previously), the bog creeper reforms at the point closest to all that remain with (beetles\*10) hp.

<mark style="color:green;">**Carrion Consumption \[healing] \[recharge]**</mark> - Bog creepers often keep decaying and diseased creatures inside of its lair for later consumption. As a swift action, a bog creeper can consume such a creature to gain 20 temp hp and recharge its breath weapon.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 19)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Bite (P)**</mark>  +10\
d8+4 (8)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Make 2 thorn attacks.

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - Blight, Bone Cage

<mark style="color:red;">**Boss Actions**</mark> -&#x20;

<mark style="color:orange;">Back and Better than Ever</mark> - After using reformation, use breath weapon.\
<mark style="color:orange;">Freedom! \[companion]</mark> - After taking damage, summon a bone beetle. All beetles are companions.

<mark style="color:red;">**Breath Weapon \[recharge]**</mark> - Unleash a wave of putrid and caustic liquid. 60 ft cone, 7d6 (24) poison damage and applying sickened 1 (reflex halves dmg and negates sickened). In its wake, it leaves behind hazardous waste that deals 2d6 (7) poison damage to any creatures that end their turn in the area.

Recharges after 1 minute or after using reformation/carrion consumption.

<mark style="color:red;">**Bad Breath \[persistent]**</mark> - For 1 round after using its breath weapon, or carrion consumption, a bog creeper emits a horrific stench. Creatures it bites during this time become sickened 2. Any creature within 10 ft of the bog creeper during this time is made sickened 1 (fort negates). Creatures with the scent ability have disadvantage on this save.
