# Dragonslayer Hound

**Level 5 Medium Beast**

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 19\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 60\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 29\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +6, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +10 (evasion), <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +8

<mark style="color:green;">**Evasion**</mark> - Take half damage from reflex saves. If the hound passes, it negates the effect entirely.

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Fire

2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark>  - Whenever a dragonslayer hound takes fire damage, it gains access this multiattack option until the end of its next turn.\
Make 3 bite attacks.

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Technique**</mark> - [Boiling Blood](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/pyromancy/level-2/boiling-blood), [Pack Attack](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-1/pack-attack), [Jaws of the Beast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-2/jaws-of-the-beast), [Down to Earth (U3)](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/arc/level-1/down-to-earth-su)\
1/day - [Blazing Beam](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/prismancy/level-3/blazing-beam), [Feast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/beastmode/level-3/feast)\
Constant - [Jump](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/wildcard/level-1/jump)

<mark style="color:red;">**Fire Fang \[fire]**</mark> - A creature bitten by a hound is set ablaze (reflex negates).&#x20;

<mark style="color:red;">**Dragonslayer**</mark> - A dragonslayer hound has very specialized combat training. It gets a +4 bonus to attack against dragons, including dragon adjacent creatures or creatures with a draconic affinity.

<mark style="color:red;">**Flash Fire (Su)**</mark> - A dragonslayer hound can redirect fire effects, similar to how the [Lightning Rod](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/clairvoyance/level-1/lightning-rod) technique functions as an immediate action if the effect originates within 60 ft of it. Spells such as [Fireblast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/pyromancy/level-3/fire-blast) are now centered on the hound while AoE spells are directed towards the hound instead of their intended direction.

Against dragon breath weapons specifically, a dragonslayer hound can use this ability as an instant action. When it does so, it turns the effect into a line attack directed towards itself, regardless of the original direction and shape of the ability.
