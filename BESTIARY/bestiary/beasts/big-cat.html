Big Cat

Level 4 Large Beast

Defense Traits

AC 18
HP 60
Poise 32
Fort +7, Refl +9, Will +5

Graceful - Big cats take 1 damage per 10 feet they fall and take no poise damage from falling.

Offense Traits (DC 16)

Bite (P) (can only be used against a grappled creature)
4d6+3 (17)

Claw (S) +7
2d6+3 (10)

Multiattack - Make 2 claw attacks, one of which must be a grapple

Innate Technique - Roar, Pack Attack
1/day - Jaws of the Beast

Special Attack: Bushwack - While hiding, a big cat can take the jump action and attack a creature, dealing an additional 2d8 (9) dmg. If big cat successfully hides, it can also grapple the creature as an instant action.

Rend - 2 claws, or a grapple and a claw (2d6)

Species - Big cats come in a variety of shapes and sizes, each with their own unique boons.

Cheetah - Gain powerful charge. When it charges or dashes in a straight line, it can move up to 160 feet.
Lion - Can innately use the Warcry technique 1/day.
Jaguar/Panther - While grappled by a jaguar, the target is also staggered.
Tiger - While grappled by a tiger, the creature is also knocked down.

Weaknesses/Deep Lore

Watery Grave - Big cats will avoid water if they can get away with it and prefer attacking targets not in water. If made wet, a big cat will always spend their swift action to remove the wet condition with a violent shake.

Other Traits

Ability Scores - Str +3, Dex +3, Lucc +0, Int -3, Wis +1, Cha +0

Speed - 40

Blindsense - Scent (10 ft)

Enhanced Leap - Big cats can leap up to 30 ft

Favored Terrain - In its natural habitat, a big cat gets a +4 bonus on stealth checks

Feats - SF Stealth, Maneuver Initiate, Swift Grappler

Skills - +7(+4) perception, +9 athletics, +9(+4) (adv) stealth
