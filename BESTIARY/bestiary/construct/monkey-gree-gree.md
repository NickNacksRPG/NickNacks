## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 15)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Unarmed (P)**</mark>  +5 (+4 grapple) (20/40)\
d10+3 (8) +d6 zap (overcharge)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Make 2 unarmed attacks.

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Mad Monkeys](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/mother-earth/level-3/mad-monkeys)

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: Rocket Punch**</mark> - Make a thrown weapon attack using unarmed damage. If successful, make a drag attack with no MA penalty. If the drag is successful, the creature is pulled adjacent.

<mark style="color:red;">**Aura: Stance Change**</mark> - The Monkey Gree Gree swaps between three different faces: Monkey covering eyes, monkey covering ears and monkey covering mouth. It starts covering its ears, and swaps at the start of each turn after the first in a repeating loop (cover ears, cover eyes, then mouth). Each stance has an aura that radiates 60 ft with a different effect.

<mark style="color:orange;">Cover Ears</mark> - All creatures within the aura are deafened, giving them -4 perception.\
<mark style="color:orange;">Cover Eyes</mark> - All creatures within the area are blinded.\ <mark style="color:orange;">Cover Mouth</mark> - All creatures within the aura are silenced.

The Gree Gree radiates this aura while hiding in plain sight. The Gree Gree is affected by its own aura.\


## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Yes Master**</mark> - When an automaton is programmed, it is done so while listening to a voice. By mimicking this voice with a DC 20 disguise check, you can give the automaton new orders. Can only be attempted once. With a DC 13 engineering check, an inactive automaton can be reprogrammed.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Hand Needed**</mark> - When an automaton uses its pin down technique, it loses access to the hand and its multi-attack on subsequent turns until the creature removes it and it magnetizes back.
