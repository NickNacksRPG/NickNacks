# Space Crab

**Level 0 Tiny Aberration**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 12\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 20\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 15\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> -1, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +3, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +0

<mark style="color:green;">**Resistance**</mark> - Necrotic

<mark style="color:green;">**Home Field Advantage**</mark> - While attached to a creatures head, the space crab gains DRA 10 and +4 AC.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 12)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Bite (P)**</mark>  +1\
d8+1 (5) (+d8 vs DD)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Finisher](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/martial-techniques/scoundrel/level-1/twist-the-knife)

<mark style="color:red;">**Sneak Attack**</mark> - Against a DD target, a space crab does an additional d8 damage.

<mark style="color:red;">**New Home**</mark> - Against a defeated creature, a space crab can attach to the creature's head and gain control over the actions of the soon to be corpse. After 1 hour, it kills the creature and reanimates it as a zombie as though casting [animate dead](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/necromancy/level-2/animate-dead), but the creature retains its full level. It can then spend all of its actions each turn to command this undead. Only through the space crab's or it's host's death will it detach. When the space crab is killed, the host automatically dies. The space crab must use this on a creature that is initially alive. It cannot use this ability on a creature that was already dead. Use the zombie stat block for generic humanoid host. Hosts can be anything; the space crab just has to get lucky.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**What's Poppin?**</mark> - A space crab can only make an attack roll in the same turn it jumps. It does not threaten.

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Enhanced Leap**</mark> - When the space crab jumps, it moves 30 feet, regardless of its speed, as long as it is not 0.
