# Froggalier

**Level 1 Small Cryptid/Beast**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 14\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 28\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 23\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +4, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +0, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +5

<mark style="color:green;">**Sticky Hold**</mark> - A froggalier cannot be separated from its snail mount. The creatures are considered 1 entity.

<mark style="color:green;">**Madness?**</mark> - A froggalier is blessed by Frogg Saron. It receives a bonus to will saves equal to its negative wisdom.

<mark style="color:green;">**Shelly**</mark> - A froggalier takes no penalty to AC when charging, and can charge through difficult terrain.

## <mark style="color:orange;background-color:red;">Offense Traits (DC 13)</mark>

<mark style="color:red;">**Ram (C)**</mark>  +4\
2d6+3 (10)

<mark style="color:red;">**Multiattack**</mark> - Ram and cast metronome.

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Metronome](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/cantrips/metronome), [Mage Hand](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/cantrips/mage-hand)\
1/day - [Power Overwhelming](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/frogg-saran/level-1/power-overwhelming), [Tentacles for Arms](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/frogg-saran/level-1/tentacles-for-arms), [Plague of Frogs](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/frogg-saran/level-1/plague-of-frogs)

<mark style="color:red;">**Special Attack: Charge Forth! \[elusive]**</mark> - Special: Can only be made as part of a charge attack. \
Get +2 attack. You can move past the creature you attack. You can also turn during this charge.

Allies that charge before your next turn get this benefit as well.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Nearsighted**</mark> - The symbiotic relationship between the frogg and the snail mount muddies the senses of the frogg. They can only see creatures within 30 feet of them. Any attacks made outside of this range treat the froggalier as DD.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Solvent**</mark> - With an application of super solvent (requiring a successful attack roll or a grapple) a froggalier can be forcefully separated from its sticky mount. This kills the froggalier.
