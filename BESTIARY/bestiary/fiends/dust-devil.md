<mark style="color:red;">**Choking Debris (Su) \[air] \[recharge]**</mark> - Targets a creature within a Dust Storm Aura. Exhale a large quantity of dust in their direction and force it down their throat. They take 2d6 (7) slashing damage and are sickened 1 immediately (fort negates). At the end of their next 3 turns they take this damage and are sickened 1 again, then make a new save to end the effect.
