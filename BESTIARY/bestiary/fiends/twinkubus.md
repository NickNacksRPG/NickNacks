<mark style="color:blue;">Touch Starvation</mark> - Take a -1 penalty to all d20 rolls and have disadvantage on saves vs a twinkubus' abilities.

<mark style="color:blue;">Cure</mark> - Have intercourse with a twinkubus, or deprive yourself of intimate contact for 1 week.
