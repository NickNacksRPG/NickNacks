d6+2 (5)

<mark style="color:red;">**Innate Techniques**</mark> - [Fireblast](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/pyromancy/level-3/fire-blast)

<mark style="color:red;">**Honorable Sudoku**</mark> - If a sudoku defeats itself with its fireblast technique, all saves made against it have disadvantage. It never gets a save against its own fireblast, but also can't be knocked down from it. Fireblast's DC is 2 higher when it includes itself in the blast.

<mark style="color:red;">**Return the Favor**</mark> - Creatures that make AoOs against a sudoku provoke AoOs from it.

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Rise from Ashes**</mark> - It is a sudokus eternal torment that they must die again and again. After a sudoku is defeated it turns into a pile of smoldering ash. The ash stays warm for 24 hours, then the sudoku rises again, immediately exploding in a fireblast. If it is inside of a container too small for it to fit, it bursts out.

Only by dousing the ashes in holy water can it be permanently slain.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Wet Blanket**</mark> - While wet, a sudoku's fireblast DC is decreased by 2. While mostly submerged in water, it cannot use fireblast or fire bending.

<mark style="color:blue;">**KYS NOW**</mark> - With a DC 20 persuasion check as a standard action, a creature can convince a sudoku to end it all immediately. Sudoku prefer to perform some kind of dramatic and explosive finish, but if no such option is available, they jump off of cliffs, snap their necks etc, immediately killing them.

<mark style="color:blue;">**Weakness**</mark> - 3 holy, 3 Frost

## <mark style="color:yellow;background-color:yellow;">Other Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Ability Scores - Str +0, Dex +2, Lucc +3, Int +0, Wis -1, Cha +0**</mark>

<mark style="color:yellow;">**Unicycle**</mark> - A sudoku's preferred method of transportation is a unicycle with a spiked wheel. It can travel at a speed of 50 while riding on it. If poise broken, it falls off of it in addition to any other effects and must spend a swift action to remount.
