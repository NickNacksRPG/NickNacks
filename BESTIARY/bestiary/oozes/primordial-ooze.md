# Primordial Ooze

**Level 3 Large Ooze \[boss]**

## <mark style="color:green;background-color:blue;">Defense Traits</mark>

<mark style="color:green;">**AC**</mark> 15\
<mark style="color:green;">**HP**</mark> 100\
<mark style="color:green;">**Poise**</mark> 26\
<mark style="color:green;">**Fort**</mark> +8, <mark style="color:green;">**Refl**</mark> +5, <mark style="color:green;">**Will**</mark> +3

<mark style="color:green;">**Color Change**</mark> - A primordial ooze is immune all damage. After being affected by a unique type of elemental damage (see _new life_), it instead becomes resistant to damage for 1 round. When it loses this damage immunity, it takes half damage from the ability used.

Once it takes damage from 7 unique elemental types, it loses this quality until it rests.

<mark style="color:green;">**Amorphous**</mark> - +12

<mark style="color:green;">**Immune**</mark> - Blinded, Blurred Vision

## <mark style="color:blue;background-color:purple;">Weaknesses/Deep Lore</mark>

<mark style="color:blue;">**Leftovers**</mark> - A DC 23 alchemy check allows safe storage of the potent oils required for the [Rebirth](https://app.gitbook.com/s/2kNIiIcUKxqLFlLgDKSI/magical-techniques/mother-earth/level-5/rebirth) spell.

<mark style="color:blue;">**New Life**</mark> - Primordial oozes are made of creatures that are yet to be. The first time a primordial ooze takes elemental damage of new type, the primordial ooze summons a (up to) level 3 creature of a specific type in an adjacent space, depending on damage type. The creature is never hostile towards the ooze.

Fire          - Fiend\
Necrotic  - Undead\
Psychic   - Aberration\
Force       - Cryptid\
Zap          - Construct\
Frost        - Elemental\
Poison     - Beast\
Holy         - Celestial\
Sonic       - Fey
