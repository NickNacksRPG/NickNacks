Boons

For every point of luck you possess, roll on this chart. Permanent increases to luck also include a bonus boon. You can roll multiple of the same result (for most results), but you can reroll if you aren't feeling like +15 hp is your thing.

# Name Effect
1 Mysterious Strange Recharge You can shout to the heavens for a savior, and a creature from the
Humanoids section of the bestiary of your LvL or lower comes to your aid. They help you escape danger, or otherwise help you solve a problem. Once you do this, you cannot do it again for once every 3 months.
2 Wide Your max +HP increases by 5.
3 Lottery You get an additional 3k starting gold. You also own Property. An luxurious home in an exotic paradise.
4 Thane Privelege If you are harassed by guards or similar types of authorities for any reason, you can announce some form of special title that you received from royalty, dissipating the situation and immediately gaining forgiveness. Once you do this, you cannot do it again in the same settlement.
You do not have to decide where this works before hand. Make up whatever works on the spot.
5 Natural Talent You gain 2 +Cantrips as innate techniques
6 Eager to Serve You have a follower that is a non-combatant. They are an apprentice, a big fan or are indebted to you. They will aid you in any way they can. (use commoner stats).
7 Lucky 7s You treat rolls of 7 as 17
8 Dicey Gain a luck dice, usable 1/day.
9 Suffused Your Mana pool increases by 1.
10 Copycat The first time each day an enemy casts a spell that affects you, you get a scroll in your pocket of that spell that disappears by the end of the day.
11 Second Chance Healing Whenever you die, don't. You instantly become Drained for half your max hp. Heal to your new max hp.
12 Pandora's Box (Recharge) You start play with an ornate box. Once per day when you open the box, it provides you with an item to aid you in your current struggle (decided by DM). Items are typically cheap.
