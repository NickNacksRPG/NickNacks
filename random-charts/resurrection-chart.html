Resurrection Chart

When a character is resurrected, roll a d4.

1 Bad
2-3 Neutral
4 Good

After that roll your respective chart. Characters with positive LUK can decide to Reroll their result. Characters with negative luck roll twice and have the GM decide what effect happens.
