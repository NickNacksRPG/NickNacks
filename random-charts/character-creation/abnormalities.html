Abnormalities

Another feature that can help you decide if your character is a weirdo or not. If you want a relatively average character, don’t roll this.

Roll a d20, then roll a d3. The d20 represents what feature is in question and the d3 represents whether the abnormality tends towards being positive (1) unusual (2) or negative (3). You can roll a few times to decide a few unusual features, decide you want a specific number of each feature, then roll a few d20s, or not roll at all.

Depending on the race, some of the features described as weird or negative may actually be positive or completely normal. Some features may be completely absent from your race. An easy way to create a new negative feature is to completely remove or damage certain features (one eye).

Feel free to go down the whole list if you're a mad lad (though the ladies might not appreciate some of the hair options)

2NosePositive: Your nose is small, and placed appropriately on the face
Strange: Instead of a nose you have nostrils, a snout or some other method of olfaction

Strange: Your teeth more closely resemble that of a predator, your tongue is exceptionally long
Negative: You have large buck teeth, you're a mouth breather or you permanently frown

Strange: You have an unnatural hair color, it grows obnoxiously long and fast, or in strange patters
Negative: You are balding, greasy, or your hair is very knotty and unwieldy
5VoicePositive: You have a golden voice, an exotic accent or an incredibly wide range
Strange: You're monotone, your voice echoes, or changes when extremely angry
Negative: You frequently have voice cracks, always sound like you're sick or have an impediment
6Body ShapePositive: You are toned/muscular or thin, with curves in all the right places
Strange: You have exaggerated body proportions, making you very top heavy or bottom heavy
Negative: You have a beer belly, are dangerously thin or have asymmetry in limb size
7EarsPositive: Your ears end in a point, or have some other appealing shape
Strange: Your ears fold over onto themselves, or droop slightly
Negative: Large ears, chipped ears
8Sex CharacteristicsPositive: Ideal or exaggerated proportions, bigger and better, pleasant shapes
Strange: You borrow characteristics from other races or produce large quantities of fluids
Negative: PP smol, flat as a board, low libido or low/no functionality
9SkinPositive: Smooth and without blemishes, with maybe some well placed freckles or dimples
Strange: You are an uncommon color, have patterns, or you have regions with scales/feathers
Negative: Vitiligo, extreme paleness, high visibility veins or frequent moles
10TailPositive: Ideal size for maintaining balance and appearance, a charming shape at the tip
Strange: Absurd length, have one when you typically shouldn't
Negative: Misplaced base of tail, weird angle, asymmetrical fur or scales, cut short or vestigial
11FacePositive: You have good symmetry, a strong jaw, ideal chin, round cheeks
Strange: You have coloration around your eyelids
Negative: You have asymmetry, double chin, massive forehead or a Neanderthal brow
12ScarsPositive: None, or only a handful that don’t obscure important regions of the body/face
Strange: Your scars form shapes and patterns, turning into an art form
Negative: Numerous large scars with unfortunate placements on the face or intimate regions
13NailsPositive: Your nails always seem to be the correct length, polished
Strange: Your nails more closely resemble claws or are retractable
Negative: Your nails are thick, discolored or unusually long
14HandsPositive: Thin lengthy fingers with few blemishes or wrinkles
Strange: Paws, additional opposable fingers, spiky knuckles
Negative: Knobby fingers, a missing or bonus digit, thick veiny hands
15HornsPositive: You have symmetrical horns placed at the top of the forehead
Strange: Your horns mimic other animals, like a moose or ram, you have multiple pairs or just one
Negative: Your horns are asymmetrical, cracked or broken
16Body HairPositive: Completely hairless or ideal placement
Strange: Your body hair makes you appear more like a specific type of animal
Negative: Patchy or thin, or a thick coat completely covering most of your body
17LimbsPositive: You have long legs, and limbs that transition well between body parts
Strange: Your limbs are thicker by the forearms than biceps, or calves rather than thighs
Negative: You have short stubby legs, or long arms that approach reach your knees
18Facial HairPositive: You can grow nice sideburns, beard, mustache or all the above
Strange: You can grow very long facial hair (Chinese master, wizard beard), even including eyebrows
Negative: Your facial hair is sporadic and insubstantial, you have a neckbeard
19OdorPositive: You always seem to smell pleasant, like flowers or fruit
Strange: Your natural aroma is something very specific, but not good or bad, like carrots or grass
Negative: You're extra greasy and sweaty or you frequently belch and flatulate
