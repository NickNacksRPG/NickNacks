T2 - Born Again Believer

Tags: Sp, Recharge
You can innately cast reincarnate 1/day. When you cast reincarnate, you gain access to a random innate technique that you can use once, or that is immediately Prepared for the remainder of the day.

While you have this technique, you lose access to reincarnation. After you expend the technique, you regain access to reincarnation. See chart below for determining what technique you gain.

Using this technique in a particularly creative and chaotic way can potentially grant you GBP. Up to GM interpretation what qualifies.

When you truly believe, you are rewarded. Roll on this chart to see what technique tree, then roll d6 until you get a technique level you have access to. Then roll another dice in a similar method to determine what technique in that tier (something like a d10 should usually suffice).

Ex. I roll 12 and get Cataclysm. Then I roll d6 and get 6, but I'm only level 5 and don't have access to tier 6 techniques. I roll d6 again and get a 1. I roll d8 and get a 1, giving me access to the 5G technique.
