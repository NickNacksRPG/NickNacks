# Shield Bash

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shield Bash \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 special attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
Special: This special attack can be made as an attack of opportunity. It doesn't consume the special attack on your turn.
{% endhint %}

Bash your opponent with a shield adding d8 (4) dmg and one of the following on-hit effects.

\>Inflict clumsy 2\
\>Halt a creature's movement\
\>Apply grounded for 1 turn

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
