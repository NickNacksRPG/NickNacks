# Elemental Barrier (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Elemental Barrier \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius aura, centered on user </td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You brace yourself against incoming elemental damage. Choose a damage type (other than true, slash, crush or pierce). Any ally within 10 feet of you gains resistance to that damage type.

Multiple creatures using elemental barrier can overlap to grant more resistances.
