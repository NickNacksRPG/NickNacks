While this technique is prepared, you can cause phantom shields to appear inbetween your allies and incoming attacks.&#x20;

You can use shield actions on any allies within 60 ft of you. You gain a new shield action.

<mark style="color:green;">Shield Heroism (immediate)</mark> - Reduce the damage of an incoming effect by your level.

For tower shield's _tank up_ action, you can choose to provide the cover from any position within 60 ft of you.
