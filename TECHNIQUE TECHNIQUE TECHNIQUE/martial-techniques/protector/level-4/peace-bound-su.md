# Peace Bound (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Peace Bound</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a creature to internally struggle when harming creatures (will negates). Whenever it deals damage, it deals half damage. Whenever it inflicts a harmful effect that doesn't deal damage, the DC is decreased by 4.

The caster also suffers this debuff while the spell persists.

The spell ends if the creature spends 1 round doing nothing.
