# It's Time to Duel!

## <mark style="color:orange;">It's Time to Duel</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You issue a challenge towards the target to engage in an honorable duel. You and the target are taunted by each other until they deal damage to you, you deal damage to them, or the spell ends.
