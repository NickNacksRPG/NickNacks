# Noble Sacrifice

## <mark style="color:orange;">Noble Sacrifice \[elusive]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 ally</td></tr></tbody></table>

When a creature lands an attack against an ally within 30 ft, you can immediately move adjacent to the ally, become the new target of the attack, and automatically get hit by the attack.
