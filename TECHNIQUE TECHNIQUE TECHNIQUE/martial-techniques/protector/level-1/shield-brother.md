# Shield Brother

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shield Brother</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 ally</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
If you have the shield focus feat, you can also use this technique as an instant action with shield focus.
{% endhint %}

You can move 10 ft closer to an ally. If that puts them within your natural reach, you can use a shield action on them.

You can also move 10 ft while using tank up to help protect as an immediate action.
