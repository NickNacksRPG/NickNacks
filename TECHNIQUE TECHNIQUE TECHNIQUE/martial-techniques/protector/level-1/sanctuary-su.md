# Sanctuary (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sanctuary</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 ally</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Affected creature cannot be targeted by harmful effects such as spells or attacks. The creature can still be affected by things that are AoE, they just can't be directly affected by hostile actions.

If the target takes aggressive actions, the effect ends.

A creature who has been affected by this technique cannot have used on them again for 1 round.
