# Boost!

## <mark style="color:orange;">Boost</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Instant Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td></td><td></td></tr></tbody></table>

An ally can walk into a space you occupy to use a shield you wield as a springboard. You give them a jump action with additional movement and height equal to your natural reach. If they are a larger size than you, then you need to possess exceptional strength or carry weight to do this.&#x20;
