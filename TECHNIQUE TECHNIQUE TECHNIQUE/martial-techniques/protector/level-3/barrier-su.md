# Barrier (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barrier \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Protector 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20x20 ft wall</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

Erect a transparent barrier that blocks movement and line of effect. These effects only apply to enemies and allies can freely attack and move through the barrier.

The barrier can be harmed, and has 5+(2\*lvl) hp. When it drops to 0 hp, it shatters.
