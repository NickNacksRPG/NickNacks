# Power Lift (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Power Lift \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

You infuse yourself with the power of LIFT, drastically increasing your carrying capacity.

You can lift objects weighing up to 10 tons. The following special attack can be used at will.

<mark style="color:green;">Heavy Artillery</mark> - You can throw an object weighing at least 1 ton to deal 3d12+Str (16+str) dmg, defaulting to crush dmg.
