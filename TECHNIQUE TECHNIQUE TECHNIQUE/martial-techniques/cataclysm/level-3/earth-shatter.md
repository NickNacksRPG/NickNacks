# Earth Shatter

## <mark style="color:orange;">Earth Shatter \[earth] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>60 ft line, 30 ft cone or 15 ft radius burst, centered on user</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
You must be touching the ground to cast this spell.
{% endhint %}

You smash the ground violently, causing it to crack and crumble under your strength. The area becomes difficult terrain. Creatures on the ground in the area take 4d8+lvl (18+lvl) crush damage and are knocked down (reflex halves damage and negates knock down).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
