# Ferocious Howl

## <mark style="color:orange;">Ferocious Howl \[sonic] \[fear] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>20/40 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

Shout at your enemy to create a sonic projectile and scare them off. Make a thrown attack that deals 3d8+lvl (13+lvl) sonic damage. The target is frightened for one round (will reduces to shaken 1).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Every point of additional mana spent on this ability increases the damage by d8 (4). If you spend at least 3 mana, its range increases to 40/80. If you spend at least 5, 60/120.
