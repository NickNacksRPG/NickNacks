# Thunder Stomp

## <mark style="color:orange;">Thunder Stomp \[earth]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
You and your target must be touching the ground to use this technique.
{% endhint %}

You smash the ground violently, causing a shockwave under the target. Roll a topple attack. If successful, the creature takes 2d6 (7) crush dmg in addition to any other effects.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d6 (3).
