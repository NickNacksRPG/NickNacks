# Roar

## <mark style="color:orange;">Roar \[fear] \[sonic] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
Special: If your natural reach is greater than the AoE of the spell, then use that instead.
{% endhint %}

You roar loudly, sounding more monster than man. Creatures within the ability's area take d6+lvl (3+lvl) sonic damage. They are frightened for one round (will negates).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d6 (3).
