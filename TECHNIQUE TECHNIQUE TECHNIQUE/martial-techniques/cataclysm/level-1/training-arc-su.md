# Training Arc (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Training Arc \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Charge your attack so it leaves behind a lingering effect, causing the target to experience the gravity of a larger planet. On hit the target is grounded.

As a swift action, you can end the grounded debuff and pull the creature 30 ft closer to you.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
