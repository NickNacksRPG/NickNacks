# Sonic Boom

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sonic Boom \[sonic] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cataclysm 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>15 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You clap your hands together with violent force. Creatures in the area take 2d8+lvl (9+lvl) sonic damage and are pushed back 10 ft (fort halves damage negates pushback).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
