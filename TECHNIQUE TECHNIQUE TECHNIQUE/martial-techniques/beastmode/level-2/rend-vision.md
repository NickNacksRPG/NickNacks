# Rend Vision

## <mark style="color:orange;">Rend Vision \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Grow a beak and poke at a creature's eyes. On hit the creature takes an additional d8 (4) dmg and is blinded for 1 round (reflex reduces to blurred vision).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
