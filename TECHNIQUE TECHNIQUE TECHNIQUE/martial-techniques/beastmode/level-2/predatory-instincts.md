# Predatory Instincts

## <mark style="color:orange;">Predatory Instincts \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 Minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You can instinctively tell when a creature is a lower level than you just by looking at them. You get a +2 bonus to melee damage rolls against said creatures. If the creature is a minion, you can tell, and this damage bonus increases to +4.
