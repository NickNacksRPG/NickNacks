# Feast

## <mark style="color:orange;">Feast \[healing] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature, living, defeated or dead</td></tr></tbody></table>

Grow rows of jagged teeth, unhinge your jaw and take a meaty bite out of a creature, dealing an additional 2d8 (9) dmg. If after the attack is resolved, the creature is defeated or dead, gain (2\*lvl) temp hp.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
