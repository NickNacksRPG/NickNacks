# Octolock

## <mark style="color:orange;">Octolock \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Grow several tentacles and make a grapple attack with a +4 bonus. While grappled in this way, the target has -4 AC, you have resistance to poise damage and you constrict them for 3d6 (10) damage each turn, including the turn you initiate it.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase attack bonus by 2.
