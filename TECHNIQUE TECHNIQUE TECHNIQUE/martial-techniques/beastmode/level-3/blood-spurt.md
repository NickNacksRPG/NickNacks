# Blood Spurt

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blood Spurt</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 3</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

While you are bleeding, or whenever a creature within your natural reach deals slashing or piercing damage, you can respond by squirting some of your blood at them. You take 2 true damage and the target is blinded for 1 round (reflex reduces to blurred vision).
