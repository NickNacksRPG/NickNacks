# Unseen Predator

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unseen Predator</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 special attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

While hiding, leap 30 ft, requiring a jump action. You appear suddenly next to the creature and make a melee attack against them that deals an additional 2d8 (9) dmg. If the target fails its perception against your stealth, instead deal 4d8 (18) additional dmg.
