# Lick

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lick \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>See text</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your tongue grows in size and strength to attack a creature. You can use it to make melee attacks with an additional 15 ft of reach for the remainder of your turn.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase the reach by an additional 15 ft.
