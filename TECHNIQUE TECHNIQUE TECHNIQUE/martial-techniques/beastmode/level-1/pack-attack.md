# Pack Attack

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pack Attack \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Keep up the pressure on an opponent by making it appear like many copies of you are attacking it at once. If the attack lands, an ally (other than yourself) has advantage on their next attack against the target in the next round.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
