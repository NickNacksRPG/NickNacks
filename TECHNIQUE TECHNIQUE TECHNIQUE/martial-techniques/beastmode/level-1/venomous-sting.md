# Venomous Sting

## <mark style="color:orange;">Venemous Sting \[poison] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

Grow a large stinger, or long fangs to sink into an enemy. On hit they are envenomed (fort negates).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) poison damage.
