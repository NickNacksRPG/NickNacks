# Frenzied Leap

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frenzied Leap \[upcast] \[earth]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, centered on landing point</td></tr></tbody></table>

Take the jump action and crash upon your foes with a shockwave. Creatures within 10 ft of your landing position (or your natural reach) are clumsy 1.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase your jump distance by 5.
