# Bestial Claw

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bestial Claw \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Beastmode 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>15 ft radius, centered on user or 20 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Dig your claws into the ground and unleash a cone of sharp claw shaped shockwaves dealing 2d8+lvl (9+lvl) slashing damage (reflex halves). Creatures take an equal amount of poise damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Every point of additional mana spent on this ability increases the damage by d8 (4).&#x20;
