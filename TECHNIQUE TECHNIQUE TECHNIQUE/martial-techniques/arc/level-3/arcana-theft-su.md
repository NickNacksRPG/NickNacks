# Arcana Theft (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcana Theft</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make an attack against a creature and steal their magic away from them. If they are currently affected by a buff spell, you take it away from them on hit. You remove the buff from them and grant it to yourself or an ally within 30 ft.
