# Blood Price (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blood Price \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

Inflict a curse that is particularly annoying to mages (will negates). Whenever the cursed creature casts a spell, it starts bleeding. The creature immediately takes the bleed damage after the spell is cast. The bleed damage is equal to (the spells level+2). The bleed effect happens after the effects of the spell (so using a healing spell has no effect on the subsequent bleed).

By not casting a spell for a full day, the curse ends.
