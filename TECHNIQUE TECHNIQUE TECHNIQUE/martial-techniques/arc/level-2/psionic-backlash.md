# Psionic Backlash

## <mark style="color:orange;">Psionic Backlash \[psychic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 spell caster</td></tr></tbody></table>

When you hear magic words or see magic at play, you can respond by making the creature's mana manipulation unstable. They take d6 (3) psychic damage for each point of mana they spend (or would spend with innate casting).
