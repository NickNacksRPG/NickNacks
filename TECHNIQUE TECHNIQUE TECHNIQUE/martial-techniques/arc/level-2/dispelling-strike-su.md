# Dispelling Strike (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dispelling Strike \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature or magical effect</td></tr></tbody></table>

Roll an attack roll in place of a dispel check against a magical effect you can attack. If it is attached to a creature, the attack only effects the spell, not the creature.&#x20;

If you successfully dispel the effect, the caster takes 2d8+lvl (9+lvl) force damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
