# Mana Burn (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mana Burn \[curse] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You weaken the creatures connection to the source of their magic power. They mana burn 2 (will negates) decreasing their current and max mana by the burn value. Mana burn curses can stack.

By not casting a spell for a full day, the curse ends.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Increase mana burn by 1 per mana spent.
