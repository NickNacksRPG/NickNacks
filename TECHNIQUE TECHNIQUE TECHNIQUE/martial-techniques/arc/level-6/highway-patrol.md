# Highway Patrol

## <mark style="color:orange;">Highway Patrol \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 6</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft (see text)</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will (see text)</td></tr></tbody></table>

You are a master at negating teleportation, but why not play along a little? Every time a creature within 60 ft of you teleports, you can redirect them to another position within 60 ft of you as an instant action and cause them to become clumsy 2 (will negates redirection).
