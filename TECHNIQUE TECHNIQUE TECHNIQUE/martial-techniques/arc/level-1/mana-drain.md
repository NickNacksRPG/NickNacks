# Mana Drain

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mana Drain \[buff] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You radiate a 10 ft aura that drains the mana from living things. Every time a creature spends mana within this aura, it spends an additional mana. If the ability costs no mana, such as an innate spell, its CL is reduced by 2.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast 4</mark> - Double the penalties of the aura (+2 mana expenditure, -4 CL for innate spells).
