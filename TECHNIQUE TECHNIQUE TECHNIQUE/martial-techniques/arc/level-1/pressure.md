# Pressure

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pressure \[elusive]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arc 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Instant action

Move 10 ft in response to an enemy casting a spell. You must end this movement in a space adjacent to said enemy.

Because this action happens _before_ the casting completes, you can benefit from effects like [Mana Drain](mana-drain.md) or [Fighter's Lament](https://app.gitbook.com/s/vxnMGGHnEtmcEQDFxcK6/combat-feats/fighters-lament).
