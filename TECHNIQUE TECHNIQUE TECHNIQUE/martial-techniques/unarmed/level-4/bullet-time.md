# Bullet Time

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bullet Time \[Elusive]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scoundrel 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Instant Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Special: Cannot be used while fatigued.\
You react instantaneously to fast moving projectiles, giving you a +4 bonus to perception and a +4 bonus to AC against ranged attacks for 1 round. Each time a ranged attack is made against you during this time, you can move 5 feet.\
After bullet time ends you are fatigued.
