# Clothesline

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clothesline</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 AoO

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

When a creature provokes an AoO from movement, you can make a simple unarmed strike against them. On hit, their speed is reduced to zero.
