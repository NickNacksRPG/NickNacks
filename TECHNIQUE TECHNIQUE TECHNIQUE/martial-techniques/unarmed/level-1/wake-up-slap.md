# Wake-Up Slap

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wake-Up Slap \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Help an ally snap out of it with the back of your hand. Deal 1 true damage and immediately wake them up from sleep or free them from fascination. If they are confused, add 2 to their rolls.

If they are charmed or frightened, they get a new save against the effect.
