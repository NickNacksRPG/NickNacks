# Sucker Punch

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sucker Punch \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 special attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

This functions as though you automatically succeeded on the "sucker punch" feature of [deception](https://nick-nacks-rpg.gitbook.io/nicknacksrpg.com/skills/skills/deception).

Make an unarmed strike against an unassuming creature. They are DD for this attack.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
