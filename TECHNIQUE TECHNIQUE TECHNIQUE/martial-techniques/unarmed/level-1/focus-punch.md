# Focus Punch

## <mark style="color:orange;">Focus Punch</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Free action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Focus your energy into a mighty punch. If you do not take damage or get poise broken by the start of your next turn, you can make an unarmed strike with +10 attack that deals an additional 2d8 (9) dmg at any point during your turn as a free action.
