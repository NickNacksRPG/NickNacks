# Toe Smasher

## <mark style="color:orange;">Toe Smasher</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

On-hit smush a creature's toes to limit their movement and deal an additional d8 (4) dmg. They cannot move farther away from you for 1 round. If you are poise broken or forcefully moved out of reach, this effect ends.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
