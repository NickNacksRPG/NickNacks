# Body Slam

## <mark style="color:orange;">Body Slam \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
This attack must be performed from above the target or combined with a jump action.
{% endhint %}

Crash onto your foe. This attack inflicts knock down on you and your target and deals an additional d8 (4) dmg. If the target is already knocked down, it is instead staggered and takes an additional 3d8 (13) dmg.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
