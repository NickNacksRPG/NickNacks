# Crush Joints

## <mark style="color:orange;">Crush Joints \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Bend your foe in ways that cause permanent damage and strike at vulnerable joints. The creature is made clumsy 2 and cannot remove clumsy stacks until it receives first aid (class DC).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage of the follow-up attack by d8 (4).
