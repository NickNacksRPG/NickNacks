# Five-Finger Death Punch

## <mark style="color:orange;">Five-Finger Death Punch \[concentration] \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unarmed 6</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Instant Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>24 hours</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Creature feels their heart stop and gain a stacking debuff (fort changes to stagger 1 round). For every 5 feet they move on their own, they gain a stack of doom, dying when they reach 5.

Wears off after 24 hours.
