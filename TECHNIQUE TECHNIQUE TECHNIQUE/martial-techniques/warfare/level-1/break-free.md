# Break Free

## <mark style="color:orange;">Break Free</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Warfare 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr></tbody></table>

Gain +4 bonus to your next attack against a creature grappling you or an ally this round. This bonus increases to +8 if you shove the enemy.
