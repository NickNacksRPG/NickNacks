# Boasting Taunt

## <mark style="color:orange;">Boasting Taunt</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Warfare 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

Goad a creature within 30 ft of you into attacking you. They are taunted for three rounds (will negates). At the end of each turn, they are allowed to make another save to shake off the taunt.
