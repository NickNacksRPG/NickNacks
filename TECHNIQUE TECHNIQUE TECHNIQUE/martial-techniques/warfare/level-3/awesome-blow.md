# Awesome Blow

## <mark style="color:orange;">Awesome Blow \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Warfare 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Roll a shove attack. If successful, deal damage with a bonus 2d8 (9), shove and knock down.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase the bonus to the shove attack by 2 and add an additional d8 (4) dmg.
