# Warcry

## <mark style="color:orange;">War Cry \[fear]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Warfare 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on user</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>All enemies</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will (see text)</td></tr></tbody></table>

Lift your weapon into the air and let out a battle cry. Enemies of a lower level than you are frightened for 1 round. Enemies of your level or higher are frightened for 1 round (will negates).

After a creature is the target of this technique, it cannot be affected by it again for 1 minute.
