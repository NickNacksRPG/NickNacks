# No Retreat

## <mark style="color:orange;">No Retreat \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Warfare 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
Special: Can only be used while being threatened.
{% endhint %}

You stand and fight, no matter what. You gain +4 attack. However you must choose a creature to be taunted by, choosing a new creature if the one you're taunted by is defeated as long as the buff persists.
