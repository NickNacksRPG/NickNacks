# Arrow Splitter

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arrow Splitter</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 1</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>One Creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Using combination of muscle memory and using your last shot as a reference, make a ranged attack, matching the result of the last ranged attack you made (either a miss, graze, hit or crit) with no roll required.

You must also target the same creature with it.
