# Pin Down

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pin Down \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Hit your opponent with a projectile in such a way to pin them to the ground or a wall. They are entangled on hit until they spend a swift action to remove it.

<mark style="color:orange;">High Ground</mark> - Effect happens on graze.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) dmg.
