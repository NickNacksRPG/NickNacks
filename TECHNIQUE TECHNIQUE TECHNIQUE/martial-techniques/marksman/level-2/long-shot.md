# Long Shot

## <mark style="color:orange;">Long Shot \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>One Creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack, adding 20 range and d8 (4) dmg. If you graze the target, you instead miss completely.

<mark style="color:orange;">High Ground</mark> - Damage bonus dice increases to d10.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) dmg and add an additional 10 range.
