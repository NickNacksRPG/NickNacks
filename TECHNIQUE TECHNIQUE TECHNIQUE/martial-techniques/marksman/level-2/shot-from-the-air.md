# Shot From the Air

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shot From the Air \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 projectile</td></tr></tbody></table>

Prepare yourself for incoming fire.

When a projectile fired from a weapon enters the first range increment of a ranged weapon you currently wield, fire at it. If your attack roll meets or exceeds theirs, the attack is negated.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast 4</mark> - Usable as an immediate action.
