# Lights Out

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lights Out \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>One Creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack, dealing an additional d10 (5) dmg. If this attack defeats the target, regain the mana cost of this ability and reload your weapon.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d10 dmg.
