# Rain of Arrows (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Rain of Arrows</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Marksman 3</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>5 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr></tbody></table>

Fire a single powerful shot directly up into the air, when it falls it splits into many projectiles. Make an attack roll and compare it to all targets in the area, dealing an additional 2d8 (9) dmg.

<mark style="color:orange;">High Ground</mark> - The radius increases to 10 ft.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) dmg.
