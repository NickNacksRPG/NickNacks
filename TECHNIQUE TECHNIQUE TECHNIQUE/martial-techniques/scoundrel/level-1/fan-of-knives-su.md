# Fan of Knives (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Fan of Knives</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scoundrel 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

While under the effects of this buff, you always have 2 non magical light weapons on your person with the thrown property. They are in your voluminous hair, fat folds or keistered in less comfortable places...You can draw these weapons as a free action any number of times each turn if your hands are empty.

Gain access to the following special attack, usable any number of times per combat.

<mark style="color:green;">Fan of Knives</mark> - Throw up to 3 knives at up to 3 creatures within 10 feet of each other. Ignore attack penalties from attacking creatures in the second range increment.

You also always have thieves tools in your back pocket, in the form of a single lockpick.
