# Shadow Step \[Su]

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shadow Step \[teleportation]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scoundrel 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Free Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Current Space</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
Special: This ability must be used in space of dim light or darkness.
{% endhint %}

Place a symbol on the ground. You can teleport back to this spot as an immediate action at any point until the start of your next turn.

If no creatures have line of sight of you when you teleport back, immediately hide.
