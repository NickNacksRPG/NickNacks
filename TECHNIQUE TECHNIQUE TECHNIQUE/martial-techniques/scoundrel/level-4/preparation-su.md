# Preparation (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Preparation \[teleportation] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scoundrel 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Enter a defensive stance, giving you +4 AC, +4 perception for 1 round and resetting your poise. When an attack made by a creature you can see misses you or fails to break your poise, you can choose to lose these bonuses to teleport within 5 feet of them and strike them with a melee attack.&#x20;

They are DD for this attack and take an additional 3d8 (13) dmg.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Each additional mana spent increases the bonuses to AC and perception by 2.
