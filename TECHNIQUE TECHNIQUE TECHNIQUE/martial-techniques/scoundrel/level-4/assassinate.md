# Assassinate

## <mark style="color:orange;">Assassinate</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scoundrel 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make an attack roll against a DD creature. The effect varies depending on how much hp the target has remaining.\
Not Full HP - Staggers them for 1 round (fort negates) and deals an additional 3d8 (13) damage.\
Full HP - Stun them for 1 round and deal an additional 4d8 (18) damage.
