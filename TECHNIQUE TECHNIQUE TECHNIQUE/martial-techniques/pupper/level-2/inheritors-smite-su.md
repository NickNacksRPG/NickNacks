# Inheritor's Smite (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Inheritor's Smite \[holy] \[light] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your attack does an additional d10 (5) holy damage. If the creature has weakness to holy damage, it also takes the attacks damage as poise damage. If this poise breaks them, they are knocked down.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d10 (5).
