# Bladed Dash (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bladed Dash \[teleportation] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft line</td></tr></tbody></table>

Rapidly teleport forward in small increments, briefly leaving behind colorful afterimages. Move in one direction 30 ft. You can attack a single creature that is within your reach at any point along this line. Deal d8 (4) bonus damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
