# Crash

## <mark style="color:orange;">Crash</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 charging creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

When a creature moves into melee range of an ally within 30 ft of you, you can immediately make a charge attack to intercept them. You make a combat maneuver attack against them. If you poise break them, instead of the usual effect, reduce their speed is zero until the start of their next turn just outside of the ally's reach.

Your speed is 0 on your next turn.
