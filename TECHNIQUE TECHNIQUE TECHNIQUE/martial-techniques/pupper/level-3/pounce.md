# Pounce

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pounce \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 FRA

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Charge attack a creature and make an additional melee simple attack against them. You can jump once as a free action during this charge.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - If you spend 5 mana you can make 2 additional melee simple attacks against them.
