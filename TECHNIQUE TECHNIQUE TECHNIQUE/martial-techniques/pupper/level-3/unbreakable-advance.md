# Unbreakable Advance

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unbreakable Advance</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You are protected as long as you are on the move.&#x20;

* You take no penalty to AC from charge attacks.&#x20;
* You ignore difficult terrain.
* During your turn you are immune to damage and poise damage, unless it is from a damage over time effect, like bleed.

This effect ends if you do not move at least 10 ft on your turn.
