# Courage

## <mark style="color:orange;">Courage \[buff] \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

You hold yourself with such confidence that your mind has no choice but to follow suite. You get advantage on saving throws against \[fear] effects.

When you pass a save against such an effect, you and allies that can see you gain 5 temp hp.
