# Extreme Speed

## <mark style="color:orange;">Extreme Speed</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Instant Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr></tbody></table>

Take the initiative by force. At the end of another creature's turn, take your turn, even if you were previously surprised. This becomes your new spot in initiative.

You cannot cast the spell if you've already taken your turn this round.
