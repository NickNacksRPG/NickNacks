# Flash Forward (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Flash Forward \[teleportation] \[outplay]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 FRA

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a charge attack with a twist: You teleport back to your starting position at the end of your turn. The first attack you land during this charge deals an additional 3d8 (13) dmg.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
