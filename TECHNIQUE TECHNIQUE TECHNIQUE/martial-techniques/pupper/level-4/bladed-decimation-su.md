# Bladed Decimation (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bladed Decimation \[teleportation] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pupper 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft line</td></tr></tbody></table>

Rapidly teleport forward in small increments, briefly leaving behind after images. Move in one direction 30 ft. You can attack ANY creature that is within your reach at any point along this line. Deal an extra 3d8 (13) dmg on all your attacks.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
