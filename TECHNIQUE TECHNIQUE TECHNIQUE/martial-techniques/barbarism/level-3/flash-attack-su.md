# Flash Attack (Su)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Flash Attack \[teleportation] \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>A threatened space</td></tr></tbody></table>

You can teleport 30 ft, as long as you appear in a threatened space. Any creatures you threaten take 2d8 (9) force damage and an equal amount of poise damage.

After you activate this ability, your turn immediately ends.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
