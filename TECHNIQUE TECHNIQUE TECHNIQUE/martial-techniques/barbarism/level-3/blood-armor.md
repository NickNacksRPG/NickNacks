# Blood Armor

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blood Armor \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
Special: Can only be used while below 50% hp or bleeding.
{% endhint %}

Bathe in your own blood. While below 50% hp or if you took bleed damage this turn, gain +2 AC. If you stop bleeding, or heal above 50% hp, the buff remains, but you lose the AC bonus until you meet the techniques requirements.

If you are below 10 hp, gain +4 AC.
