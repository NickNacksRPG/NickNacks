# Man Drop

## <mark style="color:orange;">Man Drop \[recharge] \[earth]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 FRA

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>100 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Leap with incredible strength 300 ft into the air and crash down onto an area. Creatures within 30 ft of your landing position take 6d8+lvl (27+lvl) crush damage and are staggered for 1 minute (fort halves damage and reduces stagger to 1 round). Creatures get a new save against the stagger effect at the end of each of their turns.

Usable 1/combat.
