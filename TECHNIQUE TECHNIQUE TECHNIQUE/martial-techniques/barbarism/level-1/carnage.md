# Carnage

## <mark style="color:orange;">Carnage \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

The more blood that flows from your battle, the more flows through your veins, pushing you forward with reckless abandon. When you defeat an enemy, you take a -1 penalty to ac and get a +1 bonus to attack. This can stack up to 5 times.

{% hint style="danger" %}
You cannot receive another buff effect while under the effects of carnage.
{% endhint %}
