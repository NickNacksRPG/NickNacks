# Fore!

## <mark style="color:orange;">Fore! \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
This special attack can only be initiated against a knocked down target.
{% endhint %}

Make a melee attack against a creature that deals an additional d8 (4) dmg. On hit launch it 20 ft in any direction with a slight parabola to knock them over obstacles. The creature remains knocked down in it's new position.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
