# Put Me In!

## <mark style="color:orange;">Put Me In! \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Barbarism 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Special Attack

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 willing or helpless creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Deal 20 poise damage to an ally and throw them into combat. You can throw them up to 20 ft, including into the air.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana you spend, you can throw your ally an additional 10 ft.
