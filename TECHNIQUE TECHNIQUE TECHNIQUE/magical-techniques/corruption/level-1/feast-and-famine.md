# Feast and Famine

## <mark style="color:orange;">Feast and Famine \[healing] \[overheal] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corruption 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>Inanimate plants</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="danger" %}
This spell only functions if you are not in a form of desolate terrain. This could include places like, arctic terrain, dessert the surface of deep ocean or many urban areas.

If it's iffy, you can always roll luck! (with a DC dependent on terrain)
{% endhint %}

You wave your staff in a circle to absorb the life from any plants in the area. You heal yourself for 6+CL hp and automatically end all bleed effects. This kills the plants.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, heal 4 more hp.
