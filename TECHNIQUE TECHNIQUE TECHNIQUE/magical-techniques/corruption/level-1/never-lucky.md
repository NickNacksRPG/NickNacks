# Never Lucky

## <mark style="color:orange;">Never Lucky</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corruption 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Cause a creature to be unlucky. They next time they roll an attack or saving throw, they roll it at disadvantage.

Outside of combat, this has the \[subtle] tag, but only affects skill checks, ability checks and luck checks.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - By spending 3 mana, the creatures next 2 rolls are at disadvantage.\
By spending 5, the creatures next 3 rolls are at disadvantage.
