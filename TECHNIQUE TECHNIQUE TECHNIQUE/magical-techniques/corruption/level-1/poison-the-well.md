# Poison the Well

## <mark style="color:orange;">Poison the Well \[curse] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corruption 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Minute

## <mark style="color:orange;">Focus</mark> - One gold piece

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 water source</td></tr></tbody></table>

You stand at the edge of a well and fill a coin with malefic energy. By dropping the coin into the well, it subtly causes it to become poisonous. Creatures that drink from it become drained 1 (fort negates).

This curse can be removed by removing the coin from the well.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - You can affect larger water sources by spending more mana.\
At 2 mana you can effect larger bodies of water, such as lakes and creeks.\
At 3 mana you can effect large lakes and 1 mile of river.\
At 6 mana you can effect an entire river, regardless of its length.
