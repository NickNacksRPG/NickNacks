# Curse of Blindness

## <mark style="color:orange;">Curse of Blindness \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

The target's vision wanes permanently as the world disappears around it. It is blinded (fort reduces effect to blurred vision for one round).

Removed by consuming the raw eyes of a freshly killed creature.
