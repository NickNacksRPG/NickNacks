# Corrupting Mist

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corrupting Mist \[poison] \[persistent] \[concentration] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corruption 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a purple or green mist to spread over and area. Creatures inside cough and wheeze, becoming sickened 1 (fort negates). If the creature is already sickened, then they instead take 3d8+CL (13+CL) poison damage (fort halves).

You are immune to your own cast of corrupting mist.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast 5</mark> - You can increase the duration of the spell to 1 hour. Every round it persists, it spreads an additional 10 ft. It only spreads in one direction 180 degrees.
