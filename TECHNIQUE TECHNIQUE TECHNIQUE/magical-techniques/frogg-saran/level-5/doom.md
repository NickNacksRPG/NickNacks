# DOOM

## <mark style="color:orange;">DOOM \[necrotic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>60 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fortitude</td></tr></tbody></table>

Drain the life from the world around you in a massive burst that has a rebound effect proportional to the number of creatures you hit with it.

Deal 7d8+CL (31+CL) necrotic damage to creatures other than you in the spells radius. For every creature you hit, you take d6 (3) true damage and gain 1 mana reserve.
