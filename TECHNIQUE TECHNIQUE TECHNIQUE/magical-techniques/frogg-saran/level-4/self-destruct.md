# Self-Destruct

## <mark style="color:orange;">Self Destruct \[force] \[recharge] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

You explode violently, setting yourself to 0 hp. All creatures within 20 ft of you take 30+CL force damage and are knocked back 20 feet (reflex halves).

Caster gains a T2 affliction.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by 7.
