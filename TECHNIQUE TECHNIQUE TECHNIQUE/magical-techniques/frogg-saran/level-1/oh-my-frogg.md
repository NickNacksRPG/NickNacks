# Oh my Frogg

## <mark style="color:orange;">Oh my Frogg \[contingent]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Minute

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

Before you would normally be affected by a hostile (non cantrip) spell, you change the nature of the magic into something completely different (will negates). The spell changes to another spell of the same level with you as the primary target or origin point of the spell, regardless of its typical range/targeting. Make saves as appropriate.

Some spells may have other restrictions like material components. Ignore them.

Upcast spells count as spells of a higher level.
