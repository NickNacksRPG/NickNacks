# Lucky Star

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lucky Star \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You point at your foe and a star-shaped projectile unerringly falls upon them, dealing d20 force damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, the star bounces, affecting an additional creature within 10 ft of the previous.
