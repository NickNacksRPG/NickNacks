# Power Overwhelming

## <mark style="color:orange;">Power Overwhelming \[Buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Cause the creature to surge with unbelievable power as their body rapidly deteriorates. They get a +4 bonus to all d20 rolls, damage rolls and CL. After the buff ends, they take 50% of their max hp in true damage.&#x20;
