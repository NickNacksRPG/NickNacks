# Time Shudder

## <mark style="color:orange;">Time Shudder \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You emit waves of entropy, hindering or helping ALL creatures nearby (including you). Immediately upon casting and at the start of your turn unleash a pulse to determine the effects of the spell for one round. There is a 50% chance to Hinder and there is a 50% chance to help.

<mark style="color:green;">Hinder</mark> - Creatures are staggered

<mark style="color:green;">Help</mark> - Creatures gain an additional 30 speed and their movement is \[elusive]
