# Spontaneous Combustion

## <mark style="color:orange;">Spontaneous Combustion \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr></tbody></table>

Randomly set a creature ablaze. All enemies within 30 and yourself are suitable targets. You are always lit ablaze on a 1.

{% hint style="info" %}
Consider using a dice larger than the number of creatures for uneven numbers and then re rolling unsuitable results.
{% endhint %}
