# Taste the Rainbow

## <mark style="color:orange;">Taste the Rainbow \[force] \[varies] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frogg Saran 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You launch a beam of energy at a creature that rapidly changes between the seven colors of the rainbow. Make an attack against a creature that deals 4d8+CL (18+CL) force damage, then roll to see which effect happens on a successful hit.

<table data-header-hidden><thead><tr><th width="214"></th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>1</td><td>Confused one round [Mind affecting]</td></tr><tr><td>2</td><td>Frightened one round [fear]</td></tr><tr><td>3</td><td>Drained 1</td></tr><tr><td>4</td><td>Blinded one round</td></tr><tr><td>5</td><td>Deal 15 poise damage</td></tr><tr><td>6</td><td>Charmed one round  [charm]</td></tr><tr><td>7</td><td>Bounce to nearest enemy using the same attack roll</td></tr><tr><td>8</td><td>Roll twice ignoring further 8s</td></tr></tbody></table>

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
