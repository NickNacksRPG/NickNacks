# Plasma Blade

## <mark style="color:orange;">Plasma Blade \[buff] \[fire] \[light] \[martial]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Held melee slash weapon</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Imbue a melee slashing weapon in hand with searing light, changing its base damage to d8+CL (4+CL) Fire damage. It becomes weightless. It gives off bright light within 20 feet.

As part of casting this spell, and as a swift action every turn for its duration, you may make a simple attack with this weapon. If it leaves your possession, this spell ends after one round.
