# Thunderlord's Decree

## <mark style="color:orange;">Thunderlord's Decree \[zap] \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 6</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>All Enemies</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your shout echoes like thunder across the battlefield as you call down lightning bolts to strike all enemies you can see within range. Creatures takes 6d8+CL (27+CL) zap damage and are staggered for 1 minute (fort halves and negates stagger). Creatures get a new save to end the stagger at the end of their turn.
