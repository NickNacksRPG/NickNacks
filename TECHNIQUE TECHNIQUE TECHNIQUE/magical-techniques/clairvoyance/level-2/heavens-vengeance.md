# Heavens' Vengeance

## <mark style="color:orange;">Heavens' Vengeance \[buff] \[zap] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

A dark cloud spawns above your head that crackles with electricity. It is angered whenever you take damage, and deals 3 zap damage to the creature that inflicted the damage if it is within 30 ft.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by 1.
