# Achoo!

## <mark style="color:orange;">Achoo \[air] \[elusive] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your nose twitches before you unleash a mighty sneeze. You knock yourself and a creature back 10 feet in opposite directions. They are staggered for one round and take 2d8+CL (9+CL) crush damage, or two rounds with 3d8+CL (13+CL) crush damage if they impact a solid surface (fort halves dmg and negates stagger).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
