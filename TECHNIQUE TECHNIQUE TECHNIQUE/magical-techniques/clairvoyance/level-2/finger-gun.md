# Finger Gun

## <mark style="color:orange;">Finger Gun \[air] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You point your finger at someone and then snap your fingers with your thumb pointing towards the sky. You unleash a powerful concentrated stream of air at a creature, requiring a ranged attack. The attack deals 3d8+CL (13+CL) crush damage. The creature is staggered for 1 round (fort negates).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
