# Lightning Bolt

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lightning Bolt \[upcast] \[zap]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>See Text</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Lightning erupts from your fingertips and bounces around the battlefield from enemy to enemy. You initially target a single creature, then the lightning bounces from that target to another target of your choosing (that you can see) within 30 ft. You affect a total of three creatures. Each creature affected takes 4d8+CL (18+CL) zap damage and is staggered for one round (fort halves damage and negates stagger).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent the lightning bolt bounces to an additional target and deals and additional d8 (4) damage.
