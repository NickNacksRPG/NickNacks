# Temperamental Tempest

## <mark style="color:orange;">Temperamental Tempest \[air] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft line</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You create a tornado about the height of a man that rapidly moves around the battlefield tossing around foes. Immediately after casting and for an additional 2 rounds at the start of your turns, it barrels in a line of your choosing 30 feet. Enemies within its path are tossed in a random direction 10 ft, take 2d6+CL (7+CL) crush damage and are knocked down (fort negates dmg, knockback and knocked down).
