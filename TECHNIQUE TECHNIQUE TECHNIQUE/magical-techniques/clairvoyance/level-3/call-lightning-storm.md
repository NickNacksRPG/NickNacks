# Call Lightning Storm

## <mark style="color:orange;">Call Lightning Storm \[upcast] \[concentration] \[zap]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

As part of casting the spell, and as a standard action every turn after, you call down a lightning bolt to strike a specific creature. The creature takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) zap damage (fort halves).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, target an additional creature with each lightning strike.
