# Storm Step

## <mark style="color:orange;">Storm Step \[elusive] \[air]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Clairvoyance 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

The direction of the wind changes to briefly haste you. As part of casting the spell, dash.&#x20;

Creatures within reach of you at the end of your turn take 3d8+CL (13+CL) crush dmg (fort halves).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
