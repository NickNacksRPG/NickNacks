# Foxdie

## <mark style="color:orange;">Foxdie</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Nack 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You summon a relatively average looking fox at a point you can see. At your command, you can cause it to make noises typical fox. After which, it explodes violently in a tiny fiery mushroom cloud.

This does not cause damage to anybody or anything, nor does it dirty anything.

If you do not cause it to make noise, it disappears within 1 minute.
