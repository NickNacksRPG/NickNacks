# Pants

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pants</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Nack 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You undo belts, buttons and zippers on a creature's pants as they are forcefully removed and folded into a pile next to them.

This spell does not work when used against heavily armored targets.
