# Crystalline Explosion

## <mark style="color:orange;">Crystalline Explosion \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane 2</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, centered on target creature</td></tr><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack by firing a magical crystal at a creature, dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) force dmg. Regardless if it hits or misses, it explodes in the targets space, knocking down all creatures within 10 ft (fort negates).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
