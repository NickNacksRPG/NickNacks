# Great Gavel

## <mark style="color:orange;">Great Gavel \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane 3</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius around target creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Conjure an oversized gavel from the tip of your staff and slam it down onto a creature within reach. They take 4d10+CL crush damage and are knocked down on hit. Creatures (other than you) within a 10 ft radius of the target take this damage and are knocked down (reflex halves dmg and negates knockdown).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
