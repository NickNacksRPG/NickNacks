# Scholar's Shield

## <mark style="color:orange;">Scholars Shield \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Free Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>5 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

A shield made of vibrant magical force rotates around your body. You can take shield actions, but only against spell attacks. Gets a few additional options

* Can also be used to block force effects that normally automatically hit (such as lucky star and magic missile).
* Can also be used to grant a +2 bonus to a saving throw against a spell as an immediate action
