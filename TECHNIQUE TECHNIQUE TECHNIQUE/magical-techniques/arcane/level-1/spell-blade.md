# Spell Blade

## <mark style="color:orange;">Spell Blade \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

## <mark style="color:orange;">Focus</mark> - 1 melee weapon

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Coat a melee weapon into pure magic force and make a melee attack, dealing d6+CL (3+CL) bonus force damage. It counts as a spell attack.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d6 (3).
