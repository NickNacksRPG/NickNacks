# Magic Dart

## <mark style="color:orange;">Magic Dart \[force] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack with a dart that deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) force dmg. The dart sticks inside of them, magnetizing other force effects to it. Creatures get a +2 bonus on your spell attacks with the \[force] tag against this creature within 1 minute.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
