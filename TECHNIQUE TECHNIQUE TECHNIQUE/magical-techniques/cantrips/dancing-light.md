# Dancing Light

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dancing Light \[light]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 willing creature or object</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr></tbody></table>

You conjure a tiny being of light. It gently floats around the target and gives off bright light and a 20-foot radius, and dim light in a 20 foot radius after that. Casting this spell again causes any other instances of it to end.
