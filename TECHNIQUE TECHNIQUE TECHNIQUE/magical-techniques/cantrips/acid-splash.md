# Acid Splash

## <mark style="color:orange;">Acid Splash \[poison]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your hand, or your staff/seal turn a sickly green color and start bubbling. You flick it towards a creature, making a ranged attack roll, and a sticky glob of caustic goo flies at them. Deals d4+CL (2+CL) poison damage. This damage happens again at the end of the creatures turn unless they are wet, or otherwise remove the acid through some kind of washing.
