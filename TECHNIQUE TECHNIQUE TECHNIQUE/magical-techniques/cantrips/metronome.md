# Metronome

## <mark style="color:orange;">Metronome</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Effects Vary</td><td></td></tr></tbody></table>

Wiggle your staff and cause a random effect from the following list. Roll a D6.

<mark style="color:green;">1 -</mark> Bury yourself underground until the start of your next turn, effectively putting you in stasis against anything that isn't an earth-based attack. If you attack a foe on follow up turn, they are DD.\
<mark style="color:green;">2 -</mark> Confuse yourself. Immediately roll on confusion chart, then end the confusion.\
<mark style="color:green;">3 -</mark> Cause a banana peel to appear under a creature within 30 ft of you. They are knocked down, (reflex negates).\
<mark style="color:green;">4 -</mark> Your tongue becomes long, powerful and elastic, allowing you to make a free drag attempt with a range of 30 ft.\ <mark style="color:green;">5 -</mark> Move 60 ft in a straight line of your choosing \[elusive], stopping when you hit a solid surface. Creatures along the way take d6 (3) crush damage\ <mark style="color:green;">6 -</mark> You and an enemy within 30 ft of you are set ablaze (reflex negates for you both).
