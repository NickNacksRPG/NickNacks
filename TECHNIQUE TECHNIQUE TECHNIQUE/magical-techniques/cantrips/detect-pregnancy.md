# Detect Pregnancy

## <mark style="color:orange;">Detect Pregnancy</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

By holding your hand close to the creature, you can tell whether or not it's pregnant and how far along in it's pregnancy it is.
