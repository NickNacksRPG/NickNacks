# Shocking Grasp

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shocking Grasp \[zap]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>One creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your staff transforms into electricity and coats your hand. Make a melee attack against a creature that deals d8+CL (4+CL) damage. The creature cannot make AoOs for 1 round on hit.

If the creature is wearing metal armor or is sufficiently metallic, get a +4 bonus to attack.
