# Living Roots

## <mark style="color:orange;">Living Roots \[earth]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Choose a creature that is standing on some form of dirt/earth/sand. Roots burst up from the ground and entangle the creature (reflex negates). They can remove this condition automatically as a standard action, or a swift action with an athletics check (spell DC).
