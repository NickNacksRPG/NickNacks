# Death Ward

## <mark style="color:orange;">Death Ward</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>10 minutes</td></tr></tbody></table>

A rune appears on each willing creature inside of the area. All creatures that posses a death ward cannot kill creatures that were subject to the same casting of death ward by defeating them. This spell is used for organized duels and tournaments to prevent contestants from dying.
