# Catch Flame

## <mark style="color:orange;">Catch Flame \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

A mote of flame manifests in an empty hand, or attached to a staff/seal. As part of casting the spell, you can blow this flame onto a creature within reach by making an attack roll. Does d10+CL (5+CL) fire damage. You can continue making more attacks by blowing on it again as a standard action each turn as long as the flame persists.

You can also use this mote of flame to gently warm things, start fires and provide light (10 ft bright light).
