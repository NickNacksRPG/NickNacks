# Arcane Mark

## <mark style="color:orange;">Arcane Mark</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature or object</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Permanent</td></tr></tbody></table>

You touch a surface and inscribe a symbol or a single word. The mark can be no larger than 4x4 inches.

In order to use the spell on a creature, it must be willing, you must land an attack roll or you must make a successful sleight of hand check.

Dispel magic automatically succeeds on an arcane mark.
