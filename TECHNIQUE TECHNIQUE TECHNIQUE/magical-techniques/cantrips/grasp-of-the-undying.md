# Grasp of the Undying

## <mark style="color:orange;">Grasp of the Undying</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack against a creature with a bony spectral hand. Deals d6+CL (3+CL) necrotic damage and applies bone chill for 1 round.

Special: If the target creature is within your natural reach, you can make this attack as a melee attack.
