# Innervate

## <mark style="color:orange;">Innervate \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 immediate action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You call upon the energy of all living things to empower you or an ally. You cast this spell in response to a spell being cast and it increases the spells CL by 1.&#x20;

A creature cannot be the target of this spell more than 1/day.
