# Spirit Animal

## <mark style="color:orange;">Spirit Animal \[companion] \[concentration] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cantrip</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr></tbody></table>

You create a small spectral animal of significance to you. This animal follows basic commands and can roll survival ONLY to guide people through wilderness using your bonus. It cannot interact with the physical world and disappears if it takes damage. Casting this spell again causes any other instances of it to end.

<mark style="color:orange;">Upcast</mark> - Increase duration by 8 hours per mana spent
