# Blink

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blink \[buff] \[teleportation]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">God Mode 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You sporadically blink out of the mortal plane into a private demiplane. At the end of your turn, roll a d6. On a 5 or 6, you blink out of existence, putting you in stasis until the start of your turn.
