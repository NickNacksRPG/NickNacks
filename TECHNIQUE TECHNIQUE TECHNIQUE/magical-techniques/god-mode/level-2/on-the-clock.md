# On the Clock

## <mark style="color:orange;">On the Clock \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">God Mode 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr></tbody></table>

Cause magic circle that resembles the face of a clock to appear on a surface. As long as you concentrate, enemies treat this area as difficult terrain, even if they attempt to jump/fly over it. Allied movement through this area is elusive.
