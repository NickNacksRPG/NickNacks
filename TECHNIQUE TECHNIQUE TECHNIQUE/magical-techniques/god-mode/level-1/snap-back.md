# Snap Back

## <mark style="color:orange;">Snap Back \[elusive]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Time 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

Reverse time ever so slightly, only doing so for 1 particular creature. They move back to the position they were in at the start of their last turn. If used on an enemy (will negates).

Has no effect on a creature that has not acted in combat.
