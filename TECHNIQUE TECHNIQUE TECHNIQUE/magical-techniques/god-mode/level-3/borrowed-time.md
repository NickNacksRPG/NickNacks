# Borrowed Time

## <mark style="color:orange;">Borrowed Time \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">God Mode 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Free Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr></tbody></table>

Borrow some time from your future self. Gain an additional swift action, but you lose your swift action next turn. You can choose to \[mana burn] 3 to not consume your swift action next turn.

Can only be used once a turn.
