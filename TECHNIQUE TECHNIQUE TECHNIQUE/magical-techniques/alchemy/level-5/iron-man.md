# Iron Man

## <mark style="color:orange;">Iron Man \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your body undergoes a rapid and temporary transformation to be completely made of metal.\
You gain the following benefits…

* You do not possess biology, and therefore cannot be envenomed, bleed, suffocated etc. Any non-permanent instance of these effects immediately end on cast.
* Resistance to all physical damage unless it originates from a weapon of adamantine or greater toughness
* Resistance to all poise damage

And the following drawbacks…

* You are silenced
* You speed is 30, but cannot be decreased or increased
* You cannot swim, fly or hover. You automatically sink when submerged in water.
* Your weight increases to be 100x heavier.

If you have the anime theme song racial feature, it changes during this transformation.
