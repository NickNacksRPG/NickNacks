# Blood to Ichor

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blood to Ichor \[poison] \[companion]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fortitude</td></tr></tbody></table>

Summon an ooze inside of a creature. The ooze immediately tries to melt its way out. The affected creature takes 3d8+CL (13+CL) poison damage (fort halves).&#x20;
