# Chimp Out

## <mark style="color:orange;">Chimp Out \[poly, chimeric] \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 Creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Transform a creature so they become a more ape-like version of themselves (fort negates). They gain a climb speed of 30, have their land speed reduced by 15 and are effectively silenced, only capable of making animal noises.

They gain the following special attack\
<mark style="color:green;">Special Attack: Poop Toss</mark> - Make a thrown weapon attack. On hit the creature takes 2d6 (7) poison damage and becomes sickened 2.

Curse can be removed by eating a banana or using poop toss.
