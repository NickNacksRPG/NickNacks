# Fool's Gold

## <mark style="color:orange;">Fool's Gold \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>Metal </td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>10 minutes</td></tr></tbody></table>

You touch metal and give it a shiny new coat of paint, making it shimmer like gold. This changes nothing about the metals physical properties, only its appearance.

You can affect a 1ft cube of metal.

A DC 10 nature check reveals the gold is fake.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Per additional mana you spend, you can add 2 ft to the dimension of the cube you affect. (3 ft cube, 5 ft cube etc).
