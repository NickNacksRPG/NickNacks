# Rubbery

## <mark style="color:orange;">Rubbery \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 Creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your entire body becomes incredibly bouncy and flexible. You only take 1 damage per 10 ft you fall and cannot be drained from falling. You can take the jump action as a free action whenever you take fall damage. You gain compression.
