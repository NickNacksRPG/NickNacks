# Third Leg

## <mark style="color:orange;">Third Leg</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 Creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a creature to grow an additional oversized and unwieldy limb. The creature is clumsy 2 (fort negates).

The additional limb does not disappear over time and must be removed manually. When the creature takes at least 5 slashing damage, the limb quickly withers away.
