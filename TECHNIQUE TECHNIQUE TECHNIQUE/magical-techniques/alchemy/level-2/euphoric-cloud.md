# Euphoric Cloud

## <mark style="color:orange;">Euphoric Cloud \[concentration] \[persistent] \[air]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alchemy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

## <mark style="color:orange;">Focus</mark> - A smoke pipe

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, at least once space of which must be adjacent to caster.</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You take a deep hit from your pipe. When you exhale a cloud of colorful fragrant smoke fills the area. The smoke does not obscure vision significantly enough to give concealment.

Any creature within the area is fascinated by its incredible colors and tantalizing aroma (will negates).
