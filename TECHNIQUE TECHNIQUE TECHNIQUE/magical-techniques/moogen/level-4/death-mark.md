# Death Mark

## <mark style="color:orange;">Death Mark \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>One creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

A red mark appears along the creatures neck(s) resembling a bloody wound (will negates). They gain weakness 2. As an immediate action over any distance, you can "pop" the mark, removing the curse and dealing 5d6+CL (17+CL) true damage.

This curse is removed by killing another creature.
