# Umbral Trespass

## <mark style="color:orange;">Umbral Trespass \[elusive] \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Fade away into shadowy mist and flow into the target of the spell. You disappear and enter stasis while you observe the world from the creature's PoV. On your next turn, you must spend another standard action to leave the creature's body in a violent slash, dealing 5d8+CL (22+CL) slash or pierce damage (fort halves) and dashing 15 ft away from the creature.

This spell cannot be cast again for 1 minute.
