# Encroaching Darkness

## <mark style="color:orange;">Encroaching Darkness \[darkness] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground: see text</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

A cloud of black mist emerges from your fingertips. This mist absorbs all natural light and leaves the area where it covers in complete darkness. At the end of each of your turns, the darkness spreads an additional 10 ft.

You can use the spell effectively as dispel magic against a spell with the \[light] tag. If you succeed, the darkness spell consumes the light and takes hold.
