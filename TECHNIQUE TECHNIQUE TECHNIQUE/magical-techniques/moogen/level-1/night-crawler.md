# Night Crawler

## <mark style="color:orange;">Night Crawler \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

You gain night vision.&#x20;

While in an area of dim light or darkness, you turn into a dark silhouette version of yourself, with only your eyes remaining the same. If you are exposed to bright light, your form slowly returns back to normal over 3 rounds (\~20 seconds). Returning back to darkness turns you back into a silhouette. You can toggle this shadow form off/on if you want yourself to be seen.

While a silhouette, you get a +2 bonus on disguise and stealth.
