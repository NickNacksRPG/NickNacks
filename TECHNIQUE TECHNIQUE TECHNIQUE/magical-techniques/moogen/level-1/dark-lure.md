# Dark Lure

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dark Lure \[subtle]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

A nearly invisible whip of shadows emerges from your staff and wraps around a target. The target is pulled towards you in a subtle way that makes it feel like it is of their own volition. On their next turn, they spend all of their movement to move closer to you and cannot move away.
