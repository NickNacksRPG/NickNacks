# Wave of Darkness

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wave of Darkness \[darkness] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft line, originating from caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You slash at the air with your staff and a dark cloud washes over the area in front of you. The area is total darkness while the spell persists.

You can increase the cast time of this ability to a standard action to have it deal 2d6+CL (7+CL) cold dmg when you initially create it (fort halves). This also gives the spell the \[cold] tag.
