# Silence

## <mark style="color:orange;">Silence \[dismissable] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on a point</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self, or an object</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Sound cannot be created in or enter the area of this spell. Creatures are unable to speak, cry for help etc. Certain abilities dependent on sound like bardic performance, spell casting and abilities that deal sonic damage are all completely disabled.

If you cast the spell on yourself, then it stays anchored to you, but it changes to a 10-foot radius and gains the \[buff] tag.&#x20;
