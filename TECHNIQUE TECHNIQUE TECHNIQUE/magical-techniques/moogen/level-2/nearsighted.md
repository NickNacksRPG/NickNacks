# Nearsighted

## <mark style="color:orange;">Nearsighted \[darkness]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a shadowy mist to cover a creatures eyes and reduce their vision range to 15 ft (will negates). All attacks made against the creature from beyond this range are made as though the target was DD.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, target an additional creature.
