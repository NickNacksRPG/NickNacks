# Darkness

## <mark style="color:orange;">Darkness \[darkness]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moogen 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

A cloud of black mist emerges from your fingertips. This mist absorbs all natural light and leaves the area where it covers in complete darkness.

You can use the spell effectively as dispel magic against a spell with the \[light] tag. If you succeed, the darkness spell consumes the light and takes hold.
