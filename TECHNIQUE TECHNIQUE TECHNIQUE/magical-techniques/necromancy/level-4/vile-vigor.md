# Vile Vigor

## <mark style="color:orange;">Vile Vigor \[healing] \[mana burn]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You can remove practically any status ailment at the cost of your own magic. Mana burn to remove it, increasing in amount based on the status ailment.

1 - Minor Status - Asleep, bleed, blurred vision, charmed, entangled, fatigued, fascinated, grounded, knocked down, shaken/sicken/clumsy stacks, frosty, wet.

2 - Major Status - Bone chilled, ablaze, blinded, confused, envenomed, frightened, staggered, silenced, taunt.

3 - Extreme Status - Dazed, pacified, stun, stasis.

You can change the mana burn into drain to cast this spell as a swift action.
