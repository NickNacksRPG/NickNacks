# Unholy Frenzy

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unholy Frenzy</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr></tbody></table>

Give undead you control within the spells area a +4 bonus to attack this turn.

Choose one specific creature within range of the spell. All undead under your control rush this target down, moving up to it and making a simple attack. If the undead can make ranged attacks, it need not move.
