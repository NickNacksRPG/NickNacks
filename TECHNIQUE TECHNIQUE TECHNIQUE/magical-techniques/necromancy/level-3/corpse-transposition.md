# Corpse Transposition

## <mark style="color:orange;">Corpse Transposition \[teleportation]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 undead you control, or 1 corpse</td></tr></tbody></table>

Briefly vanish in a pool of blood before exploding violently out of the target corpse/minion. If you targeted an allied undead, this kills the undead.
