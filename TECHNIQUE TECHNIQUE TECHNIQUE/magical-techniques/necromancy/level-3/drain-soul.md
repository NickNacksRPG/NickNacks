# Drain Soul

## <mark style="color:orange;">Drain Soul \[healing] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You borrow a piece of creature's soul. They are drained 2 (fort reduces effect to 10 necrotic damage). You regain 10 hp.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase drain by 1 and healing by 5 (with passed saves dealing the would be drained value\*5 necrotic damage).
