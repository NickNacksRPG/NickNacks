# Mr Skeltal

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mr Skeltal \[companion]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

## <mark style="color:orange;">Focus</mark> - 1 Instrument

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 corpse</td></tr></tbody></table>

Raise a skeleton from a corpse whose only calling is to jam. Give it the instrument used as the focus of this spell and it will play it with a bonus of (CL+2) whenever you ask. When not playing, it follows you around.

If you give the skeleton a trumpet, its bonus increases to (CL+4).
