# Bone Shaker

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bone Shaker \[necrotic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>varies</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
The spell cannot target creatures that are BONELESS. A successful monster lore check against a creature reveals if it has bones.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

The spell's effect varies depending on what you target.

<mark style="color:green;">Living Creature</mark> - The creature takes 2d6+CL (7+CL) necrotic damage and is staggered for one round (fort haves damage and negates stagger).

<mark style="color:green;">Willing Undead</mark> - The creature spends an immediate action to make an attack against a creature within reach or move 30 feet

<mark style="color:green;">Unwilling Undead</mark> - Functions as willing undead (fort negates)
