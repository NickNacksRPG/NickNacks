# Halt Undead

## <mark style="color:orange;">Halt Undead</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 undead</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You command an undead creature to bend to your will, and you will it to stand still. It is entangled (will negates). An intelligent undead creature gets a new save at the end of each of its turns.
