# Call of the Grave

## <mark style="color:orange;">Call of the Grave \[upcast] \[earth]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Cause several arms to erupt from the ground and grab onto a creature. The creature is entangled (reflex negates).

The hands share your AC, and have poise equal to their AC. On hit, when the hands are poise broken, or with a successful athletics check (DC=their AC) they retreat back into the ground.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, target an additional creature in range.
