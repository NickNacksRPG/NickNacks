# Blight

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blight \[necrotic] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>varies</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You briefly allow a creature to experience the joy of decomposition. They take 2d8+CL (9+CL) necrotic damage and are sickened 2 for one minute (fort halves dmg and negates sickened).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana you spend, deal an additional d6 damage. If you cast at 3rd lvl or higher, increases the sickened duration to 1 hour.
