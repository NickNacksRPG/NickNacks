# Final Hour

## <mark style="color:orange;">Final Hour \[buff] \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 willing undead</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>2 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

A surge of necrotic energy imbues an undead creature, giving it a very noticeable aura. It takes a -5 penalty to stealth and becomes immune to hp damage. It is afflicted with a curse that destroys it at the end of its second turn.
