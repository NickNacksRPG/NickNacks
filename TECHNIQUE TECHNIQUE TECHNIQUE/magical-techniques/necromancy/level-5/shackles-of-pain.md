# Shackles of Pain

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shackles of Pain \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>10 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You share your pain with another creature, represented by an ethereal shackle that connects you two (will negates). While within 60 ft of this creature, you take half damage, and the other half of the damage is transferred to it as true damage. If the creature leaves this range, the shackle breaks.
