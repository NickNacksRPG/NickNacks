# Gloom Bolt

## <mark style="color:orange;">Gloom Bolt \[necrotic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You shoot a ray of necrotic energy that interferes with the creature's vision. Make a ranged attack that deals 3d8+CL (13+CL) necrotic damage and blinds on hit for 1 round.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, fire an additional ray at a different creature.
