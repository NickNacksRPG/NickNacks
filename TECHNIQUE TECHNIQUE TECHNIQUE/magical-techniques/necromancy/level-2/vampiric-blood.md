# Vampiric Blood

## <mark style="color:orange;">Vampiric Blood \[healing] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 bloody corpse</td></tr></tbody></table>

You drain the blood from a recently deceased creature and use its life force to bolster yourself and a chosen ally within range. You restore 2d6+CL (7+CL) hp and grant the same amount to the chosen ally.

The same corpse cannot be targeted multiple times with this spell.

&#x20;<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For every additional mana spent, increase healing by d6.
