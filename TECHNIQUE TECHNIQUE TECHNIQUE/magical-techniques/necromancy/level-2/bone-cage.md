# Bone Cage

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bone Cage \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Necromancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>10 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 corpse</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
The spell cannot target creatures that are BONELESS. A successful monster lore check against a creature reveals if it has bones.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

You tear the bones out of a corpse and use them to shield yourself. You get +2 AC. This bone armor can be destroyed with a successful sunder.
