# Mind Crush

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mind Crush \[mind-affecting] \[psychic] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Larella 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 Creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You overload a creatures brain with information, giving them a hell of a headache. They take 2d8+CL (9+CL) psychic damage (will halves).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - The target takes d6+CL (3+CL) damage again at the end of its next turn (will halves). Each mana spent adds an additional turn. A creature cannot suffer from multiple mind crushes at the same time.
