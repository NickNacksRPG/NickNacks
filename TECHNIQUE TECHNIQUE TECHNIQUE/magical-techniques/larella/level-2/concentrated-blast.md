# Concentrated Blast

## <mark style="color:orange;">Concentrated Blast \[force]</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Larella 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
This spell can only be cast when concentrating on a spell.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Release your mind from the strain of concentration, channeling this raw mental energy at a target within range. Make an attack that deals 3d10+CL (16+CL) psychic damage. This spell immediately ends the spell you were concentrating on, immediately restoring the ended spells mana cost.

If you are [concentrating on multiple spells at once](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N6QDh7J4iyEozmJJtVa8/classes-1/psychic#id-4-multitasking), you can end both spells to restore more mana.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase dmg by d10 (5).
