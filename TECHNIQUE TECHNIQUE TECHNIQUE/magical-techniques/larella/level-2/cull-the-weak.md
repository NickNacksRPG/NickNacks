# Cull the Weak

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cull the Weak \[psychic] \[buff]</mark>&#x20;

## <mark style="color:orange;">Larella 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

You sense weakness in your enemies. Whenever a creature falls below (CL) hp, you know. You can spend 1 mana and an immediate action to pounce on this weakness, dealing CL psychic damage to them.

If they currently under the effect on one of your spells, this can be done as an instant action.
