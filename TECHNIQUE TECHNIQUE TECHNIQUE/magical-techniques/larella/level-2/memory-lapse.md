# Memory Lapse

## <mark style="color:orange;">Memory Lapse \[mind-affecting] \[subtle]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Larella 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a target to forget the events that have transpired in the past 2 rounds of combat (12 seconds). Certain details may cause them to recall what you did during that time, such as seeing you in a restricted area, or an ally reminding them.
