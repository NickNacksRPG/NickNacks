# Telekinetic Lift

## <mark style="color:orange;">Telekinetic Lift \[buff] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Larella 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr></tbody></table>

You exert your psychic power to manipulate heavy unattended objects within the spells range. You can lift objects weighing up to (100\*CL) pounds, having them float in the air around you. Lifting objects with this spell has the \[concentration] tag, but otherwise does not restrict your movement, occupy your hands etc.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Increase carrying capacity to (1000\*CL) lbs.
