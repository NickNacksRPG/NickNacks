# Dimensional Anchor

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dimensional Anchor</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>10 minutes</td></tr></tbody></table>

You fire an anchor-shaped projectile a creature. Make a ranged attack; if it hits, the target is dimensionally anchored, preventing them from using \[teleportation] effects, either on themselves or others.

The DC to dispel this effect is 13+CL.
