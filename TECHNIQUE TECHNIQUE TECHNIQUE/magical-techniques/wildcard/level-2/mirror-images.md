# Mirror Images

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mirror Images \[buff] \[figment] \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

4 illusory copies appear within your space and mirror your actions. Increase your AC by 1 for every clone you still have. If a creature grazes you, they destroy a clone.

You can only cast this spell once every minute.
