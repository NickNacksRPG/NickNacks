# Alarming Sigil

## <mark style="color:orange;">Alarming Sigil \[sigil]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>20 ft radius, emanating from a point</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

You inscribe a magic rune on the surface of an object. Whenever a creature of at least tiny or larger size enters this area, the sigil triggers, creating one of 2 effects you decided on casting. In either case, you are mentally alerted and awakened if sleeping.

<mark style="color:orange;">Silent Alarm</mark> - Only you are mentally alerted\
<mark style="color:orange;">Loud Alarm</mark> - A loud sound starts to play and repeat, like beeping. Creatures within 300ft can hear it in ideal conditions.
