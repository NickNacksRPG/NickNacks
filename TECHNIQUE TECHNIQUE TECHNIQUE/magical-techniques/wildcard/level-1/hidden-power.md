# Hidden Power

## <mark style="color:orange;">Hidden Power \[elemental] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Unleash a wave of elemental energy that homes in on a creature, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) damage (reflex halves). The damage type can be any elemental damage type and is chosen when cast.&#x20;

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, deal an additional d8 (4) damage.
