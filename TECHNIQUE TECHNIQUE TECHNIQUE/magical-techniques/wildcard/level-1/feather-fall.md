# Feather Fall

## <mark style="color:orange;">Feather Fall \[air] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Immediate Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Used to save people from deadly falls. The target gently falls 60 ft per round and safely lands on the ground. The spell ends early if the target lands.

<mark style="color:orange;">Upcast 2</mark> - Target up to 4 creatures within 30 ft of each other with the spell.
