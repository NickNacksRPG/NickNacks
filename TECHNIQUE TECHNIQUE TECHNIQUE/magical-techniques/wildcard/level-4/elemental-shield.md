# Elemental Shield

## <mark style="color:orange;">Elemental Shield \[buff] \[dmg type]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wildcard 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

While this spell is active, you are wreathed in an elemental damage type of your choosing. You gain resistance to this damage type. 1/turn when you take damage, the spell rebounds and deals (CL) of your chosen damage type to the creature that did the damage (instant action).
