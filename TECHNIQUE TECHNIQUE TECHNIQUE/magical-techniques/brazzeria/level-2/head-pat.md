# Head Pat

## <mark style="color:orange;">Head Pat \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Brazzeria 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 Creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You roll a caster level check to immediately end a harmful effect. The DC is (10+CL of the effect). For non-spell effects its (10+creature's lvl). If the status is removed, the creature heals 5 hp.

This spell cannot be used in order to remove permanent status effects, like curses or afflictions.
