# Regenerate

## <mark style="color:orange;">Regenerate \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Brazzeria 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 minute

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You infuse a creature with incredible healing powers. They instantly regenerate damaged organs/limbs. Severed limbs in the creature's possession are automatically reattached. Destroyed or misplaced limbs are instead regenerated over 1 minute, during which the creature is stunned.

The creature is drained 1 after casting.
