# Cure Wounds

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cure Wounds \[healing] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Brazzeria 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Magically close wounds and replace lost blood. The creature gains 5+CL hp and is cured of fatigue.

By adding \[mana burn] equal to spell level you give it the \[overheal] tag.

<mark style="color:orange;">Upcast</mark> - For every additional mana you spend, increase healing by 3 and you can target an additional creature. You can move in between delivering the spell to reach more than one creature.
