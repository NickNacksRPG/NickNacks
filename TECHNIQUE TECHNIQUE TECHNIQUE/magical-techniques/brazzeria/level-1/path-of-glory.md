# Path of Glory

## <mark style="color:orange;">Path of Glory \[healing] \[concentration] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Brazzeria 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You bless the ground that you walk on. Your movement this turn leaves behind a glowing golden radiance for 1 minute. Allies that exist in or move through one of these spaces restore 2 hp. This effect cannot happen more than once per turn.

<mark style="color:orange;">Upcast</mark> - Heal 1 more per mana spent
