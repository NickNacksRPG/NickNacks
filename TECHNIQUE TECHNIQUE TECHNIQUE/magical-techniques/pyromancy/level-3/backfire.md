# Backfire

## <mark style="color:orange;">Backfire \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

This spell functions exactly like [dispel magic](../../wildcard/level-2/dispel-magic.md), except when you successfully counterspell or dispel a \[buff], the creature you target takes 4d8+CL (18+CL) fire damage.
