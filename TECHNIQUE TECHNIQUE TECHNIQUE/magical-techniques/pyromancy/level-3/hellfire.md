# Hellfire

## <mark style="color:orange;">Hellfire \[fire] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute (see text)</td></tr></tbody></table>

Temporarily borrow some fire from the demon realm, burning yourself in the process. You explode violently dealing 4d8+CL (18+CL) fire damage and setting creatures ablaze (reflex halves dmg and negates ablaze). This spell and the fires it causes ignore fire resistance.

The area of the spell is scorched for 1 minute, causing the movement of any fiends inside of it to increase by 20 for 1 round.

You yourself take (CL) fire damage from the burst, and ignore being set ablaze.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, the spell deals an additional d8 (4) dmg.
