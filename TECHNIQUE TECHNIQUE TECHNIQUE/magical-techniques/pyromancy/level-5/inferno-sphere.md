# Inferno Sphere

## <mark style="color:orange;">Inferno Sphere \[buff] \[fire] \[persistent]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>15 ft radius aura, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

The area around you is pelted with a whirling storm of flame. All creatures (except you) within 15 feet are instantly and persistently set ablaze. Creatures that start their turns in range or move into range take (CL) fire damage.

Your speed is reduced to 10 while buffed by this spell.
