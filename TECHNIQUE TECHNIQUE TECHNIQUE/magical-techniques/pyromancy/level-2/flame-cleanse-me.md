# Flame Cleanse Me

## <mark style="color:orange;">Flame Cleanse Me \[healing] \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

You hold a flame to your chest that burns away your ailments. Poisons, bleed, and magical damage-over-time or status conditions are removed at the cost of 5 hp.
