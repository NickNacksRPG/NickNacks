# Boiling Blood

## <mark style="color:orange;">Boiling Blood \[concentration] \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Ignite a creatures insides, making them convulse in pain. They are staggered and take (CL) fire damage each round at the start of its turn (fort negates). At the end of each turn, save again.

Creatures that are already ablaze take a -4 penalty to their save.
