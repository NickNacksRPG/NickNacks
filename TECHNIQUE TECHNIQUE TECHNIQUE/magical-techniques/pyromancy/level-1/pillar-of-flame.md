# Pillar of Flame

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pillar of Flame \[fire] \[concentration] \[persistent] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>One space, 30 ft high</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Ignite a circle on the ground and cause a pillar of flame to erupt from it. Creatures in the area of the pillar take 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Increase damage by d8 (4) and create an additional pillar.
