# Forbidden Flame

## <mark style="color:orange;">Forbidden Flame \[fire]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Convert all of your mana into heat with a spark. Cause the air to explode in a creatures face, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage. You automatically spend all of your mana, including any reserves, when you use this technique. For each mana spent beyond the first, increase the spells CL by 1.
