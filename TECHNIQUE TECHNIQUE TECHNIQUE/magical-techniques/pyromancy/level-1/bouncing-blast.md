# Bouncing Blast

## <mark style="color:orange;">Bouncing Blast \[fire] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature (see text)</td></tr></tbody></table>

You toss a mote of flame that hungers for more if it successfully consumes its target. Roll an attack against a creature that deals 2d8+CL (9+CL) fire damage. If it defeats the creature, you can target a new one within 30 ft with an additional attack. This can continue indefinitely.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8.
