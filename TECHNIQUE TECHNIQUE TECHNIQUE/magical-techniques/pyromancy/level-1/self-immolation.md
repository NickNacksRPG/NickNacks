# Self Immolation

## <mark style="color:orange;">Self Immolation \[fire] \[buff] \[persistent] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pyromancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>5 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You light yourself on fire and the fire hungers for things to burn. At the end of your turn, you take 4 fire damage. Whenever a creature is within 5ft of you, they take 4 fire damage.\
Your speed increases by 5 ft.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - The damage increases by 2 and your speed increases by 5 per additional mana spent. If you spend at least 3 mana, your movement is \[elusive].
