# Splatter

## <mark style="color:orange;">Splatter \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Spit a slimy ball at the ground that splatters on impact. Creatures in the spells area at the time of casting, or that walk into the spells area are affected by it.\
\
Choose one:\
<mark style="color:green;">Greasy</mark> - Creatures are knocked down (reflex/acrobatics negates). Treating the grease as difficult terrain while moving through it prevents the creature from being knocked down.

<mark style="color:green;">Gooey</mark> - Creatures are entangled (fort negates). Athletics as a swift action to remove snare.
