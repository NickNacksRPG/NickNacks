# Gooball

## <mark style="color:orange;">Gooball \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Your throat swells as your mouth fills with bile. You spit a glob of goo at a creature as a ranged attack roll. Their speed is reduced by 15 for 1 minute. A creature can remove this goo automatically as a standard action, or a swift action with athletics.
