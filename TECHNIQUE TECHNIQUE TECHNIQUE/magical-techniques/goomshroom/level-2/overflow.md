# Overflow

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overflow \[water] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>One 15 ft diameter hemisphere, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a hemisphere of water to suddenly form in an area. This water has no inherently negative effects, but [aquatic terrain](https://app.gitbook.com/s/LcHjOjPnBbxeu9BQoJ8P/environmental-hazards/water) can be a problem for some and a boon for others.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - By spending 4 mana, the diameter of the sphere increases to 25ft.
