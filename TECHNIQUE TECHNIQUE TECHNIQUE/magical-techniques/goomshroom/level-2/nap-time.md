# Nap Time

## <mark style="color:orange;">Nap Time \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You inflict a wave of drowsiness on a creature, causing it to fall asleep (will reduces to clumsy 1). If the creature stays asleep until the end up the spells duration, it gains 20 temp hp.
