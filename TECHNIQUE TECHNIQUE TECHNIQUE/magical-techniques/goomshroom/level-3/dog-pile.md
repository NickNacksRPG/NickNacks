# Dog Pile

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dog Pile \[elusive]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

Cover the ground underneath your foe in sticky goo, entangling them for 1 round (reflex reduces to -15 speed).

Your allies are magnetized to this enemy. Any allies can move 30 ft closer to a creature entangled by this spell as a free action once.
