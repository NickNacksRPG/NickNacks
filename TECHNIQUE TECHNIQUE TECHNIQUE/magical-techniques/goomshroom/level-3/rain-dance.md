# Rain Dance

## <mark style="color:orange;">Rain Dance \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>1 mile radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr></tbody></table>

You dance a jig. After you complete your dance, clouds roll in and it starts to lightly rain. After the spell ends, the weather returns back to what it was before casting.

All weather inducing spells do not function underground.
