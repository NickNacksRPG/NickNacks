# Serenity

## <mark style="color:orange;">Serenity</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Goomshroom 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You compel the enemies around you to cease fighting. Enemies are pacified (will negates). They get a new save against this effect each round.

If an ally harms a pacified enemy, they get an additional save to end the effect.
