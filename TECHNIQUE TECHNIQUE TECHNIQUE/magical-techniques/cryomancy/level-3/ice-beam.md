# Ice Beam

## <mark style="color:orange;">Ice Beam \[frost]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Fire a ray of ice at a foe. Make a ranged attack. It deals 4d8+CL (18+CL) cold damage and makes them frosty and entangled on hit. If they are already frosty or entangled, they are instead entombed. Athletics as a swift action to escape entangle (vs DC), or standard if entombed.
