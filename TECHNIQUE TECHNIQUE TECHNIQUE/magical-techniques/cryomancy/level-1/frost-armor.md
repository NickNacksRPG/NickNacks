# Frost Armor

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frost Armor \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cover yourself in a thin protective ice barrier. You gain resistance to the next instance of HP damage that is not crush, slash or pierce.

Once you take damage from any source, minus damage over time effects like bleed or self inflicted damage, the ice barrier shatters and frost armor ends.

If you are ablaze when you cast this spell, instead of the usual benefits, put out the flames.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast 4</mark> - This spell becomes an immediate action.
