# Ice Skating

## <mark style="color:orange;">Ice Skating \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

You coat the bottoms of your feet/footwear etc with ice, allowing you to slide along cold terrain with ease. While in an arctic environment, or while moving across ice or snow, your gain a "skating speed" of 50.
