# Frost Breath

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frost Breath \[frost]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>15 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You exhale and fill the area with ice cold air. Creatures inside take 2d8+CL (9+CL) frost dmg and are made frosty (fort halves dmg and negates frosty).
