# Blanket of Snow

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blanket of Snow</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

A shadow appears beneath the target, and a blanket of snow falls upon them immediately after. The creature takes 2d8+CL (9+CL) crush damage and has to spend 30 ft of movement to dig themselves out of the snow (reflex halves dmg and getting buried).

While buried, the creature cannot move, has resistance to fire damage and has cover.
