# Snowball

## <mark style="color:orange;">Snowball \[frost]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You toss a snowball at a creature, aiming for their weak points. Make a ranged attack that does 2d8+CL (9+CL) frost damage. They also suffer one of the following effects (fort negates).

<mark style="color:green;">Eyes</mark> - Creature has blurred vision for 1 round.

<mark style="color:green;">Mouth</mark> - Creature is staggered for 1 round.
