# Ice Lance

## <mark style="color:orange;">Ice Lance \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Turn your staff into a deadly ice spike, and stab a creature with it for 3d8+CL (13+CL) pierce damage.

If the target is entangled by a \[frost] effect, you make the attack with advantage and it does 3d10+CL (16+CL) pierce damage.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, the spell deals an additional d8 (or d10 if entangled) damage.
