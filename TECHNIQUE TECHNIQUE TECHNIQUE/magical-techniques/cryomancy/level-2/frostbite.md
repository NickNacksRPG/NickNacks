# Frostbite

## <mark style="color:orange;">Frostbite \[frost]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You subject to creature to a cold winter's night over the course of a few seconds. On a successful attack roll, the creature takes 3d8+CL (13+CL) frost dmg. It gains weakness 5 frost for the spells duration.
