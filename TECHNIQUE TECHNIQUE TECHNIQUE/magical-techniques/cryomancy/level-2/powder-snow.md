# Powder Snow

## <mark style="color:orange;">Powder Snow \[concentration] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

Snowflakes permeate the air, chilling creatures to the bone. The spell leaves behind a cloud that persistently inflicts weakness 5 to frost damage to all creatures without frost resistance in the cloud.

Your allies gain frost resistance while within the area of powder snow.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - Per each additional mana spent, increase the weakness the spell inflicts by 2, and increase its radius by 5 ft.
