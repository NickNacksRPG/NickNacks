# Creeping Ice

## <mark style="color:orange;">Creeping Ice \[frost] \[concentration] \[persistent]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>5 ft radius, centered on a point in the air</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You drain the heat from the air, coating an area in frost. The radius of the AoE increases by 10 feet each round on your turn. Creatures in the AoE at the time of casting or that enter/end their turn in the AoE take 2d8+CL (9+CL) cold damage and are staggered for 1 round (fort negates stagger).
