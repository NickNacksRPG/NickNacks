# Cold Feet

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cold Feet \[frost]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>60 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Call forth icicles underneath all of your foes. Each one is entangled and takes 6d6+CL (21+CL) cold damage (reflex removes entangle and halves dmg).

Athletics as a standard action to escape.
