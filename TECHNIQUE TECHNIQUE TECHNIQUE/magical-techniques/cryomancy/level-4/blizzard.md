# Blizzard

## <mark style="color:orange;">Blizzard \[frost] \[air]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cryomancy 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>60 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Pelt your foes with a wave a frigid air in a wide area. Creatures take 5d8+CL (22+CL) cold damage and makes them frosty and entangled (fort halves dmg and negates entangle). If they are already frosty or entangled, they are instead entombed. Athletics as a swift action to escape entangle (vs DC), or standard if entombed.
