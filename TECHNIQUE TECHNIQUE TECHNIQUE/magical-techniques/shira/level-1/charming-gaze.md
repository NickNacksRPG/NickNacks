# Charming Gaze

## <mark style="color:orange;">Charming Gaze \[charm] \[subtle]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shira 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Varies</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You lock your eyes onto a target and charm them (will negates). If you're currently engaged in combat with the creature, the spells duration is 1 minute. If you are not, the spell's duration is 8 hours. Entering combat against them shortens the remaining duration to 1 minute.
