# Sense Motives

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sense Motives</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shira 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - N/A

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td></td><td></td></tr></tbody></table>

While this technique is prepared, you gain a +4 bonus to insight checks. Whenever you glean something using insight, you can transfer the knowledge you gain to any creature you can see as vague impressions, effectively treating it as though they had succeeded in using insight to detect falsehoods.
