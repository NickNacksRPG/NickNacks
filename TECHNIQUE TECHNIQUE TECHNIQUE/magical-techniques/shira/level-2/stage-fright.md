# Stage Fright

## <mark style="color:orange;">Stage Fright \[mind-affecting] \[fear] \[psychic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shira 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

The fear of failure looms over the target creature (will negates). They take a -2 penalty to attack and opposed skill checks. Whenever they graze, miss or fail an opposed skill check they initiated, they take d6+CL (3+CL) psychic damage.&#x20;

Outside of combat this gains the \[subtle] tag and gives creatures disadvantage on their next skill check made in front of a crowd. Its duration changes to 24 hours. Once they roll the check, the spell ends.
