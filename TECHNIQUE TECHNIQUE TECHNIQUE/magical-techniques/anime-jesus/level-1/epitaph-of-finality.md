# Epitaph of Finality

## <mark style="color:orange;">Epitaph of Finality \[concentration] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 undead creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You sing out to the dead, inflicting drained 1. If it is defeated while this spell is active and it does not have regeneration, it is instantly and utterly consumed in holy flames, leaving behind only ashes. If it has regeneration, this spell will cause them to become dazed for one turn upon revival.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase drained stacks by 1.
