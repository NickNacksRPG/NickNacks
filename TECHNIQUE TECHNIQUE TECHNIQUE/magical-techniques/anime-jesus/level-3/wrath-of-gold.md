# Wrath of Gold

## <mark style="color:orange;">Wrath of Gold \[holy] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cross your arms and then enter a powerful stance as you release stored energy. Creatures in range are knocked back 10 feet, knocked down and take 4d8+CL (18+CL) holy damage (fort halves dmg and negates knock down/knockback).

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d8 (4).
