# Repelling Light

## <mark style="color:orange;">Repelling Light \[light] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius burst, centered on a point in space</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You create a globe of bright light. Undead and light-sensitive creatures that start their turns inside or attempt to enter this zone immediately recoil, using their movement to escape the zone if possible (will negates), or suffering -2 attack if they do not.
