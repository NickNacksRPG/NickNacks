# Deific Presence

## <mark style="color:orange;">Deific Presence \[concentration] \[holy]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>20 ft radius burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

You assume a position akin to prayer before you throw your arms to the side and assume the pose of deific power. You radiate intense waves of holy energy that ramp up over time, damaging all enemies within 20 ft. You move at -15 speed while casting this spell. You must spend a standard action each round to continue concentrating.

<mark style="color:orange;">Round 1</mark> - 5d6+CL (17+CL) holy damage (reflex halves).

<mark style="color:orange;">Round 2</mark> - 5d8+CL (22+CL) and increases the DC by 4.

<mark style="color:orange;">Round 3</mark> - 5d10+CL (27+CL) and no longer has a save.

Any creature that fails its save (or automatically on round 3) versus this spell is staggered for 1 round.
