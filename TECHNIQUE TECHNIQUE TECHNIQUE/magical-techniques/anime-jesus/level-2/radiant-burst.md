# Radiant Burst

## <mark style="color:orange;">Radiant Burst \[holy] \[light]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius burst, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

You manifest a shining light and cause it to flash brightly before extinguishing. Creatures are blinded for two rounds (reflex negates). A creature can make a new save against this effect at the end of its turn.

Undead, fiends and creatures with light sensitivity take 10 holy damage (no save).
