# Oath to Order

## <mark style="color:orange;">Flame of Destiny \[buff] \[language] \[light]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Anime Jesus 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You recite an oath with your target, bolstering their courage in the face of darkness. The target sheds light out to 20 feet, granting them a +4 bonus to intimidate vs undead and fiends within this light and a +4 bonus to saves against fear, darkness, and blinding effects.
