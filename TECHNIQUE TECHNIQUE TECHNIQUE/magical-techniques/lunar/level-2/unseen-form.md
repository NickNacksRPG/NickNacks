# Unseen Form

## <mark style="color:orange;">Unseen Form \[glamer] \[buff] \[subtle]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Swift Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

While in the presence of moonlight, you become partially invisible, granting you total concealment from creatures beyond 30 feet and partial concealment from any creatures closer.

Taking any actions besides simple interactions or movement reduces the effect to partial concealment.
