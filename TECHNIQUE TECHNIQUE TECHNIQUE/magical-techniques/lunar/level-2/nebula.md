# Nebula

## <mark style="color:orange;">Nebula \[force] \[light]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft cone</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

Conjure a cloud of stardust that illuminates in a 20 ft radius. It obscures vision beyond 5 feet, granting partial concealment. Anything beyond 5 feet grants total concealment. At the end of the spell's duration, it ignites dealing 3d8+CL (13+CL) force damage to all creatures in the area automatically.
