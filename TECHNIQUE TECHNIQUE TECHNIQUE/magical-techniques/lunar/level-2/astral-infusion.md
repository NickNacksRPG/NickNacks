# Astral Infusion

## <mark style="color:orange;">Astral Infusion \[force] \[healing] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>10 ft radius, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Reflex</td></tr></tbody></table>

A mote of energy resembling a shining star lands on this spot, dealing 2d8+CL (9+CL) force damage to all enemies (reflex halves). Some of the affected creatures' energy is absorbed and you may divert that energy to an ally within range.

They gain restore hp equal to the (3\*number of creatures damaged by the spell).
