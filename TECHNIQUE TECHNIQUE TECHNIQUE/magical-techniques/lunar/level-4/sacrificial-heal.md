# Sacrificial Heal

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sacrificial Heal \[healing]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Sacrifice any amount of your hp and transfer it to another creature. Also transfers this spell's mana cost to the target. If you choose to \[mana burn], you can even heal mana burn from the creature an equal amount.
