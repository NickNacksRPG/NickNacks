# Night Comet

## <mark style="color:orange;">Night Comet \[force] \[light] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr></tbody></table>

Make a ranged attack roll as you fire a large dart of magic. The target takes 5d10+CL (27+CL) force damage. While in the presence of moonlight, this dart is invisible, making it DD against creatures that cannot see it.

<mark style="color:green;">Upcast</mark> - For each additional mana spent, increase damage by d10 (5).
