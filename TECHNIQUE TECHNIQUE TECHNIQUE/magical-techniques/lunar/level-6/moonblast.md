# Moonblast

## <mark style="color:orange;">Moonblast \[force] \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 6</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>60 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 round, see text</td></tr></tbody></table>

You gently float into the air and the cradle yourself with energy that takes the shape of a full moon. You enter stasis until the start of your next turn.

As a free action at the start of your next turn, launch the moon at an enemy within range. Roll a ranged attack to deal 7d6+CL (24+CL) force damage. The creature also gains weakness 10 to force damage for 1 minute.
