# Center of the Universe

## <mark style="color:orange;">Center of the Universe \[buff] \[force]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr></tbody></table>

Many glowing orbs immediately move away from you and start rotating at a fixed distance of 15 ft. At the start and end of your turns, deal (CL) force damage to creatures that are exactly 15 ft away from you.
