# Star Sight

## <mark style="color:orange;">Star Sight \[buff]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 swift action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Target</td><td>Self</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>Until Rest</td></tr></tbody></table>

By spending a standard action to concentrate each round, you gain a completely unobstructed view of the sky as though it was a clear night. You ignore things like environmental conditions, being indoors and underground, light pollution etc.

You get +2 bonus to astronomy or survival checks made to navigate.
