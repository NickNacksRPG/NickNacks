# Singularity

## <mark style="color:orange;">Singularity \[force] \[persistent] \[concentration]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Lunar 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>10 ft, centered on a point on the ground</td></tr><tr><td>AoE</td><td>300 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>3 rounds</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Choose a point in space to manifest a dark sphere with a selective gravitational pull. Immediately upon casting, and as a swift action each subsequent turn, 3 creatures (or objects weighing less than a ton) of your choosing within 100 feet of the singularity are pulled 30 feet closer (fort reduces to 10ft). Any creature in an adjacent space is considered touching it, dealing 2d6+CL (7+CL) force damage.
