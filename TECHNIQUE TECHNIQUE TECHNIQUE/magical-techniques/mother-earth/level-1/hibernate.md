# Hibernate

## <mark style="color:orange;">Hibernate</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>Reach</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 willing or defeated creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>24 hours</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a creature to enter a catatonic state akin to a deep sleep. Nothing short of taking damage can awaken the creature from its slumber. The creature gains the benefit of resting and is staggered for 1 minute after waking up if it slept the full duration.
