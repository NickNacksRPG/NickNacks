# Pollinate

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pollinate \[poison] \[upcast]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 1</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Shower a foe with a thick cloud of toxic plant dust. Creature takes d6+CL (3+CL) poison damage. The powder also has an additional effect (fort negates).

Choose One:\
<mark style="color:green;">Poison Powder</mark> - Creature takes an additional (3+CL) poison damage at the end of its turns (fort ends effect).\
<mark style="color:green;">Rage Powder</mark> - Creature is taunted by you for one round.

## <mark style="color:green;">Upcast 3</mark>

The spell instead deals 2d6+CL (7+CL) poison damage. You gain new options.

Choose One:\
<mark style="color:green;">Sleep Powder</mark> - Creature is put to sleep.\
<mark style="color:green;">Stun Spore</mark> - Creature is dazed for 1 turn.\
<mark style="color:green;">Deadly Poison Powder</mark> - Creature takes an additional 2d6+CL (7+CL) poison damage at the end of its turns (fort ends effect).
