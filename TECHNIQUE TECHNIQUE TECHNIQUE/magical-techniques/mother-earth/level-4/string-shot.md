# String Shot

## <mark style="color:orange;">String Shot</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 4</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Fort</td></tr></tbody></table>

Shoot a precise string of webbing at a creature. They are entangled and you may slam them against the ground into any position within the spells range to deal 5d6+CL (17+CL) crush damage (fort negates damage). Athletics as a standard action to remove entangled.
