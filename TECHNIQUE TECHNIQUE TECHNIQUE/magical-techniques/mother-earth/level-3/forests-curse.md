# Forest's Curse

## <mark style="color:orange;">Forest's Curse \[curse]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You curse a foe to wander aimlessly and suffer while in forested terrain. They take a -10 penalty to survival checks made to navigate in forest/jungle type terrain. They also lose poison resistance if they have it.

In order to remove this curse, the creature must plant 3 new trees.
