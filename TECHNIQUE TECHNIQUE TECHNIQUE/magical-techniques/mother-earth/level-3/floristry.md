# Floristry

## <mark style="color:orange;">Floristry \[companion]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You cause a vibrant [thorny flower](https://app.gitbook.com/s/LcHjOjPnBbxeu9BQoJ8P/bestiary/plants/thorn-spitter) to sprout from the ground. It mindlessly shoots your enemies for the spells duration, focusing a target you choose when you cast, or the nearest creature if the first target leaves the flowers range. It loses 5 hp each turn. When it is defeated, it wilts and crumbles to dust.
