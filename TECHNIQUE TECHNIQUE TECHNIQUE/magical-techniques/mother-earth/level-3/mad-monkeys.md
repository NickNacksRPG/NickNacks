# Mad Monkeys

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mad Monkeys \[companion] \[sonic]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 minute</td></tr></tbody></table>

You channel your inner tree to summon one to your aid. Gain (3\*CL) temp hp when you cast this spell. Summon a treant that understands druidic and does its best to aid you.

When you lose this temp hp, the treant disappears.
