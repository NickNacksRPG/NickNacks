# Sunny Day

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sunny Day \[recharge]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 3</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>1 mile burst, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr></tbody></table>

The clouds part and the sun shines brightly. All plant creatures in the area gain +2 attack and FH 1.

You cannot cast this spell at night.

All weather inducing spells do not function underground.
