# Power of the Sun

## <mark style="color:orange;">Power of the Sun \[psychic] \[fear] \[dismissable]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 2</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard Action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Range</td><td>30 ft</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>1 creature</td></tr><tr><td>Duration</td><td>1 hour</td></tr><tr><td>Save</td><td>Will</td></tr></tbody></table>

You temporarily instill great fear of the sun to a creature. Every turn the creature starts its turn in an area lit with sunlight or moves into a sun-lit area, it takes 2d6 (7) psychic damage and is shaken 1.
