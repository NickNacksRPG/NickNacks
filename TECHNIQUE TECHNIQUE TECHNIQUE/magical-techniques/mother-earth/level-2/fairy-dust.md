You borrow some fairy dust from the fey wilds and toss it at your enemy, with a variety of effects.

Choose One:\
<mark style="color:green;">Sparkles</mark> - Coat creatures in brilliant sparkly particles. Attacks made against a creature covered in sparkles have seeking. Sparkly creatures have a -10 stealth penalty.\
<mark style="color:green;">Flashy</mark> - The dust sparkles so brightly that it blinds creatures for 1 minute (reflex reduces to blurred vision). At the end of a creature's turn, they can make an additional save to reduce the condition.\
