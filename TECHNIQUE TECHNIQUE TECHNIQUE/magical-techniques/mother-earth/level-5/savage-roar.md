# Savage Roar

## <mark style="color:orange;">Savage Roar \[mind-affecting]</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mother Earth 5</mark>

## <mark style="color:orange;">Cast Time</mark> - 1 Standard action

<table><thead><tr><th width="161">Effect</th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>AoE</td><td>30 ft radius, centered on caster</td></tr><tr><td>Target</td><td>All allies</td></tr></tbody></table>

Let out a powerful roar to inspire savagery and anger in your allies. All allied creatures make a melee attack against a creature within reach. Beasts and any creature that can understand druidic get a +4 bonus to this attack.
