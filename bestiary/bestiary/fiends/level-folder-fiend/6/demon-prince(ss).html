Demon Prince(ss)

A well-dressed fiend carries itself with a great sense of importance as it struts across the battlefield. With a bow it introduces itself to you, and then invites its friends to join.

Level 6 Medium Fiend (Mastermind)

AC 19
HP 56
Poise 25
Saves Fort +7, Refl +8, Will +10

Resistance - Fire
Double Team Recharge Sp - A prince can use Stunt Double as a swift action. Usable 1/minute.

2d8+3 (11)

Innate Techniques (CL 8) - Dance With Me!, Venomous Promise, The Hand that Feeds, Fake Out, Spell Blade (U3)
1/day - Fire and Brimstone, Hellfire, Backfire, Mirror Images

Now its a Party! Companion Summon Ally- When a prince uses its Fire and Brimstone technique, he can also bring forth to 2 allied fiends (LvL 4 or lower) from the The Demon Realm to fight by his side while he concentrates on it. He can extend the duration by 1 round or change the direction of the technique as a standard action.
