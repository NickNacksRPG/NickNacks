Twinkubus

Level 3 Medium Fiend [mastermind] [lewd]

Defense Traits

AC 18
HP 38
Poise 20
Fort +4, Refl +4, Will +6

Enigma - Advantage on saves against effects that read your thoughts or otherwise pry into your mind.

Offense Traits (DC 15)

Slap (C) +4
d6+1 (4) +charm on-hit

Innate Techniques - Charming Gaze, Feign Defeat
1/day - Command, Tongue Twister, Power Overwhelming

Power Bottom - During sexual acts, a twinkubus can use the technique Order 66, but (will negates). If the twinkubus summoned the creature, he can spend a swift action to activate order 66 instead of a standard action.

Flirty Feint - While in combat, a twinkubus can feint a creature as a swift action and impose 1 of the following penalties.

  • Impose disadvantage on will saves against the twinkubus' abilities this turn
  • The creature is DD to all creatures until the start of the twinkubus' next turn

Summon Ally [companion] [recharge] - Two of any level 2 or lower fiend. Can also summon a single creature under the influence of their Power Bottom ability.

Any creature summoned also gains the Take the Hit teamwork feat.

Weaknesses/Deep Lore

Tucking Technique - A twinkubus is androgynous and can pretend to be female, even during sexual encounters (DC 26 perception to notice). After such an encounter, they can reveal their true gender destroying the psyche of heterosexual males and homosexual females. This inflicts a unique T2 affliction.

Shattered Sexuality - You are paranoid of all interactions, thinking about what kind of hardware the creatures you interact with have under the hood. Any and all opposed skill checks you make with disadvantage. If a creature flirts with you, attempts to seduce you or even so much as compliments you, you take 10 psychic damage. You can only take such psychic damage 1/minute.

Cure - Enter a relationship, or continue one, spending 1 week with your partner(s), culminating in intercourse, reaffirming your tastes.

Profane Loan - After a sexual encounter with a twinkubus, you temporarily get a +1 bonus to all d20 rolls for 24 hours. After this buff runs out, you acquire a unique T2 affliction.

Touch Starvation - Take a -1 penalty to all d20 rolls and have disadvantage on saves vs a twinkubus' abilities.

Cure - Have intercourse with a twinkubus, or deprive yourself of intimate contact for 1 week.

Other Traits

Ability Scores - Str +1, Dex +1, Lucc +1, Int +1, Wis +1, Cha +3

Speed - 30

Telepathy

Tongues - A twinkubus can understand and speak all non-secret languages.

Almost Human - A twinkubus only has the smallest of details that it is a demon, such as small horns and a spaded tail. It can disguise itself as a hellion without any adjustments, or get rid of these minor details to appear human. In either case, creatures proficient in planes, or that initiate sexual contact with a twinkubus can roll a DC 28 perception check to see through their ruse.

Pseudo-Intellectualism - A twinkubus can roll deception in place of any knowledge skill to roll monster lore. This does not actually tell them anything, but rather convinces another creature of something using "facts" and "logic."

Feats - Skill Focus (Deception), Signature Skill (Deception), Take the Hit

Skills - +6 perception, +6 acrobatics, +6 planes, +6 local, +6 sleight of hand, +12 (adv) deception, +12 persuasion
