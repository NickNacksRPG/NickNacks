Weaknesses/Deep Lore

Part of Me - A spirit snatcher's arcane bag is part of its body, and therefore cannot store anything particularly dangerous like pointy weapons or +Toxins. In particular, if a creature places a lump of coal in the bag (through SoH, poise breaking or as an attack), the spirit snatcher takes 10 psychic damage.
