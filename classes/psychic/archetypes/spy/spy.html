Spy

The spy is primarily concerned with information gathering and can do so in many ingenious ways. Their signature ability is the most powerful and versatile scrying effect in the entire game, allowing them to witness people and locations from the safety of their home.

This ability and its progression are pretty complicated. See Glass Reflection subsection for details.

1 - Lip Reading

As long as you have a clear view of a creature and you understand the language that it's speaking, you need not hear it to understand it.

2 - MM: Mind Touch

When a creature fails a save against an effect you inflict, it willingly accepts the benefits of one of your spells, or you land an attack, you can affect the creature as though you had successfully cast detect thoughts on them as an immediate action. You briefly gain insight into their current plans, something that weighs heavily on their mind, or whatever seems most relevant at the time.

4 - MM: All-Seeing Eye

Get a +2 bonus to 游+Perception.

You can pluck out your eye as a standard action and have it hover around autonomously with a Fly speed of 40 on each of your turns. It has a tether range of 300 ft. You can pop it back into your head as an instant action if it moves into your space. You can simultaneously see through both your eye and your detached eye without mental strain.

The eye is blurry and insubstantial so it always has Partial Concealment. To perceive it, creatures need to make a DC 20 perception check, or DC 10+your +Stealth bonus.

It shares all of your statistics and is treated as you for all targeting purposes. AoE effects targeting both you and your eye only affect you once.

6 - Innate Casting

You are persistently under the effect of Detect Thoughts allowing you to always peer into the thoughts of others. It loses the concentration tag and gains the buff tag. If gain another buff, you can reactivate detect thoughts as a swift action.
