Curses

The goal here is to create things that both have some kind of general mechanical benefit while also having some kind of role play to the curse.

Child

You‚Äôre just a kid. Those afflicted with the child curse can never grow up. They have the minds of someone much older than they appear mixed with an odd assortment of child-like tendencies and interests.

  • Benefit - You are one size category smaller than what is normal for your race. Your apparent age cannot be magically altered, even through polymorph effects.
  • Drawback - Whenever you try to appeal to authority to someone unfamiliar with your curse, you take a -2 penalty to any Skill Check related. You take a -2 penalty to poise.

Empathetic

You love people. Those afflicted with the empathetic curse consider most people their friends, and find it difficult to treat others harshly, even those on the other side of the battlefield. Woe to he who makes an enemy of an empathetic oracle.

  • Benefit - When you cast a spell or use an ability to restore +HP, it heals an additional 3 HP. Whenever you defeat a creature, they instead gain the time out condition.
  • Drawback - You are physically unable to intentionally and knowingly kill living creatures, even when magically compelled to do so. You can't leave a creature defeated in a dangerous scenario.
  • Special - In scenarios where compulsively healing people equates to suicide (such as in a warzone) you can ignore the compulsion to save lives.

Follower

You live for someone else. Those afflicted with the follower curse have separation anxiety turned up to 11. Whoever they are bound to is burdened with the undying loyalty of their companion.

  • Benefit - Choose another creature to form a permanent bond with. You can move in tandem with them if you have the movement. You can choose to be the target of a harmful effect targeting your bond as an instant action. If the effect is AoE and would target both of you, you can instead choose to automatically have them pass while you automatically fail.   • Drawback - If you have not seen your bond for 1 minute, you are shaken 1. This is considered Permanent Status as long as your bond is not around.
    If you have not seen your bond for 1 hour, you are shaken 3.
    If you have not seen your bond for 1 day, you receive the prior penalties and cannot do anything other than search for them unless physically prevented from doing so. In the event you are shown convincing evidence that your bond is dead, you attempt to use resurrection magic to get them back. If that is impossible, you are shaken 3 for one week before you must select a new curse, or a new bond.

Honest

You just can't help but speak the truth. Those afflicted with the honest curse are rarely trusted with secrets. Their honesty is seen as a virtue to many, but it gets them into trouble quite often when they suddenly start getting quiet, or worse, when they run their mouth.

  • Benefit - When you surpass the DC of an ü§î+Insight check to Convince Truth by 5, they automatically blurt out the truth. You get a +5 bonus on checks to convince truth.
  • Drawback - You cannot knowingly tell a lie. Not even a half-truth. You are compelled to answer questions to the best of your knowledge. The best you can do to not reveal your deepest secrets is to not speak at all.

Horny

Your mind is often filled with lustful thoughts. Those afflicted with the horny curse often tell dirty jokes and hit on anything with a pulse. Worse yet they tend to have deviant tastes.

  • Benefit - You gain post nut clarity. After nutting/helping someone achieve nut, you may glean information from your intimate bond. You effectively ask a question and receive an answer in three or less words. The subject does not know you gleaned this information.
  • Drawback - Your partner also gets to glean information about you with the same restrictions. You know what they gleaned.

Loner

You just want to be alone. Those afflicted with the loner curse are physically drained when in the presence of other people, and thrive when left to their own devices. Confrontation is terrible, especially when they have to do the confronting.

  • Benefit - You get a +2 bonus to AC and saving throws when you aren't being threatened and no allies are adjacent to you. When you aren't in the presence of other creatures you get a +2 bonus to skill checks. Something like a familiar, companion creature or a pet can be a reasonable exception.
  • Drawback - You take a -2 penalty to any opposed skill check. You cannot benefit from or provide flanking. You cannot perform or benefit from the Aid action.

Oblivious

You have no idea how bad things really are. Those afflicted with the oblivious curse have things fly right over their heads all the time, sometimes to their benefit!

  • Benefit - You get a +4 bonus versus fear effects. You get a +4 bonus to AC and saving throws until you act in combat. These bonuses stack. Whenever making a saving throw out of combat, also add these bonuses.

You‚Äôre always dwelling on the past. Those afflicted with the retrospective curse constantly reminisce about the great things that happened in their past. By the same token, the negative events that happen to them weigh heavy on their mind for much longer than most.

  • Benefit - You are immune to effects that magically alter your memory. You recall everything you have ever experienced with perfect clarity. You can innately cast Memory Movie at-will (self only).
  • Drawback - When you are suffering from an affliction, any numeric penalties are doubled. Twinned

You're like brothers, only closer. Those afflicted with the twinned curse come with a +1. They have 2 heads and share control over the body.

  • Benefit - You have a commoner attached to you that matches your LvL. You have primarily control of your body and your head has no actions of its own, but can still sense normally and speak.   • Drawback - You must concede control of your body in order to use these bonus skills/race features. This requires a free action. Your other head can concede control similarly, or you can forcefully take control by making your other half pass a will save 1/minute. You can also shove them.
    When your other head is in control it uses its own stat array, skill bonuses, racial features etc. This happens automatically while you are sleeping and the other head isn't, though most loud/physical activities will wake you up.
  • Special - Because this curse requires cooperation, using it to fuse with enemies can be very challenging. Proceed with caution.
