Bandit

Rogue Archetype.
A professional thief, a blur of shadows around the battlefield taking everything their opponents have and stepping back before they realize what happened.
Your very shadow steps out to reach into people's pockets when they aren't looking.

1 - Pickpocket

Crits drop coins around the target. When collected, roll Critical Damage to see how many you get; if you collect them, it's worth quadruple. You can collect the coins as a free action, but other creatures must spend an action to collect or otherwise move the money. Before it's looted or moved, the coins are Blood Money.

This ability represents taking something away from your opponent with each hit. If you use it while sparring or against allies to "farm" money, they may need that money back to replace the golden tooth they lost, jewelry you pilfered, the kidney you stole etc.

DM discretion.

1 - Cunning Action: Steal

You can use Cunning Action to Steal. You can use Steal to produce Blood Money once per creature.
Your Cunning Actions have +10 reach.

1 - Coin Explosion (Recharge)

Action-Attack. Explode ALL of your Blood Money, dealing your Critical Damage for every pile exploded in their tiles and adjacent tiles. The damage stacks, including with overlap (Reflex halves). You are immune to this damage.

Even though each explosion is a separate instance of damage, creatures only roll their save once against all of them.

2 - Band of Thieves Companion Summoning

You can easily find and hire goons to call upon for aid. These are humanoids with half your HP (including when summoned), and +1 DEX and LUK. They are half your level. They have no other abilities or statistics of their own (no techniques, feats, class features, etc).

You may equip them as you please, and Summon them as a Cunning Action as per a Summoning Contract. They take their turn on your turn - the player controls them.

They can use Steal and Critical Hits to create Blood Money for you, using your statistics. They have their own limit for Steal.

4 - Grand Larceny

When you use the Steal action, you can Steal from everything in reach.

4 - Coin Collection

Gain +20% Wealth.

6 - Assaulter Recharge Teleportation

Action-Attack. Teleport to an enemy that is ❗Surprised, up to 30ft, and Attack.

8 - Advanced Dark Sight

Steal no longer ends Dark Sight.
Crits no longer end Dark Sight.
