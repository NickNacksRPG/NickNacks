Inquisitor

Inquisitors heavily lean into ganging up on a single creature, defeating them as quickly as possible before smiting them and doing it all over again.

1 - Judgement (Su) Recharge

Mark an enemy within 30 feet for judgement as a swift action. Some type of glowing visual effect manifests on the creature while they are judged. All allies get a +2 Core Rules, How to Play/Abbreviations and Terminology/Attack against this creature.

When this enemy is defeated, this ability and smite immediately recharge. If you are unable to defeat this creature within a minute, the mark of judgement disappears from your target and judgement recharges.

1 - Multiattack

Make 2 attacks against a creature you have marked for judgement.

1 - Find the Faithful (Su)

While in a settlement, you can see a faint glowing trail leading to anybody who shares your faith. If one exists, you can also see a path leading to a place of worship, such as a temple or an altar dedicated to your deity. These paths are visually distinct from one another.

By spending a standard action to examine a creature, you can tell whether or not they share your faith.

2 - Enhanced Judgement

Apply an additional effect to creatures you mark for judgement.
