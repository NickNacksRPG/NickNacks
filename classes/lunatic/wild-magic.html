Wild Magic

When you trigger wild magic, first roll a d4 to determine which chart you roll on. 1 is red, 2 is yellow, 3 is purple and 4 is green.

After that, roll d20s until you get a result on the chart. Hopefully eventually there will be more than 20!

Dice Roll Name Effect
1 Worst Nightmare A figment of one of the caster's (no doubt numerous) fears appears somewhere within 30 feet of them (GM decides where) and spooks you, causing you to be frightened until it is proven to not be real.
2 Disarmed Your dominant arm falls off. Grab off the ground and reattach as a standard action or 30 ft of movement.
3 Stop Right There Criminal Scum! You cannot willingly move from your current position until the end of your next turn while you are harassed by the fun police.
4 Baby Mode Turn into a baby version of yourself until you spend a standard action making baby noises. This changes none of your statistics. You are affected as per Sanctuary but it lasts for 1 minute. You can choose to stay a baby for as long as you desire.
5 Magic Hot Potato Burst into painful rainbow flames, taking d6 (3) force damage immediately and another d6 (3) at the start of each of your turns until you stop, drop and roll (30 feet of movement), or succeed on a melee Spell Attack to pass it on to another creature.

That creature can pass it along or get rid of it with the same methods. Ends after creature it is attached to is defeated.
6 Invisible Spray Your pants turn Invisible until dispelled or properly washed. If not your pants, whatever covers your underwear. You cannot escape the embarrassment unless you are naked.
7 Grass Type Become a potted plant until the start of your next turn. Functions as Tree Shape, but you're a wee potted plant.
8 You Died Horribly. Then you respawn in a new body in the same state you were in moments ago, but with one less health. Roll on the Resurrection Chart after combat.
9 Turbo Thrusters Roll a d4. 1=North, 2=East, 3=South, 4=West. Move 60 feet in that direction.
