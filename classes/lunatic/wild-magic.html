Wild Magic

When you trigger wild magic, first roll a d4 to determine which chart you roll on. 1 is red, 2 is yellow, 3 is purple and 4 is green.

After that, roll d20s until you get a result on the chart. Hopefully eventually there will be more than 20!

Dice RollNameEffect
1Worst NightmareA [figment] of one of the caster's (no doubt numerous) fears appears somewhere within 30 feet of them (GM decides where) and spooks you, causing you to be frightened until it is proven to not be real.
2DisarmedYour dominant arm falls off. Grab off the ground and reattach as a standard action or 30 ft of movement.
3Stop right there criminal scumYou cannot willingly move from your current position until the end of your next turn while you are harassed by the fun police.
4Baby ModeTurn into a baby version of yourself until you spend a standard action making baby noises. This changes none of your statistics. You are affected as per Sanctuary but it lasts for 1 minute. You can choose to stay a baby for as long as you desire.
5Hot Potato

Burst into painful rainbow flames, taking d6 (3) force damage immediately and another d6 (3) at the start of each of your turns until you stop, drop and roll (30 feet of movement), or succeed on a melee attack to pass it on to another creature.

That creature can pass it along or get rid of it with the same methods. Ends after creature it is attached to is defeated.

6Invisible SprayYour pants turn invisible until dispelled or properly washed. If not your pants, whatever covers your underwear. You cannot escape the embarrassment unless you are naked.
7Grass TypeBecome a potted plant until the start of your next turn. Functions as Tree Shape, but you're a wee potted plant.
8You DiedHorribly. Then you respawn in a new body in the same state you were in moments ago, but with one less health. Roll on the resurrection chart after combat.
9Turbo ThrustersRoll a d4. 1=North, 2=East, 3=South, 4=West. Move 60 feet in that direction.
