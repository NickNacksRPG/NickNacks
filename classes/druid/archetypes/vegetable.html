Vegetable

1 - Gardening

Your body is host to a variety of plant life. You have wooden parts, blooming flowers, leafy hair, roots growing along your body or moss growing on your face.

Gain the following benefits...

  • You are constantly under the effects vine whip, you can use these vines to make a multiattack

    Make 2 simple vine attacks

  • You have advantage on stealth in areas with suitable vegetation.
  • When in direct sunlight, you gain regeneration 1.

1 - One With Nature (Su) (poly, true)

As a swift action, you can copy any inanimate plant you can see within 30 ft. You can speak normally while in plant form. You can also have the face of someone you know appear along the surface of the plant in leaves or bark. This transformation lasts indefinitely, but can be ended as a swift action.

While transformed you have advantage on +Stealth, 👀+Perception, +Disguise and +Deception. If a creature saw you transform or is already very familiar with your voice, you lose the advantage on deception, disguise and stealth.

1 - Talent

You have advantage on wild empathy checks against plant creatures.

2 - Bramble Back (persistent)

You can cause the vegetation that grows on you to become dangerously pointy as a free action. Creatures you are currently in contact with take 2d6 (7) pierce damage. Creatures you initiate prolonged contact against, or that initiate contact against you also take this damage.

"Contact" usually refers to extended physical interaction, such as with Grapple or Drag. Brief contact, like with an Unarmed Attack doesn't proc this damage.

4 - New Recruits (Sp)

You are constantly under the effects of arboreal army. Their attack bonus increases by 2. If you yourself are currently transformed into a tree, you can use arboreal army with your own tree body.

You can use them to perform any combat maneuvers instead of only bullying.
