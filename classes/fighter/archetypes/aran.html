Aran

Fighter Archetype.

Cleave through waves of enemies eventually gaining the strength to spin through entire armies. Aran is the premier AoE DPS martial. Spin to win!

1 - Smash Swing

Gain weapon focus (slash) as a bonus feat. You can use great cleave at will. When you cleave, it is referred to as Smash Swing.

1 - Combat Step (Elusive)

If you defeat at least one enemy during Smash Swing, you may activate Combat Step as a swift action to move an additional 10 feet without spending your movement.

Cleave normally requires you stand still to attack all foes in range, but movement using combat step ignores this, allowing you to continue your cleave as you step through combat. You still can't continue the endless cleave with your normal movement.

At level 6, Combat Step is always a free action.

2 - Combo

After defeating an enemy with a melee attack, you get another Superior Attack that must be used before the end of your turn (doesn't work off-turn).

Whenever you defeat another creature past the first in a round, get you an additional Combat Step that can be used as a free action.

4 - Hoard Breaker

Your melee attacks ignore damage resistance to single target attacks from group type enemies.
Your cleave attacks against group enemies always Crit on hit.

6 - Combo Drain (Healing)

Defeating an enemy heals 3 hp.

8 - Aero Swing

If you Smash Swing while in the air, you may spend all of your remaining movement to Fly in place until the start of your next turn. You may use this to fly using your Combat Steps, should you defeat enemies in the air.
You cannot be made clumsy while using aero swing.

11 - Smashing

You push your combat prowess to its absolute maximum in the heat of a combo. Superior Attacks deal maximum damage to opponents you struck with Smash Swing on the same turn.
