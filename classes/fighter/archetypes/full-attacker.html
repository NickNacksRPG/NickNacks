Full Attacker

⚔Fighter Archetype.

Feeling nostalgic about 3e, 3.5e or PF1e? Well here's me doing my best to recreate that experience in a slightly less heavy handed way than BB fighter. The full attacker is the fightiest guy, all about gaining insane amounts of movement speed and then using that speed to gain more actions. Woe to those who dare to stand in your face.

1 - Who's Next?

If you defeat an enemy and don't have enough speed to reach a new enemy, you can move until you threaten the next closest enemy, unless your speed is 0.

1 - Full Attack

Any turn you spend your standard action to make an attack, can make an additional MELEE superior attack a turn by sacrificing 45 speed once per turn.

You cannot use techniques or special attacks after full attacking. If you use a special attack or technique during your turn, you cannot full attack.

1 - Fervor of Battle

During combat, your speed increases by 15 this level and another 5 every level after. You must always end your movement with an enemy in reach. If you ever end your turn without an enemy in reach for any reason, you become clumsy 1.

2 - Action Surge

Each one can be used in place of a superior attack and they all share a global cooldown, only able to be used 1/round.
You cannot sacrifice superior attacks gained from baby slayer to use these abilities.

  • 5 Steps Ahead (elusive) - Prepare for an incoming attack. While preparing you can trigger this ability as an immediate action, moving up to 30 feet to the opposite side of a creature that affects you with a harmful effect. You get a +4 bonus to AC and saves against this effect.
  • Random Bullshit Go - Throw an item that isn't a weapon. Improvised weapons can still be used. If you throw a harmful item, such as a toxin, it applies on hit (then forcing saving throws as normal). If you throw a helpful item, such as a potion, its effects function as normal, ignoring any broken vials that may get involved.
    If for whatever reason an effect requires multiple rounds to take effect, that is still the case for this ability.
  • Cunning-ish Action - You can 🏳disengage, Jump, aid, focus or make a +Combat Maneuver.
  • Brute Force - Roll a Brute Force check.

11 - Fullest Attack

When you full attack, you can sacrifice an additional 45 speed to make an additional attack with the same restrictions.
