Plague

Plague alchemists use their mastery over toxic substances to melt their enemies. They have incredible damage potential in drawn out fights without healers.

1 - Toxic to the Touch (Poison)

You gain the Elemental Adept feat, applied to poison damage.
Whenever a creature fails a saving throw against one of your spells that deals damage, or on-hit, it applies a stack of Toxic. For the next minute, they take poison damage equal to the stacks of toxic they have at the start of each of their turns. Toxic damage can apply toxic stacks, but it does not extend its duration.

Toxic damage cannot kill creatures, only defeating them if it drops them to 0. The toxic stacks persist while defeated, but do not accumulate further.

This can be removed with Provide First Aid or by washing a creature clean, such as by being submerged in water. Any effect that removes venom can also remove toxic stacks.

1 - Master Poisoner

Whenever you use alchemical inspiration to create a toxin, you can apply it to a held weapon and make an attack with it as a free action.

Whenever a creature fails a save against one of your toxins, they also gain a stack of ~={red}toxic=~.

1 - Expunge (Su)

As a standard action, remove all toxic stacks from all creatures within 60 ft of you. Creatures take 3 true damage for each stack of ~={red}toxic=~ they possesses.

You can choose to either make it deadly or nonlethal. Nonlethal expunges can only defeat creatures, not kill them. Lethal expunges give creatures Disadvantage on luck checks to not Die.

When done out of combat, this ability has the Subtle tag.

~={green}2 - Ambush=~

When you successfully Hide from a creature and attack them or cast a spell on them, you can make an additional simple attack against them as a swift action.

~={green}4 - Vengeful Expunge=~

When you are defeated, you can expunge as an immediate action.
