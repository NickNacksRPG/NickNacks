Beast Trainer

You form a close bond with a beast companion and mold your fighting style around fighting in tandem with it. You gain special abilities that allow you to establish a closer bond with them. Fighting individually you are fairly weak, but together you can unleash powerful combo attacks.

Basic Statistics
+HP +8/LvL
Poise +6
Skills 6
Saves +2 Reflex
Memory 3+LV

~={green}1 - Techniques=~

You have mixed specialization.
You gain 2 Technique points at level 1, then another point every 2 levels after.
These technique points can be used on the 🍄Shroomlette and pupper trees.

Characters with magical specialization gain points in 📖Academy equal to their highest tier of magic technique and cantrips.
Characters with martial specialization gain points in ⚔Warfare equal to their highest tier of martial technique.

~={green}1 - Beast Companion (companion)=~

You gain the service of a specific animal to aid you on your journey. It grows more powerful as you do. More rules on beast companions in the 🐶Beast Companion section.

~={green}1 - Tandem Strike=~

When you take the attack action, your beast companion can also use its attack action to attack the same creature. You both get +2 to Core Rules, How to Play/Abbreviations and Terminology/Attack. This bonus also applies if you make an AoO against the same creature. This attack counts as one attack for the purposes of applying DR. Some forms of offensive actions that don't require an attack roll, such as maintaining a Grapple, can reasonably also grant this boon to the other member of the duo.

When you use this feature and one strike is enough to defeat the creature, the other is consumed and you cannot use your companion's strike on another.

All special attacks gained through this class deal an additional d6/odd LV dmg on hit if you and your beast companion both land an attack using tandem strike. You cannot combine these special attacks with other special attacks, even if your companion is the one performing the "special" part of the attack.
If one of your attacks is a Crit, then you crit this additional damage as well.

~={green}1 - Teamwork, Dreamwork=~

Gain a teamwork feat. Your beast companion shares all of your teamwork feats.

~={green}1 - Specialized Training=~

Select one of the following options that will shape your companion.
~={green}Mount=~ - Beast companion grows to size large. You can ride bareback (no saddle) without issue.
~={green}Guard=~ - Companion gains the bodyguard feat.
~={green}Watch=~ - Companion gains the Ready for Anything feat.
~={green}Lucky=~ - Companion gains +1 LUK

~={green}1 - Druidic=~

You learn the secret language, druidic (see Linguistics). In text form it can only be seen by other creatures that know druidic. You can essentially write secret messages by touching your finger to any natural object (typically boulders and trees) and writing. Any message written in this way can be erased by a creature that knows druidic with ease.

~={green}1 - Carry-On Companion (Su)=~

As a standard action, you can touch your beast companion to transform it into a handheld action figure version of itself. As a standard action, you can toss it into an adjacent space to transform it back. If left in figurine form for too long, your companion will free itself when it gets hungry, thirsty or lonely.

~={green}2 - Nature Stride=~

When traveling through natural terrain that restricts your movement, such as thick snow or dense jungle, you and your beast companion's speed are not affected. All allies that can see you or your beast companion also gain this benefit. After not seeing either of you for a few minutes, your allies lose this benefit.

~={green}3 - Beast Trainer Bond=~

You can speak with your beast companion as though you shared a language. Other creatures cannot understand you unless they can speak with animals.

~={green}4 - Adaptation (mana burn)=~

You can mana burn any amount to grant an equal number of adaptation points (from the adaptation points list) to your beast companion indefinitely. When you rest, this adaptation fades and you must reapply it.
