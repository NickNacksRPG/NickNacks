Prey Seeker

Classes/Beast Trainer/Beast Trainer Archetype.

Prey seekers use beast companions to harass their opponents, keeping them at a distance so you can hit them from afar. Your companion can rush back to your aid when needed.

~={green}1 - Protector=~

Your movement is Elusive against creatures your beast companion threaten.

Your beast companion gains the following special attack.
~={green}Special Attack: To My Side=~ - The beast companion can move 30 ft closer to you and attack a creature threatening you. If its current speed is less than 30 ft, this special attack's additional movement decreases to that reduced speed.

~={green}1 - Techniques=~

You gain a technique point in the Marksmanship tree at this level and every two levels after.

~={green}2 - Special Attack: Baited Arrow (healing)=~

Attach a tasty treat to your next ranged attack. On hit, apply a treat. Your beast companion gains seeking against the creature for 1 minute. Your beast companion heals (3+LvL) on hit on their first attack against this creature.

~={green}2 - Feat=~

Your beast companion gets the Classes/slayer/sentinel feat.

~={green}4 - Bothersome Buddy=~

You get a +2 bonus to Core Rules, How to Play/Abbreviations and Terminology/Attack against creatures your beast companion threatens, as though you were flanking.
Your beast companion gets the following special attack.
~={green}Special Attack: Bother=~ - Your companion taunts a creature on hit.
